Best Cardio Dvds For Beginners A Complete Guide

Written by Petter Jun 07, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you a beginner who is looking for an effective way to get fit and healthy? Do you want to improve your cardiovascular health without going to the gym? If so, then cardio DVDs might be the perfect solution for you. In this post, we will explore the best cardio DVDs for beginners that will help you achieve your fitness goals without leaving the comfort of your home.

Starting a fitness routine can be overwhelming, especially if you are new to working out. It can be challenging to decide where to start and what exercises to do. Moreover, going to the gym might not be feasible for everyone due to time constraints, budget, or personal preferences. Therefore, cardio DVDs can provide a convenient and cost-effective way to get your heart pumping and burn calories.

What are the Best Cardio DVDs for Beginners?

There are several cardio DVDs available on the market, but not all of them are suitable for beginners. As a beginner, you want a workout that is low-impact, easy to follow, and challenging enough to improve your fitness level gradually. Therefore, here are some of the best cardio DVDs for beginners:

1. Leslie Sansone: Walk at Home - 5 Day Slim Down

This DVD is perfect for beginners who want to start with low-impact cardio exercises. The workout features five 1-mile walks that will help you burn calories, boost your metabolism, and tone your muscles. The exercises are simple to follow and can be modified to suit your fitness level. Moreover, Leslie Sansone is an excellent instructor who motivates you to keep going.

2. Jillian Michaels: 30 Day Shred

Although this DVD might seem intimidating, it is an excellent choice for beginners who want to challenge themselves. The workout consists of three levels of 20-minute workouts that combine cardio, strength, and core exercises. The DVD also includes modifications for beginners, making it suitable for all fitness levels.

3. Beachbody: Country Heat

If you enjoy dancing and want to incorporate it into your fitness routine, then Country Heat is the perfect DVD for you. The workout features low-impact dance routines set to country music that will get your heart pumping and your feet moving. The DVD also includes a portion control container system to help you manage your nutrition.

4. Jane Fonda: Prime Time - Firm & Burn

This DVD is perfect for older adults who want to improve their cardiovascular health and muscle tone. The workout features low-impact exercises that are easy on the joints and can be modified to suit your fitness level. Jane Fonda is an excellent instructor who provides clear and concise instructions.

These are just a few examples of the best cardio DVDs for beginners. However, there are many other options available, depending on your preferences and fitness level.

My Personal Experience with Cardio DVDs

I have been using cardio DVDs for several years now, and I can say that they have been a game-changer for my fitness journey. As a busy mom, going to the gym was not feasible for me, and I needed a workout that I could do at home. Therefore, I started with Leslie Sansone's Walk at Home DVD, and I was hooked. The exercises were easy to follow, and I could modify them to suit my fitness level. Moreover, I enjoyed the fact that I could do them at my own pace and convenience.

Over time, I tried other cardio DVDs, such as Jillian Michaels' 30 Day Shred and Beachbody's Insanity. Although they were more challenging, I appreciated the fact that they pushed me out of my comfort zone and helped me achieve my fitness goals. Moreover, I enjoyed the variety and the fact that I could switch up my workouts whenever I wanted.

The Benefits of Cardio DVDs for Beginners

Cardio DVDs offer several benefits for beginners who want to improve their fitness level:

1. Convenient and Cost-Effective

Cardio DVDs provide a convenient and cost-effective way to get your heart pumping and burn calories. You can do them at home, without the need for expensive gym memberships or equipment. Moreover, you can do them at your own pace and convenience, without worrying about gym hours or class schedules.

2. Low-Impact and Easy to Follow

Most cardio DVDs for beginners feature low-impact exercises that are easy on the joints and suitable for all fitness levels. The exercises are also easy to follow, with clear instructions and modifications for beginners. Therefore, you can start with a low-intensity workout and gradually increase the intensity as you get fitter.

3. Variety and Fun

Cardio DVDs offer a variety of workouts that you can switch up whenever you want. Moreover, some DVDs feature dance routines or other fun exercises that make working out enjoyable and less daunting.

4. Improves Cardiovascular Health

Cardiovascular exercise is essential for heart health, and cardio DVDs provide an effective way to improve your cardiovascular fitness. Regular cardio exercise can lower your risk of heart disease, stroke, and other chronic conditions.

Frequently Asked Questions about Cardio DVDs for Beginners

1. How often should I do cardio DVDs?

You should aim to do cardio exercise for at least 30 minutes per day, five days per week. However, as a beginner, you might want to start with shorter workouts and gradually increase the duration and intensity. Listen to your body and do what feels comfortable for you.

2. Do I need any equipment for cardio DVDs?

Most cardio DVDs for beginners do not require any equipment. However, some DVDs might include resistance bands or other small equipment. Make sure to read the product description before purchasing the DVD.

3. Can I lose weight with cardio DVDs?

Cardio exercise is an effective way to burn calories and lose weight. However, weight loss also depends on other factors such as nutrition, sleep, and stress management. Therefore, make sure to adopt a holistic approach to weight loss.

4. Can I do cardio DVDs if I have an injury?

If you have an injury or a medical condition, make sure to consult with your doctor before starting any exercise program. Moreover, make sure to choose a low-impact workout that is suitable for your condition.

Conclusion of Best Cardio DVDs for Beginners

Cardio DVDs can provide a convenient and cost-effective way to improve your cardiovascular health and fitness level. As a beginner, you want to choose a workout that is low-impact, easy to follow, and challenging enough to improve your fitness level gradually. Therefore, Leslie Sansone's Walk at Home, Jillian Michaels' 30 Day Shred, Beachbody's Country Heat, and Jane Fonda's Prime Time are excellent choices for beginners. However, there are many other options available, depending on your preferences and fitness level. Remember to listen to your body, start with a low-intensity workout, and gradually increase the duration and intensity as you get fitter.
