What Is The Best Exercise For Upper Belly Fat

Written by Robby Sep 06, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling with excess belly fat? Do you find it difficult to lose that stubborn upper belly fat no matter how hard you work out or how healthy you eat? You're not alone. Many people struggle with this issue and wonder what is the best exercise for upper belly fat.

The answer to this question is not straightforward because you can't spot reduce fat. However, there are some exercises that can help you strengthen your core and burn overall body fat, which can contribute to reducing upper belly fat.

The best exercise for upper belly fat is a combination of strength training and cardio. Strength training helps to build muscle and increase your metabolism, which can help you burn fat more effectively. Cardio helps to burn calories and fat, which can lead to overall weight loss.

In this article, we will discuss the best exercises for upper belly fat and how to incorporate them into your workout routine.

My Personal Experience

As a personal trainer, I've worked with many clients who struggle with upper belly fat. One of my clients, Lisa, had been working out regularly but wasn't seeing any results in her midsection. I recommended that she incorporate strength training exercises like planks, Russian twists, and crunches into her routine, and also increase her cardio workouts. After a few weeks of following this routine, Lisa started to notice a significant difference in her upper belly fat and overall body composition.

Strength Training Exercises

The following strength training exercises can help you target your upper belly fat:

Planks

Planks are a great exercise for strengthening your core and targeting your upper belly fat. To do a plank, start in a push-up position with your hands shoulder-width apart, and your feet hip-width apart. Keep your body in a straight line from your head to your feet, and hold the position for as long as you can.

Russian Twists

Russian twists are another effective exercise for targeting your upper belly fat. To do a Russian twist, sit on the ground with your knees bent and your feet flat on the floor. Lean back slightly, and lift your feet off the ground. Twist your torso to the right, and touch your right hand to the ground. Twist back to the center, and then twist to the left, touching your left hand to the ground.

Cardio Exercises

The following cardio exercises can help you burn overall body fat:

Running

Running is an excellent cardio exercise that can help you burn fat and calories. Start with a slow jog, and gradually increase your speed and distance over time.

Cycling

Cycling is a low-impact cardio exercise that can be a great alternative to running. You can cycle outdoors or use a stationary bike at the gym.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I spot reduce upper belly fat with exercise?

A: No, you cannot spot reduce fat. You need to focus on overall weight loss through a combination of strength training and cardio.

Q: How often should I do strength training exercises?

A: Aim to do strength training exercises at least two to three times per week.

Q: How much cardio should I do?

A: Aim to do at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio or 75 minutes of high-intensity cardio per week.

Q: Can I do ab exercises every day?

A: Yes, you can do ab exercises every day, but it's important to give your muscles time to rest and recover.

Conclusion of What is the Best Exercise for Upper Belly Fat?

While you can't spot reduce fat, incorporating strength training and cardio exercises into your workout routine can help you burn overall body fat and strengthen your core, which can contribute to reducing upper belly fat. By doing exercises like planks, Russian twists, running, and cycling, you can work towards achieving a flatter, more toned midsection.
