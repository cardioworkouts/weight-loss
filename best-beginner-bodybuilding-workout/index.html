Best Beginner Bodybuilding Workout A Guide To Building Muscle

Written by Bardi Nov 03, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to build muscle and get in shape? If so, you're not alone. Building muscle is a common goal for many people, but it can be challenging to know where to start. In this post, we will discuss the best beginner bodybuilding workout to help you get started on your fitness journey.

Starting a new workout routine can be intimidating, especially if you're new to bodybuilding. You may be unsure of what exercises to do or how to structure your workout. Additionally, you may be worried about injury or not seeing results. These are all common concerns, but with the right plan, you can overcome them and achieve your goals.

The best beginner bodybuilding workout is designed to help you build muscle and increase strength. It focuses on compound exercises that work multiple muscle groups at once, allowing you to maximize your time in the gym. Additionally, it incorporates both resistance training and cardio to help you burn fat and build muscle simultaneously.

In summary, the best beginner bodybuilding workout is designed to help you build muscle and increase strength through compound exercises and a combination of resistance training and cardio.

Target of Best Beginner Bodybuilding Workout

The target of the best beginner bodybuilding workout is to help individuals build muscle and increase strength. This workout is ideal for beginners who are new to bodybuilding or those who have been away from the gym for an extended period. It is also suitable for individuals who want to build a solid foundation of strength before moving on to more advanced workouts.

Personally, I started with this workout when I was new to bodybuilding. I had minimal experience in the gym and needed a plan to help me get started. This workout provided me with a structure to follow and helped me build the foundation I needed to progress to more advanced workouts.

Exercises Included in Best Beginner Bodybuilding Workout

The best beginner bodybuilding workout includes the following exercises:

  • Squats
  • Bench Press
  • Deadlifts
  • Overhead Press
  • Barbell Rows
  • Pull-Ups or Lat Pulldowns
  • Planks
  • Cardio (Running, Cycling or Rowing)

Each exercise works multiple muscle groups and is designed to help you build strength and muscle. The workout should be done three times per week, with at least one day of rest between each workout.

Squats

Squats are one of the most effective exercises for building lower body strength and muscle. They work the quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves. To perform a squat, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart, toes pointing slightly outward. Hold a barbell across your upper back and shoulders, with your hands gripping the barbell slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower your body into a squatting position, keeping your back straight and your knees in line with your toes. Push back up to the starting position, and repeat for the desired number of reps.

Bench Press

The bench press is an excellent exercise for building upper body strength and muscle. It primarily works the chest, shoulders, and triceps. To perform a bench press, lie on a bench with your feet flat on the floor. Grip the barbell with your hands slightly wider than shoulder-width apart. Lower the barbell to your chest, keeping your elbows tucked in. Push the barbell back up to the starting position, and repeat for the desired number of reps.

Cardio Inclusion in Best Beginner Bodybuilding Workout

Cardio is an essential component of the best beginner bodybuilding workout. It helps to burn fat and build endurance, allowing you to work harder in the gym. Incorporating cardio into your workout routine can also help to prevent injury and improve overall health.

Personally, I like to include running as my cardio exercise. Running is an excellent way to burn fat and improve cardiovascular health. However, cycling or rowing are also great options. Choose the form of cardio that works best for you and incorporate it into your workout routine.

Question and Answer

Q. How many sets and reps should I do for each exercise?

A. For each exercise, you should aim for three sets of 8-12 reps.

Q. Can I do this workout at home?

A. Yes, you can do this workout at home if you have the necessary equipment. You will need a barbell, weights, and a bench to perform the exercises.

Q. How long should each workout session be?

A. Each workout session should be approximately 45-60 minutes long.

Q. How long will it take to see results?

A. Results will vary depending on your starting point and your consistency with the workout. However, with regular exercise and proper nutrition, you should start to see results within 4-6 weeks.

Conclusion of Best Beginner Bodybuilding Workout

The best beginner bodybuilding workout is designed to help you build muscle and increase strength through compound exercises and a combination of resistance training and cardio. It is ideal for beginners who are new to bodybuilding or those who have been away from the gym for an extended period. Incorporating this workout into your fitness routine can help you achieve your goals and build a solid foundation of strength.
