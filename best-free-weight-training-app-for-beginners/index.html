Best Free Weight Training App For Beginners Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Daniel Oct 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a beginner when it comes to weight training? Are you struggling to create a workout plan or stick to one? Look no further, as we have found the best free weight training app for beginners that will help you achieve your fitness goals.

Weight training can be intimidating, especially for beginners who may not know where to start. With so many exercises, sets, and reps to consider, it can be overwhelming. This is where a weight training app comes in handy - it can help you create a workout plan, track your progress, and keep you motivated.

The best free weight training app for beginners is Fitness Buddy. This app offers over 1,700 exercises and 45 workout routines that are designed for all fitness levels. It also has a feature that allows you to create custom workouts based on your fitness goals and preferences.

Overall, Fitness Buddy is a great app for beginners who are looking to get into weight training. Not only does it offer a wide variety of exercises and routines, but it also has a clean and easy-to-use interface that makes tracking your progress a breeze.

My Personal Experience with Fitness Buddy

As someone who was new to weight training, I found Fitness Buddy to be incredibly helpful. The app's custom workout feature allowed me to create a plan that was tailored to my fitness goals, and the exercise library provided clear instructions and videos for each movement.

One of my favorite features of the app was the ability to track my workouts and progress. I could easily see how much weight I was lifting and how many reps I was completing, which helped me stay motivated and focused on my goals.

Why Choose Fitness Buddy?

Aside from its vast exercise library and custom workout feature, Fitness Buddy also offers a range of other benefits for beginners. These include:

  • Workout reminders to help you stay on track
  • A calorie tracker to help you monitor your nutrition
  • The ability to connect with friends and share your progress

How to Get Started with Fitness Buddy

To get started with Fitness Buddy, simply download the app on your mobile device and create an account. From there, you can select your fitness level and goals, and the app will generate workout plans for you. You can also create your own custom workouts by selecting exercises from the app's library.

Tips for Using Fitness Buddy

Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your experience with Fitness Buddy:

  • Be consistent with your workouts - aim to exercise at least 3-4 times per week
  • Focus on form and technique to prevent injury
  • Gradually increase the weight and reps as you get stronger
  • Use the app's tracking feature to monitor your progress and celebrate your successes

Conclusion of Best Free Weight Training App for Beginners

If you're a beginner when it comes to weight training, don't let the complexity of it all discourage you. With the help of Fitness Buddy, you can create a workout plan that suits your fitness level and goals, and track your progress along the way. Give it a try and see how it can transform your fitness journey.

Question and Answer

Q: Is Fitness Buddy only for beginners?

A: No, Fitness Buddy is designed for all fitness levels, so even experienced weight lifters can benefit from the app's features.

Q: Is Fitness Buddy free?

A: While there is a free version of the app available, some features require a paid subscription.

Q: Can I use Fitness Buddy without gym equipment?

A: Yes, the app offers a range of exercises that can be done without equipment, such as bodyweight exercises and yoga poses.

Q: Can I use Fitness Buddy on multiple devices?

A: Yes, you can download the app on multiple devices and access your account from each one.
