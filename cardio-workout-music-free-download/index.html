Factors to Consider When Choosing Cardio Workout Music

When choosing cardio workout music, there are several factors to consider. These include:

1. Tempo: The tempo of the song should match your workout intensity. For example, if you are running, you should choose songs with a faster tempo to match your pace.

2. Lyrics: The lyrics of the song should be motivational and positive. They should inspire you to push through your workout and achieve your fitness goals.
