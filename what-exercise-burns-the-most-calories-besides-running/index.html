What Exercise Burns The Most Calories Besides Running

Written by Eveline Oct 02, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of running for hours on end just to burn a decent amount of calories? Do you want to switch up your workout routine and find other exercises that can burn just as many calories as running? You're in luck! There are plenty of other exercises that can help you burn calories without the monotony of running. In this post, we'll explore what exercise burns the most calories besides running.

When it comes to burning calories, many people associate it with running. While running is a great way to burn calories, it's not everyone's cup of tea. Running can be hard on your joints, and it can be boring for some people. So, what other exercises can you do to burn the same amount of calories as running?

The answer is that there are many exercises that can burn just as many calories as running. Exercises like cycling, swimming, rowing, and jumping rope are all great options. These exercises are low-impact, which means they're easier on your joints, and they can be a lot more fun than running.

In summary, there are plenty of exercises that can burn just as many calories as running, without the monotony and joint impact. These exercises include cycling, swimming, rowing, and jumping rope.

Cycling

Cycling is a great exercise that can help you burn a lot of calories. When you cycle, you're using your legs to push pedals, which can help you build strength and endurance. Cycling can also be a fun way to explore new areas and enjoy the outdoors.

Personally, I love cycling. I try to cycle at least twice a week, and I always feel great after a long ride. According to HealthStatus, cycling can burn up to 550 calories in 30 minutes for someone who weighs 155 pounds. That's a lot of calories burned in a short amount of time!

Swimming

Swimming is another great exercise that can help you burn a lot of calories. When you swim, you're using almost every muscle in your body, which can help you build strength and endurance. Swimming can also be a great way to cool off on a hot day.

I used to be on my high school's swim team, and I can attest to the fact that swimming is a great workout. According to HealthStatus, swimming can burn up to 372 calories in 30 minutes for someone who weighs 155 pounds.

Rowing

Rowing is a full-body workout that can help you burn a lot of calories. When you row, you're using your arms, legs, and core to move the rowing machine. Rowing can also be a fun way to switch up your workout routine.

I've tried rowing a few times, and it's definitely a challenging workout. According to HealthStatus, rowing can burn up to 316 calories in 30 minutes for someone who weighs 155 pounds.

Jumping Rope

Jumping rope is a great exercise that can help you burn a lot of calories. When you jump rope, you're using your legs and arms to move the rope, which can help you build endurance and coordination. Jumping rope can also be a fun way to relive your childhood.

I used to jump rope all the time as a kid, and I recently started doing it again as part of my workout routine. According to HealthStatus, jumping rope can burn up to 372 calories in 30 minutes for someone who weighs 155 pounds.

Question and Answer

Q: Can strength training burn as many calories as cardio?

A: While strength training doesn't burn as many calories as cardio exercises, it can still help you lose weight and build muscle. Strength training can also help you increase your metabolism, which can help you burn more calories throughout the day.

Q: How long should I exercise to burn the most calories?

A: The amount of time you need to exercise to burn the most calories depends on the exercise you're doing and your own body. Generally, you should aim to exercise for at least 30 minutes per day to see results. However, you can always increase your workout time if you want to burn more calories.

Q: Can I burn more calories by working out in the morning?

A: Working out in the morning can help you burn more calories throughout the day. When you exercise in the morning, you're jumpstarting your metabolism, which can help you burn more calories throughout the day. Additionally, working out in the morning can give you more energy and help you stay focused throughout the day.

Q: Can I burn more calories by working out harder?

A: While working out harder can help you burn more calories in the short term, it's not sustainable in the long term. It's important to find a workout routine that you enjoy and that you can stick to in the long term. Consistency is key when it comes to burning calories and losing weight.

Conclusion of What Exercise Burns the Most Calories Besides Running

While running is a great way to burn calories, it's not for everyone. Fortunately, there are plenty of other exercises that can burn just as many calories as running, without the monotony and joint impact. Exercises like cycling, swimming, rowing, and jumping rope are all great options that can help you burn a lot of calories and have fun at the same time. So, next time you're looking for a new workout routine, give one of these exercises a try!
