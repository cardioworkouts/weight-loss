Best Free Weight Lifting App For Android Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Petter Aug 09, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking to take your weight lifting game to the next level? Do you need a personal trainer but can't afford one? The best free weight lifting app for Android might just be the solution you need.

Weight lifting can be a daunting task for beginners and even experienced lifters. Without proper guidance, you might end up doing more harm than good. Thankfully, there are several free weight lifting apps for Android that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

In this post, we'll take a look at the best free weight lifting app for Android and explore its features, benefits, and drawbacks. We'll also answer some common questions about weight lifting apps.

So, let's dive in!

What is the Best Free Weight Lifting App for Android?

After researching and testing various weight lifting apps, we have concluded that the best free weight lifting app for Android is JEFIT.

Why JEFIT?

JEFIT is a comprehensive weight lifting app that offers a range of features to help you achieve your fitness goals. It has a large exercise database with detailed instructions and animations, making it easy to learn new exercises and perfect your form. The app also allows you to track your workouts, set goals, and monitor your progress. You can even create custom workout plans tailored to your specific needs and goals.

Another great feature of JEFIT is its social aspect. You can connect with other JEFIT users, share your progress, and even compete with them. This can provide a much-needed motivation boost and help you stay on track with your fitness journey.

However, like any app, JEFIT has its drawbacks. One of the biggest complaints from users is the app's interface, which can be overwhelming and confusing at first. Additionally, some users have reported bugs and glitches that can affect the app's performance.

How to Use JEFIT?

Using JEFIT is simple and straightforward. Here's a step-by-step guide:

1. Download and install the JEFIT app from the Google Play Store.

2. Create an account and set up your profile.

3. Browse the exercise database and select the ones you want to include in your workout.

4. Create a workout plan by selecting the exercises, reps, sets, and rest time.

5. Start your workout and track your progress.

Other Free Weight Lifting Apps for Android

While JEFIT is our top pick, there are several other free weight lifting apps for Android that you might want to consider:

StrongLifts 5x5

StrongLifts 5x5 is a popular weight lifting app that focuses on the 5x5 training program. It's great for beginners who want to build strength and muscle mass. The app provides detailed instructions and a simple interface, making it easy to use.

Fitbod

Fitbod is a personalized weight lifting app that creates custom workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and equipment. The app also provides detailed instructions and tracks your progress.

MyFitnessPal

While not specifically a weight lifting app, MyFitnessPal is a popular fitness app that can help you track your nutrition and calories. It's a great complement to any weight lifting app and can help you achieve your fitness goals faster.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use these apps without a gym membership?

A: Yes, you can use these apps at home or outdoors with minimal equipment. However, some exercises may require gym equipment.

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, these apps are great for beginners as they provide detailed instructions and can help you learn new exercises.

Q: Do I need to pay for these apps?

A: No, all the apps listed here are free to download and use. However, some apps may offer premium features that require a subscription.

Q: Can I use these apps on my iPhone?

A: Some of these apps may be available on the App Store, but this post specifically focuses on free weight lifting apps for Android.

Conclusion of Best Free Weight Lifting App for Android

If you're serious about weight lifting and want to take your fitness journey to the next level, a free weight lifting app for Android can be a game-changer. JEFIT is our top pick due to its comprehensive features and social aspect, but there are several other great options to choose from. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced lifter, these apps can help you achieve your fitness goals faster and more efficiently.
