How Long Should You Do Cardio Workout

Written by Frank Oct 27, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you wondering how long you should do cardio workout? Does it seem like you are not seeing any results despite putting in a lot of effort? If you are experiencing these feelings, you are not alone. Many people find it difficult to determine how long they should do cardio workout to achieve their fitness goals.

Doing cardio workout can be challenging, and it can be even more challenging without a clear understanding of how long you should do it. One of the pain points associated with how long should you do cardio workout is the fear of overworking or underworking oneself. It can also be challenging to find the right balance between doing too little and too much cardio.

So, how long should you do cardio workout? The American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous aerobic activity per week. This translates to 30 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise or 15 minutes of vigorous-intensity exercise per day, five days a week.

In summary, the key to determining how long you should do cardio workout is to find the right balance between doing too little and too much cardio. The American Heart Association’s recommendation provides a good starting point for those looking to incorporate cardio into their fitness routine.

How Long Should You Do Cardio Workout: Personal Experience

When I first started doing cardio, I found it difficult to determine how long I should do it. I was afraid of overworking myself and causing injury, but I also wanted to see results quickly. I started by doing 20 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise three times a week, and gradually increased the duration and frequency of my workouts as I got used to the routine.

I also realized that the duration of my workout depended on the type of cardio I was doing. For instance, I found that I could do longer sessions of low-impact exercises like cycling or swimming compared to high-impact exercises like running or jumping jacks.

How Long Should You Do Cardio Workout: Intensity

One factor that determines how long you should do cardio workout is intensity. If you are doing moderate-intensity exercises like brisk walking, you can do it for longer periods compared to vigorous-intensity exercises like sprinting. If you are a beginner, it is advisable to start with moderate-intensity exercises and gradually increase the intensity as you get used to the routine.

How Long Should You Do Cardio Workout: Types of Cardio

Another factor that determines how long you should do cardio workout is the type of cardio you are doing. Different types of cardio have different durations and intensities. For instance, if you are doing HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training), you can do shorter workouts (15-20 minutes) but at a higher intensity. On the other hand, if you are doing low-impact exercises like swimming or cycling, you can do longer workouts (30-60 minutes) at a moderate intensity.

How Long Should You Do Cardio Workout: How to Determine the Right Duration

The right duration for your cardio workout depends on your fitness goals, fitness level, and the type of cardio you are doing. Here are some tips to help you determine the right duration:

  • Start with a shorter workout duration and gradually increase it as you get used to the routine.
  • Listen to your body and adjust the duration accordingly. If you feel tired or fatigued, take a break or reduce the duration of your workout.
  • Consult a fitness expert or personal trainer to help you determine the right duration for your fitness level and goals.

How Long Should You Do Cardio Workout: Personal Experience

As I progressed in my fitness journey, I found that my cardio workouts were more effective when I varied the duration and intensity. I also realized that my fitness goals and fitness level played a significant role in determining the right duration. I consulted a personal trainer who helped me create a cardio workout routine that was tailored to my fitness goals and level.

Question and Answer

Q: How long should I do cardio workout if I want to lose weight?

A: If you want to lose weight, you should aim for at least 150-300 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week.

Q: Can I do cardio every day?

A: Yes, you can do cardio every day, but it is advisable to have rest days to avoid overtraining and injury.

Q: How long should I do cardio workout if I am a beginner?

A: If you are a beginner, start with 20-30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per day, three times a week, and gradually increase the duration and frequency as you get used to the routine.

Q: How long should I do cardio workout if I am an athlete?

A: If you are an athlete, your cardio workout duration and intensity will depend on your sport and fitness goals. Consult a coach or fitness expert to help you determine the right duration and intensity for your sport and goals.

Conclusion of How Long Should You Do Cardio Workout

In conclusion, how long you should do cardio workout depends on your fitness goals, fitness level, and the type of cardio you are doing. The American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous aerobic activity per week. However, it is advisable to consult a fitness expert or personal trainer to help you determine the right duration and intensity for your fitness level and goals.
