5 Day Bodybuilding Workout Routine A Step By Step Guide

Written by Petter Sep 07, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your old workout routine and looking for something new to challenge your muscles? Look no further than the 5 day bodybuilding workout routine! This routine is designed to target all major muscle groups and help you build strength and endurance over time. In this blog post, we will guide you through the steps of the 5 day bodybuilding workout routine and provide you with tips and tricks to make the most out of your workout.

What are the Pain Points of 5 Day Bodybuilding Workout Routine?

One of the biggest pain points of the 5 day bodybuilding workout routine is the time commitment. This routine requires you to work out for 5 days a week, which can be difficult to fit into a busy schedule. Additionally, it can be challenging to maintain the motivation to keep up with this routine over time, especially if you are not seeing the results you want.

What is the Target of 5 Day Bodybuilding Workout Routine?

The target of the 5 day bodybuilding workout routine is to stimulate muscle growth and increase overall strength and endurance. This routine is designed to work all major muscle groups over the course of a week, with each day targeting a different set of muscles. By following this routine consistently, you can see significant improvements in your overall fitness level.

Overview of the 5 Day Bodybuilding Workout Routine

The 5 day bodybuilding workout routine consists of 5 different workouts, each targeting a different set of muscles:

  1. Chest and Triceps
  2. Back and Biceps
  3. Shoulders and Abs
  4. Legs and Glutes
  5. Full Body

Each workout consists of 3-4 sets of 8-12 reps for each exercise, with a 60-90 second rest period between sets.

Chest and Triceps

On day 1, you will focus on your chest and triceps. This workout includes exercises such as bench press, incline dumbbell press, tricep pushdowns, and skull crushers. These exercises are designed to target your chest and triceps muscles and help you build strength and definition in these areas.

Back and Biceps

Day 2 is all about your back and biceps. This workout includes exercises such as pull-ups, rows, bicep curls, and hammer curls. These exercises are designed to target your back and biceps muscles and help you build a strong, toned upper body.

Shoulders and Abs

On day 3, you will focus on your shoulders and abs. This workout includes exercises such as shoulder press, lateral raises, front raises, and planks. These exercises are designed to target your shoulder and ab muscles and help you build a strong, defined upper body.

Legs and Glutes

Day 4 is all about your legs and glutes. This workout includes exercises such as squats, lunges, leg press, and calf raises. These exercises are designed to target your leg and glute muscles and help you build a strong, toned lower body.

Full Body

On day 5, you will do a full body workout that targets all major muscle groups. This workout includes exercises such as bench press, pull-ups, squats, and shoulder press. This workout is designed to help you build overall strength and endurance.

Question and Answer:

Q: How often should I do the 5 day bodybuilding workout routine?

A: You should aim to do this routine 5 days a week, with 2 rest days in between. This will give your muscles time to recover and help prevent injury.

Q: Can I modify this routine to fit my fitness level?

A: Yes! You can modify this routine by adjusting the number of sets or reps for each exercise or by using lighter weights. It's important to listen to your body and make adjustments as needed.

Q: How long should I stick to this routine?

A: You can stick to this routine for as long as you are seeing results and enjoying the workouts. However, it's important to switch up your routine every few months to prevent plateauing and keep challenging your muscles.

Q: Do I need any special equipment for this routine?

A: Yes, you will need access to a gym with weights and machines to complete this routine.

Conclusion of 5 Day Bodybuilding Workout Routine

The 5 day bodybuilding workout routine is a challenging but effective way to build strength and endurance. By following this routine consistently and making adjustments as needed, you can see significant improvements in your overall fitness level. Remember to listen to your body and make adjustments as needed, and don't forget to take rest days to prevent injury and allow your muscles to recover.
