Best Strength Training Apps On Android Your Ultimate Guide

Written by Luffy Jul 05, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym and not knowing what workouts to do? Do you want to have a personalized strength training program that fits your goals and schedule? Look no further, as we have compiled a list of the best strength training apps on Android that will help you achieve your fitness goals.

Strength training is a crucial part of any fitness program. It not only helps build muscle mass and strength, but it also aids in weight loss, improves bone density, and boosts overall health. However, creating a strength training program that works for you can be overwhelming, especially if you're a beginner. That's where these apps come in handy.

Our target is to provide you with information about the best strength training apps on Android. These apps are designed to make working out more efficient, fun, and personalized. Here are our top picks:

JEFIT

JEFIT is one of the most popular strength training apps on Android. This app provides users with a customizable workout planner, progress tracker, and social features. It has a wide range of exercises and workout routines to choose from, making it easy to create a personalized program. You can also track your progress and connect with other JEFIT users to stay motivated.

Personally, I have been using JEFIT for over a year now, and I love it. The app has helped me create a workout program that fits my schedule and goals. I also find the progress tracker feature very useful as it keeps me accountable and motivated.

StrongLifts 5x5

StrongLifts 5x5 is another popular strength training app on Android. It is designed for people who want to improve their strength and build muscle mass. The app focuses on the 5x5 program, which consists of five sets of five reps for each exercise. It is a simple yet effective program that can help beginners and advanced lifters alike.

I have recently started using StrongLifts 5x5, and I am very impressed with the app. The program is straightforward, and I can easily track my progress. I also find the app's reminders and rest timer features helpful during my workouts.

Fitbod

Fitbod is a strength training app that uses machine learning to create personalized workout plans based on your fitness level, goals, and available equipment. It also adapts to your progress and changes your program accordingly. The app has a vast exercise library, and you can choose which muscles you want to work on for each workout.

Although I haven't used Fitbod personally, I have heard great things about it from friends and fellow gym-goers. The app's ability to create customized workout plans and adapt to your progress makes it an excellent choice for anyone looking for a personalized strength training program.

Google Fit

Google Fit is not specifically a strength training app, but it is a great tool to track your overall fitness and activity level. The app records your daily steps, heart rate, and other fitness metrics. You can also connect it with other fitness apps and devices to get a comprehensive view of your health and fitness.

I use Google Fit every day to track my steps and see how active I am throughout the day. It's a great way to stay motivated and reach my daily activity goals.

Question and Answer Section

Q: Are these apps free?

A: Some of these apps offer a free version, while others require a subscription fee.

Q: Can I use these apps at home?

A: Absolutely! These apps are designed to be used anywhere, whether you're at home or at the gym.

Q: Do I need any equipment for these workouts?

A: It depends on the workout. Some exercises require equipment, while others can be done with just your body weight. Most of these apps have options for both equipment and bodyweight exercises.

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, these apps are suitable for beginners. They have options for different fitness levels and provide instructions on how to perform each exercise correctly.

Conclusion of Best Strength Training Apps on Android

These are just a few of the best strength training apps on Android that can help you achieve your fitness goals. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced lifter, these apps can provide you with personalized workout plans, progress tracking, and motivation. So, download one of these apps today and start building strength and muscle mass.
