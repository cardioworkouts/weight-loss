How Much Time Should A Beginner Workout

Written by Oliver Aug 18, 2023 · 4 min read

Starting a new workout routine can be overwhelming, especially for beginners. One of the most common questions that beginners ask is how much time they should spend working out. It can be challenging to determine how much time is enough to see results, but not too much that it causes burnout or injury. In this post, we will provide a comprehensive guide on how much time a beginner should spend working out.

Starting a new workout routine can be painful, and it is essential to start gradually. Beginners can be overwhelmed by the idea of working out every day, but it is not necessary. Instead, it would be best to start with a few days a week, and then gradually increase the number of days as you become more comfortable. Overworking the body can cause muscle soreness, which can be painful and demotivating.

The American Heart Association recommends that beginners should aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercise per week. It translates to 30 minutes a day, five days a week. However, it is essential to note that this is just a starting point. Beginners can start with a lower duration and gradually increase the duration as they become more comfortable. It is also essential to incorporate strength training exercises to build muscle and burn fat.

In conclusion, beginners should aim to start with a few days a week and gradually increase the duration and frequency of their workouts. The American Heart Association recommends 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercise per week, but this is just a starting point. It is essential to listen to your body and avoid overworking it to prevent injuries.

How Much Time Should a Beginner Workout - Personal Experience

When I started working out, I was unsure of how much time I should spend working out. I started with three days a week, and I would spend about 30 minutes doing cardio and strength training exercises. As I became more comfortable with the routine, I increased the duration and frequency of my workouts. I now work out for five days a week, with each session lasting about an hour.

How Much Time Should a Beginner Workout - Benefits of Strength Training

Strength training is an essential component of any workout routine. It helps build muscle, burn fat, and increase metabolism. Beginners should aim to incorporate strength training exercises at least two days a week. It can be in the form of bodyweight exercises or using weights.

How Much Time Should a Beginner Workout - Importance of Rest Days

Rest days are crucial for muscle recovery and growth. Beginners should aim to have at least one or two rest days a week to allow their muscles to recover. Overworking the body can lead to muscle soreness, fatigue, and burnout.

How Much Time Should a Beginner Workout - How to Stay Motivated

Staying motivated can be challenging, especially when starting a new workout routine. Here are a few tips to help you stay motivated:

  • Set achievable goals
  • Find a workout buddy
  • Change up your routine
  • Reward yourself for reaching milestones

How Much Time Should a Beginner Workout - Personal Experience

When I started working out, I struggled to stay motivated. I found it challenging to stick to a routine, and I often skipped workouts. However, I found that setting achievable goals and finding a workout buddy helped me stay motivated. Having someone to work out with made it more enjoyable, and we would hold each other accountable.

Question and Answer

Q: How long should a beginner spend doing cardio?

A: Beginners should aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio, five days a week, and gradually increase the duration as they become comfortable.

Q: Should beginners incorporate strength training into their workout routine?

A: Yes, strength training is essential for building muscle, burning fat, and increasing metabolism. Beginners should aim to incorporate strength training exercises at least two days a week.

Q: How many rest days should beginners have?

A: Beginners should aim to have at least one or two rest days a week to allow their muscles to recover.

Q: How can beginners stay motivated?

A: Beginners can stay motivated by setting achievable goals, finding a workout buddy, changing up their routine, and rewarding themselves for reaching milestones.

Conclusion of How Much Time Should a Beginner Workout

Starting a new workout routine can be overwhelming for beginners, but it is essential to start gradually. Beginners should aim to start with a few days a week and gradually increase the duration and frequency of their workouts. The American Heart Association recommends 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercise per week, but this is just a starting point. It is vital to listen to your body and avoid overworking it to prevent injuries. Incorporating strength training exercises and rest days is also essential for muscle recovery and growth. Staying motivated can be challenging, but setting achievable goals, finding a workout buddy, changing up your routine, and rewarding yourself can help you stay on track.
