Best Chest Exercises At Home Without Equipment

Written by Daniel Aug 24, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you looking to build a stronger chest without having to go to the gym or use any equipment? You're in luck! There are several chest exercises that you can do at home without any equipment that will help you achieve your fitness goals.

Many people struggle with finding the time to go to the gym or the cost of a gym membership. Others may feel intimidated or uncomfortable working out in a public setting. Whatever your reason may be, there are effective chest exercises that you can do at home without any equipment.

Push-Ups

Push-ups are one of the best chest exercises that you can do at home without any equipment. They are a compound exercise that works not only your chest but also your triceps, shoulders, and core. To perform a push-up, assume a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your feet together. Lower your body until your chest touches the floor, then push back up to the starting position.

There are several variations of push-ups that you can do to target different areas of your chest. For example, wide-grip push-ups will work your outer chest, while diamond push-ups will work your inner chest.

Dips

Dips are another excellent chest exercise that you can do at home without any equipment. They primarily work your chest and triceps, but they also engage your shoulders and core. To perform a dip, find two stable surfaces that are parallel to each other, such as the backs of two chairs. Place your hands on each surface and lower your body until your elbows are at a 90-degree angle, then push back up to the starting position.

Plank Walks

Plank walks are a challenging but effective chest exercise that you can do at home without any equipment. They primarily work your chest, shoulders, and core. To perform a plank walk, assume a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your feet together. Walk your hands and feet to the right for a few steps, then back to the left for a few steps. Continue alternating sides for a set amount of time or repetitions.

Bear Crawls

Bear crawls are a fun and challenging chest exercise that you can do at home without any equipment. They work your chest, shoulders, and core. To perform a bear crawl, start on all fours with your hands shoulder-width apart and your knees bent. Lift your knees off the ground and walk forward on your hands and feet, keeping your back flat and your core engaged. Continue for a set amount of time or distance.

Question and Answer

Q: How many reps and sets should I do?

A: It's recommended to do 3 sets of 10-15 reps for each exercise.

Q: Can I do these exercises every day?

A: It's recommended to give your muscles at least one day of rest in between workouts to allow them to recover and grow.

Q: Can I modify these exercises if they are too difficult?

A: Yes, you can modify these exercises by doing them on your knees or by performing fewer reps or sets.

Q: Can I combine these exercises into a workout routine?

A: Yes, you can combine these exercises into a workout routine by doing 3 sets of 10-15 reps of each exercise with a minute of rest in between sets.

Conclusion of Best Chest Exercises at Home without Equipment

Building a stronger chest doesn't have to involve going to the gym or using any equipment. By incorporating these chest exercises into your at-home workout routine, you can achieve your fitness goals and improve your overall strength and health.
