What Are High Intensity Exercises A Beginner s Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 31, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Do you want to take your fitness to the next level? High intensity exercises might just be what you need. These workouts are designed to challenge you both physically and mentally, pushing you to your limits and helping you achieve your fitness goals faster. In this beginner's guide, we'll explore what high intensity exercises are, their benefits, and some of the best exercises to try.

Are you tired of your current workout routine? Are you looking for a way to break through your fitness plateau? High intensity exercises might be just what you need. These workouts are designed to be challenging, pushing you to your limits and helping you achieve your fitness goals faster. However, they're not for everyone, and they can be quite intimidating for beginners. That's why it's important to understand what high intensity exercises are and how they work before you start.

What Are High Intensity Exercises?

High intensity exercises are workouts that involve short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest or lower-intensity exercise. These workouts are designed to challenge your body both aerobically and anaerobically, helping you improve your endurance, strength, and overall fitness. High intensity exercises can be done with or without equipment, and they can be modified to suit your fitness level and goals.

Some of the best high intensity exercises include burpees, jumping jacks, high knees, mountain climbers, and squat jumps. These exercises can be done in a circuit format, where you perform each exercise for a set amount of time before moving on to the next one, or in a Tabata format, where you perform each exercise for 20 seconds with 10 seconds of rest in between.

My Personal Experience with High Intensity Exercises

When I first started doing high intensity exercises, I was intimidated. I wasn't sure if I was fit enough to handle the intensity, and I was worried about getting injured. However, I quickly realized that these workouts were the perfect challenge for me. They pushed me to work harder than I ever had before, and I saw results in just a few weeks.

One of my favorite high intensity exercises is the burpee. This exercise works your entire body, from your legs to your chest to your core. I like to do burpees in a circuit format, where I perform them for 30 seconds followed by 30 seconds of rest. I repeat this circuit for 10 minutes, and by the end, I'm completely exhausted but also exhilarated.

The Benefits of High Intensity Exercises

There are many benefits to adding high intensity exercises to your workout routine. For starters, they can help you burn more calories in less time, making them a great option for people who are short on time. They can also help you improve your cardiovascular health, build muscle, and increase your metabolism.

Another benefit of high intensity exercises is that they can help you break through a fitness plateau. If you've been doing the same workouts for a while and aren't seeing results, high intensity exercises can help you challenge your body in new ways and see progress again.

How to Modify High Intensity Exercises for Beginners

If you're new to high intensity exercises, it's important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity over time. You can modify high intensity exercises by doing them at a lower intensity or for a shorter amount of time. You can also take longer breaks in between exercises or reduce the number of circuits you do.

It's important to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard. If an exercise feels too difficult, you can always modify it or choose a different exercise that works the same muscle groups.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of High Intensity Exercises

If you want to get the most out of your high intensity workouts, there are a few things to keep in mind. First, make sure you're properly warmed up before you start. This can help prevent injury and ensure that you're ready for the intensity of the workout.

Second, focus on good form. High intensity exercises can be challenging, and it can be tempting to sacrifice form for speed. However, this can increase your risk of injury and prevent you from getting the most out of the exercise. Take your time and focus on performing each exercise with good form.

Conclusion of What Are High Intensity Exercises

High intensity exercises are a great way to challenge yourself and take your fitness to the next level. They can help you burn more calories, improve your cardiovascular health, and break through a fitness plateau. However, it's important to start slowly, listen to your body, and focus on good form to get the most out of these workouts. With a little practice and patience, you'll be able to tackle even the most challenging high intensity exercises.

Question and Answer

Q: Are high intensity exercises safe for everyone?

A: High intensity exercises can be safe for most people, but it's important to talk to your doctor before starting a new exercise program, especially if you have any health concerns.

Q: How often should I do high intensity exercises?

A: It's generally recommended to do high intensity exercises 2-3 times per week, with at least one day of rest in between workouts.

Q: Can I modify high intensity exercises if I'm not fit enough?

A: Yes, high intensity exercises can be modified to suit your fitness level. You can do them at a lower intensity or for a shorter amount of time, and you can always take longer breaks in between exercises.

Q: What are some good high intensity exercises for beginners?

A: Some good high intensity exercises for beginners include jumping jacks, high knees, mountain climbers, and squat jumps. These exercises can be done without equipment and can be modified to suit your fitness level.
