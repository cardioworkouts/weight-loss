Workouts For Beginners At Gym To Lose Weight A Beginner s Guide

Written by Robby Nov 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you new to the gym and looking to lose weight? Starting a new workout routine can be intimidating, especially if you're not sure where to begin. The good news is that there are plenty of workouts for beginners at gym to lose weight that are effective and easy to follow.

Many people struggle with weight loss and finding the right workout routine can be a challenge. Not knowing what to do or how to do it can be discouraging and lead to giving up. However, with a little guidance, you can achieve your weight loss goals and feel confident in the gym.

If you're looking to lose weight, you need to focus on two things: cardio and strength training. Cardio workouts help you burn calories and fat, while strength training builds muscle which increases your metabolism, helping you burn more calories even at rest. A combination of both types of workouts is key to weight loss success.

In this article, we'll cover some of the best workouts for beginners at gym to lose weight, how to perform them correctly, and some tips for staying motivated.

Cardio Workouts for Beginners at Gym to Lose Weight

Cardio workouts are an essential part of any weight loss program. They help you burn calories, increase your heart rate, and improve your overall fitness level. Here are some cardio workouts for beginners at gym to lose weight:

Treadmill Walking

Walking on a treadmill is an excellent way to burn calories and is an easy workout for beginners. Start by walking at a moderate pace for 5-10 minutes, then gradually increase your speed to a brisk walk. Aim for 30-45 minutes of walking, three to four times a week.

Running on a Treadmill

If you're looking for a more intense workout, running on a treadmill is a great option. Start by jogging at a moderate pace, then increase your speed to a run. Aim for 20-30 minutes of running, three to four times a week.

Cycling

Cycling is a low-impact cardio workout that is perfect for beginners. Start with a stationary bike and aim for 20-30 minutes of cycling, three to four times a week. As you become more comfortable, you can try outdoor cycling or spin classes at your gym.

Strength Training Workouts for Beginners at Gym to Lose Weight

Strength training is essential for weight loss because it helps build muscle which increases your metabolism. Here are some strength training workouts for beginners at gym to lose weight:

Bodyweight Squats

Bodyweight squats are an excellent exercise for building leg strength and toning your glutes. Start by standing with your feet shoulder-width apart, then lower your body as if you're sitting in a chair. Make sure to keep your back straight and your knees over your ankles. Aim for 3 sets of 10-15 reps.

Dumbbell Rows

Dumbbell rows are a great exercise for building upper body strength. Start by holding a dumbbell in each hand and bending forward at the waist. Lift the dumbbells towards your chest, making sure to keep your elbows close to your body. Aim for 3 sets of 10-15 reps.

Tips for Staying Motivated

Starting a new workout routine can be challenging, but staying motivated is the key to success. Here are some tips to help you stay on track:

-Set achievable goals

-Find a workout buddy

-Mix up your workouts

-Track your progress

-Reward yourself for reaching milestones

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I workout to lose weight as a beginner?

A: Aim for 3-4 days a week of cardio and strength training workouts.

Q: Can I lose weight just by doing cardio?

A: Cardio is an essential part of weight loss, but strength training is also important for building muscle which increases your metabolism.

Q: Do I need to hire a personal trainer?

A: While a personal trainer can be helpful, it's not necessary. There are plenty of beginner workouts available online or at your gym.

Q: How long will it take to see results?

A: Results vary for each person, but with dedication and consistency, you should start to see results within a few weeks.

Conclusion of Workouts for Beginners at Gym to Lose Weight

Starting a new workout routine can be a challenge, but with the right guidance, you can achieve your weight loss goals. Remember to focus on a combination of cardio and strength training workouts, set achievable goals, and stay motivated. With dedication and consistency, you can lose weight and feel confident in the gym.
