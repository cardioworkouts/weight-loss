The Science Behind HIIT Workouts

HIIT workouts are designed to increase your heart rate and metabolism. When you perform high-intensity exercises, your body uses more oxygen to help you recover. This process is called excess post-exercise oxygen consumption (EPOC). EPOC is what helps your body continue to burn calories even after your workout is over. This is why HIIT workouts are so effective for weight loss.
