How Many Calories Does 20 Minute Hiit Workout Burn

Written by Christine Aug 24, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a high-intensity workout that burns a lot of calories in a short amount of time? If so, you might be wondering how many calories a 20 minute HIIT workout burns. HIIT, or high-intensity interval training, has become a popular workout method in recent years due to its efficiency and effectiveness. However, many people are unsure of how many calories they can expect to burn in a 20 minute session.

A common pain point for those who want to lose weight is finding the time and motivation to work out. Many people have busy schedules and find it challenging to fit a 60 minute workout into their day. Others may lack motivation to exercise for an extended period. HIIT workouts are an excellent solution for both of these challenges, as they are short and intense, making them easy to fit into a busy schedule and more manageable to motivate oneself to do.

A 20 minute HIIT workout can burn anywhere from 200 to 400 calories, depending on factors such as your weight, age, sex, and the intensity of the workout. The goal of HIIT is to push your body to its limits during the high-intensity intervals and then give it a short rest during the recovery periods. This cycle of intense exercise followed by a brief rest is what makes HIIT so effective in burning calories.

In summary, a 20 minute HIIT workout can burn a significant number of calories quickly. The intensity of the workout and the individual's factors such as age and weight can affect the number of calories burned. However, you can expect to burn anywhere from 200 to 400 calories in a 20 minute HIIT session.

Personal Experience: My 20 Minute HIIT Workout

As someone who has been doing HIIT workouts for a while, I can attest to their effectiveness in burning calories. I typically do a 20 minute HIIT workout three times a week, and I have noticed a considerable difference in my weight and overall fitness level. One of my favorite HIIT workouts is the Tabata method, which is 20 seconds of intense exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated for eight rounds. I typically do a mix of exercises such as burpees, squats, lunges, and push-ups. After each workout, I can feel the sweat pouring down my face and the endorphins kicking in.

Benefits of a 20 Minute HIIT Workout

A 20 minute HIIT workout has many benefits besides burning calories. HIIT workouts are efficient, which makes them easy to fit into a busy schedule. They also help build endurance, increase metabolism, and improve heart health. Additionally, HIIT workouts require no equipment, making them perfect for at-home workouts.

Factors That Affect Calories Burned During a 20 Minute HIIT Workout

Several factors can affect the number of calories burned during a 20 minute HIIT workout. These factors include:

  • Weight: The more you weigh, the more calories you will burn during exercise.
  • Age: Younger people tend to burn more calories than older people during exercise.
  • Sex: Men usually burn more calories than women due to having more muscle mass.
  • Intensity of the workout: The more intense the workout, the more calories you will burn.

Tips on How to Maximize Calories Burned during a 20 Minute HIIT Workout

If you want to maximize the number of calories burned during a 20 minute HIIT workout, here are some tips:

  • Choose high-intensity exercises such as burpees, jumping jacks, and mountain climbers.
  • Try to maintain a high level of intensity during the exercise intervals.
  • Use proper form to ensure that you are engaging the correct muscles and getting the most out of each exercise.
  • Take short rest periods to allow your body to recover before the next high-intensity interval.

Personal Experience: My Favorite 20 Minute HIIT Workout

One of my favorite 20 minute HIIT workouts is the following:

  • Round 1: 20 seconds of burpees, 10 seconds of rest. Repeat for eight rounds.
  • Round 2: 20 seconds of jump squats, 10 seconds of rest. Repeat for eight rounds.
  • Round 3: 20 seconds of mountain climbers, 10 seconds of rest. Repeat for eight rounds.
  • Round 4: 20 seconds of push-ups, 10 seconds of rest. Repeat for eight rounds.

Question and Answer

Q: How many calories does a 20 minute HIIT workout burn compared to other exercises?

A: A 20 minute HIIT workout can burn more calories than other exercises such as jogging, cycling, or weight lifting in the same amount of time.

Q: Is a 20 minute HIIT workout enough to lose weight?

A: Yes, a 20 minute HIIT workout can be enough to lose weight, especially when combined with a healthy diet.

Q: How often should I do a 20 minute HIIT workout?

A: It is recommended to do a 20 minute HIIT workout two to three times a week, allowing for rest days in between.

Q: Can anyone do a 20 minute HIIT workout?

A: While HIIT workouts can be challenging, they can be modified to accommodate different fitness levels and abilities.

Conclusion of How Many Calories Does 20 Minute HIIT Workout Burn

A 20 minute HIIT workout is an effective way to burn a significant number of calories in a short amount of time. The intensity of the workout and individual factors such as weight and age can affect the number of calories burned. However, a 20 minute HIIT workout can burn anywhere from 200 to 400 calories. By incorporating HIIT workouts into your fitness routine, you can take advantage of their efficiency and effectiveness in helping you achieve your fitness goals.
