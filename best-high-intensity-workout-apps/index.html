Best High Intensity Workout Apps For A Fitter You

Written by Aprilia Nov 12, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to take your fitness to the next level? Are you looking for high intensity workout apps that will challenge and motivate you? Look no further than these top-rated apps that are designed to help you achieve your fitness goals.

Many people struggle to find the time or motivation to exercise regularly. Others may not know how to structure their workouts effectively. High intensity workouts can be particularly challenging, but they are also incredibly effective for burning calories, building muscle, and improving overall fitness. However, finding the right workout routine can be difficult, especially for beginners.

Fortunately, there are many high intensity workout apps available that can help you get started and stay on track. These apps offer a variety of features, including personalized workout plans, real-time coaching, progress tracking, and community support. By using these apps, you can take your fitness to the next level and achieve your goals faster than ever before.

In this article, we will explore the best high intensity workout apps available today, and highlight their key features and benefits. We will also provide tips and advice for getting the most out of these apps, so you can achieve your fitness goals and enjoy a healthier, happier lifestyle.

Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is a popular high intensity workout app that offers hundreds of free workout videos, ranging from beginner to advanced levels. The app is easy to use and features a variety of workout types, including HIIT, strength training, and cardio. You can also customize your workouts based on your fitness goals and preferences.

As a personal user of Fitness Blender, I find this app very helpful in my fitness journey. The videos are easy to follow, and the workout plans are challenging yet achievable. The best thing is, it is free!

7 Minute Workout

The 7 Minute Workout app is perfect for those who are short on time but still want to get a high-intensity workout. The app features 12 different exercises that you can do in just 7 minutes, making it a great option for busy people who want to stay fit.

As a personal user of 7 Minute Workout, I find this app very suitable for me who have a busy schedule. The exercises are designed to be quick, but challenging enough to feel the burn. I can easily do it anywhere, anytime.

MyFitnessPal

While not strictly a high intensity workout app, MyFitnessPal is a great tool for tracking your nutrition and fitness goals. The app features a comprehensive database of foods and exercises, and allows you to log your meals and workouts easily. By tracking your progress, you can stay motivated and on track to achieve your goals.

As a personal user of MyFitnessPal, I find this app very useful for tracking my food intake and calories burned. The app also provides a community support system that helps me stay motivated in my fitness journey.

Tabata Timer

The Tabata Timer app is designed to help you perform Tabata workouts, which are high intensity interval training sessions that alternate between short bursts of intense exercise and rest periods. The app features customizable timers that can be tailored to your fitness level and goals, making it a great option for those who want to challenge themselves and take their fitness to the next level.

As a personal user of Tabata Timer, I find this app very helpful in designing my own Tabata workouts. The app is easy to use, and the customizable timers allow me to push myself to my limits.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use these apps as a beginner?

A: Yes, most of these apps offer beginner-friendly workout plans and videos.

Q: Are these apps free?

A: Some of these apps are free, while others require a subscription or one-time payment.

Q: Will these apps help me lose weight?

A: Yes, high intensity workouts are great for burning calories and losing weight.

Q: Can I use these apps at home?

A: Yes, all of these apps can be used at home with little or no equipment.

Conclusion of Best High Intensity Workout Apps

High intensity workouts can be challenging, but they are also incredibly effective for improving overall fitness and achieving your goals. By using these top-rated high intensity workout apps, you can take your fitness to the next level and enjoy a healthier, happier lifestyle. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced fitness enthusiast, there's an app out there that's perfect for you.
