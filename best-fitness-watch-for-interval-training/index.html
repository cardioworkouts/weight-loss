Best Fitness Watch For Interval Training

Written by Robby Jul 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a fitness watch that can help you with your interval training? Look no further! In this article, we will dive into the best fitness watch for interval training and how it can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Interval training can be challenging, especially if you're not sure when to push harder or take a break. This is where a fitness watch comes in handy, as it can help you track your progress and monitor your heart rate, making your workouts more efficient and effective.

The best fitness watch for interval training is one that has advanced features such as heart rate monitoring, GPS tracking, and customizable interval settings. It should also be comfortable to wear and have a long battery life.

In summary, the best fitness watch for interval training should have the following features:

Heart Rate Monitoring

Heart rate monitoring is essential during interval training as it can help you determine if you're working too hard or not hard enough. The best fitness watch for interval training should have a built-in heart rate monitor that can accurately track your heart rate.

Personally, I've been using the Garmin Forerunner 245 Music, and it has been a game-changer for my interval training. It has a wrist-based heart rate monitor that provides accurate heart rate data, even during high-intensity workouts.

GPS Tracking

GPS tracking is another important feature to look for in a fitness watch for interval training. It can help you track your distance, pace, and route, making it easier to plan your workouts and set goals.

The Garmin Forerunner 245 Music also has GPS tracking, which has been helpful for my outdoor interval training sessions. It accurately tracks my distance and pace, and I can see my route on the Garmin Connect app after my workout.

Customizable Interval Settings

The best fitness watch for interval training should have customizable interval settings that allow you to set your work and rest intervals. This is important because everyone's fitness level is different, and you need to be able to customize your intervals to your fitness level.

The Polar Vantage V2 is another excellent fitness watch for interval training that has customizable interval settings. You can set your work and rest intervals and customize the duration and intensity of each interval.

How to Choose the Best Fitness Watch for Interval Training

When choosing the best fitness watch for interval training, consider your fitness goals, budget, and the features you need. Determine which features are most important to you and choose a fitness watch that meets your needs.

Some other features to consider when choosing a fitness watch for interval training include:

  • Battery life
  • Water resistance
  • Compatibility with fitness apps
  • Comfort and fit

Conclusion of Best Fitness Watch for Interval Training

Interval training can be challenging, but with the right fitness watch, it can be more manageable and effective. The best fitness watch for interval training should have heart rate monitoring, GPS tracking, and customizable interval settings. Consider your fitness goals and budget when choosing a fitness watch and choose one that meets your needs. With the right fitness watch, you can take your interval training to the next level and achieve your fitness goals.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use my regular fitness watch for interval training?

A: It depends on the features of your fitness watch. If it has heart rate monitoring and customizable interval settings, then you can use it for interval training. However, if it doesn't have these features, you may want to consider upgrading to a fitness watch that does.

Q: How often should I do interval training?

A: It's recommended to do interval training 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between to allow your body to recover.

Q: Can interval training help me lose weight?

A: Yes, interval training can be an effective way to lose weight as it can help you burn more calories in a shorter amount of time. However, it's important to combine interval training with a healthy diet and lifestyle for optimal results.

Q: How long should my work and rest intervals be during interval training?

A: The duration of your work and rest intervals depends on your fitness level and goals. A common interval ratio is 30 seconds of work and 30 seconds of rest, but you can adjust the intervals to your fitness level.
