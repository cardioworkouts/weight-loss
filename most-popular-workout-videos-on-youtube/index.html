Most Popular Workout Videos On Youtube A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 09, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of paying for expensive gym memberships or personal trainers? Do you want to work out from the comfort of your own home? Look no further than YouTube! With thousands of free workout videos available, it's no wonder why YouTube is a go-to source for fitness enthusiasts.

However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right workout video for you. You may be asking yourself: what are the most popular workout videos on YouTube? Which videos will give me the best results? And how do I know which videos are safe and effective?

In this guide, we'll answer all of your questions about the most popular workout videos on YouTube. We'll provide a list of our top picks, explain their target audience, and give you a breakdown of what to expect from each video. Let's get started!

Yoga with Adriene

Yoga with Adriene is one of the most popular workout channels on YouTube, with over 8 million subscribers. Adriene Mishler, the host of the channel, offers a variety of yoga videos for all levels and interests. From beginner-friendly videos to advanced flows, there's something for everyone on this channel.

Personally, I've been practicing with Adriene for years and have found her videos to be both challenging and rejuvenating. Her calming demeanor and clear instructions make it easy to follow along, even if you're new to yoga. Plus, her videos are free and can be done from the comfort of your own home.

Popsugar Fitness

Popsugar Fitness is another popular workout channel on YouTube, with over 5 million subscribers. This channel offers a wide variety of workouts, including HIIT, dance, and strength training. They also have videos for specific body parts, such as arms, abs, and glutes.

I've personally tried several workouts from Popsugar Fitness and have found them to be challenging and fun. The instructors are engaging and the workouts are easy to follow along with. Plus, with so many options available, you'll never get bored with your workouts.

The Benefits of Working Out with YouTube

One of the biggest benefits of working out with YouTube videos is the convenience factor. You can work out from the comfort of your own home, without having to worry about commuting to a gym or hiring a personal trainer. Plus, most workout videos on YouTube are free, which can save you a lot of money in the long run.

Another benefit of working out with YouTube is the variety of options available. With thousands of videos to choose from, you can find a workout that meets your specific needs and interests. Whether you're looking for a high-intensity workout or a gentle yoga flow, there's something for everyone on YouTube.

Tips for Working Out with YouTube

If you're new to working out with YouTube videos, here are a few tips to get you started:

  • Choose a video that matches your skill level. If you're new to working out, look for beginner-friendly videos that provide clear instructions and modifications.
  • Make sure you have enough space to move around. Clear a space in your living room or bedroom to ensure that you have enough room for your workout.
  • Invest in a good mat. If you're doing yoga or any other floor-based exercises, it's important to have a good mat to cushion your joints and prevent slipping.
  • Listen to your body. If a certain exercise doesn't feel right, don't do it. It's important to listen to your body and make modifications as needed.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: Are workout videos on YouTube safe?

A: As with any workout, it's important to listen to your body and make modifications as needed. If you have any injuries or health concerns, it's always a good idea to consult with a doctor before starting a new workout routine.

Q: Do I need any equipment to work out with YouTube?

A: It depends on the workout you choose. Some workouts, such as yoga or bodyweight exercises, require little to no equipment. Others, such as strength training, may require dumbbells or resistance bands. Be sure to read the video description before starting to ensure that you have any necessary equipment.

Q: Can I really get a good workout from YouTube videos?

A: Absolutely! Many YouTube workouts are just as challenging as those you would do in a gym. Plus, with so many options available, you can find a workout that meets your specific needs and interests.

Q: How often should I work out with YouTube videos?

A: It's recommended that adults get at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise per week. However, the amount of exercise you need may vary depending on your goals and fitness level. Be sure to listen to your body and make modifications as needed.

Conclusion of Most Popular Workout Videos on YouTube

Whether you're new to working out or a seasoned pro, YouTube is a great resource for free, convenient, and effective workouts. By choosing the right videos and following a few simple tips, you can get a great workout from the comfort of your own home. So what are you waiting for? Grab your mat and start sweating!
