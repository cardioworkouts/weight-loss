This Best Cardio Exercises For Beginners Muscle Gain

Written by Aprilia Jul 01, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you new to the world of fitness and looking for the best cardio exercises for beginners? Look no further! In this article, we will explore the most effective cardio exercises for beginners that will help you achieve your fitness goals.

Starting a new exercise routine can be daunting, especially when it comes to cardio workouts. Many beginners struggle with finding the right exercises that are both effective and easy to perform.

What are the best cardio exercises for beginners?

When it comes to cardio exercises for beginners, it's important to focus on low-impact workouts that will help you build endurance and burn calories without putting too much strain on your joints. Here are some of the best cardio exercises for beginners:

1. Walking

Walking is a great low-impact exercise that can be easily incorporated into your daily routine. Whether you're walking around your neighborhood or hitting the treadmill at the gym, walking can help you burn calories and improve your cardiovascular health.

Personally, I like to go for a brisk walk in the morning to start my day off on the right foot. It's a great way to clear my mind and get some fresh air before starting my workday.

2. Cycling

Cycling is another low-impact exercise that is perfect for beginners. Whether you're cycling outdoors or using a stationary bike at the gym, cycling can help you burn calories and improve your cardiovascular health.

When I first started working out, I was intimidated by the idea of cycling. However, once I gave it a try, I quickly fell in love with the exercise. Not only is it a great workout, but it's also a fun way to explore the outdoors.

3. Swimming

Swimming is a great full-body workout that is perfect for beginners. It's a low-impact exercise that can help you build endurance and burn calories without putting too much strain on your joints.

Personally, I love swimming because it's a great way to cool off during the hot summer months. Plus, it's a great way to switch up your workout routine and challenge yourself in new ways.

Why are these exercises the best for beginners?

These exercises are the best for beginners because they are low-impact, easy to perform, and effective at burning calories and improving cardiovascular health. They are also versatile, meaning they can be easily adapted to fit your fitness level and personal preferences.

How to get started with these exercises?

If you're new to these exercises, it's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts. Start with shorter workouts and gradually increase the time and intensity as you build endurance.

4. Jumping Jacks

Jumping jacks are a great cardio exercise that can be done anywhere and anytime. They are easy to perform and can help you build endurance and burn calories.

Personally, I like to incorporate jumping jacks into my at-home workouts. They are a great way to get my heart rate up and get my blood pumping before moving on to other exercises.

5. High Knees

High knees are another great cardio exercise that can be done anywhere and anytime. They are easy to perform and can help you build endurance and burn calories.

When I first started doing high knees, I found them to be a bit challenging. However, with practice, I was able to improve my form and increase the intensity of the exercise. Now, high knees are one of my go-to cardio exercises.

Conclusion of best cardio exercises for beginners

Overall, these cardio exercises are the best for beginners because they are low-impact, easy to perform, and effective at burning calories and improving cardiovascular health. Whether you're walking, cycling, swimming, or doing jumping jacks and high knees, these exercises are a great way to get your heart rate up and improve your overall fitness level.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should beginners do cardio exercises?

A: It's recommended that beginners do cardio exercises at least three to four times per week for 30-60 minutes per session.

Q: Do I need any special equipment for these exercises?

A: No, these exercises can be done with little to no equipment. However, if you choose to do cycling, you may need access to a stationary bike.

Q: Can these exercises be modified for people with injuries?

A: Yes, these exercises can be easily modified to accommodate injuries or physical limitations. Consult with a healthcare professional or certified personal trainer for guidance on modifying exercises.

Q: Can these exercises help with weight loss?

A: Yes, these exercises are effective at burning calories and can help with weight loss when combined with a healthy diet and lifestyle.
