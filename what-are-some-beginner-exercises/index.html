What Are Some Beginner Exercises

Written by Oliver Jun 26, 2023 · 5 min read

Do you want to start exercising but have no idea where to begin? Are you looking for beginner exercises that are easy to follow and won't overwhelm you? If so, keep reading to learn about some great exercises that are perfect for beginners.

Starting a fitness journey can be intimidating, especially if you've never exercised before. You may be worried about not knowing what to do, feeling out of shape, or not having enough time. However, it's important to remember that everyone has to start somewhere, and there are plenty of beginner exercises that can help you get started.

So, what are some beginner exercises? Here are a few examples:

Walking

Walking is one of the easiest and most accessible forms of exercise. All you need is a good pair of shoes and a place to walk, whether that's outside or on a treadmill. Walking is a low-impact exercise that can improve your cardiovascular health, strengthen your muscles, and boost your mood. Start with a 10-minute walk and gradually increase the duration or distance as you feel more comfortable.

Bodyweight Exercises

Bodyweight exercises are exercises that use your own body weight as resistance, such as squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks. These exercises can help you build strength, improve your balance, and increase your flexibility. You can do bodyweight exercises at home or at the gym, and there are plenty of beginner-friendly variations that you can try.

Yoga

Yoga is a gentle form of exercise that can improve your flexibility, strength, and balance, as well as reduce stress and anxiety. There are many different types of yoga, from gentle restorative yoga to more intense power yoga. Look for beginner-friendly classes or online videos to get started.

Swimming

Swimming is a low-impact exercise that can improve your cardiovascular health, tone your muscles, and reduce stress on your joints. If you have access to a pool, consider taking a beginner swim class or simply swimming laps on your own.

These are just a few examples of beginner exercises that you can try. Remember, the key is to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity or duration of your workouts as you feel more comfortable. Don't be afraid to ask for help or guidance from a fitness professional if you need it.

Personal Experience with Walking

When I first started exercising, I was intimidated by the idea of going to the gym or doing anything too intense. So, I decided to start with something simple: walking. I started by taking a 10-minute walk around my neighborhood every day, and gradually increased the duration until I was walking for 30 minutes at a time. Walking helped me feel more energized and less stressed, and it was easy to fit into my busy schedule.

Personal Experience with Bodyweight Exercises

As I got more comfortable with walking, I decided to try some bodyweight exercises at home. I started with simple squats and lunges, and gradually added in push-ups and planks. I was surprised at how quickly I noticed a difference in my strength and endurance. Bodyweight exercises helped me feel more confident in my body and motivated to keep exercising.

Benefits of Beginner Exercises

There are many benefits to doing beginner exercises. For one thing, they're easy to follow and don't require any special equipment. This makes them a great option for people who are just starting out or who don't have access to a gym. Beginner exercises can also help you build a foundation of strength and endurance that will make it easier to tackle more challenging exercises down the line.

Tips for Getting Started with Beginner Exercises

If you're ready to start exercising but don't know where to begin, here are a few tips to get you started:

  • Start small: Begin with exercises that feel easy and comfortable, and gradually increase the intensity or duration as you feel more comfortable.
  • Find a buddy: Exercising with a friend or family member can help keep you motivated and accountable.
  • Set realistic goals: Don't expect to see results overnight. Set achievable goals for yourself and celebrate each small victory along the way.
  • Listen to your body: If an exercise feels uncomfortable or painful, stop and rest. It's important to listen to your body and avoid pushing yourself too hard.

Question and Answer

Q: What are some beginner exercises that can be done at home?

A: Bodyweight exercises like squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks can all be done at home with no equipment necessary.

Q: How often should I do beginner exercises?

A: Aim to do some form of exercise at least three to four times per week, with at least one rest day in between workouts.

Q: Can I lose weight by doing beginner exercises?

A: Yes, regular exercise can help you lose weight and improve your overall health. However, it's important to pair exercise with a healthy diet for best results.

Q: How long should I exercise for?

A: Start with 10-15 minutes of exercise and gradually increase the duration or intensity as you feel more comfortable.

Conclusion of What Are Some Beginner Exercises

Starting a fitness journey can be daunting, but it doesn't have to be. By starting with simple, beginner-friendly exercises like walking, bodyweight exercises, yoga, or swimming, you can build a foundation of strength and endurance that will help you tackle more challenging workouts down the line. Remember to start slowly, listen to your body, and celebrate each small victory along the way. Happy exercising!
