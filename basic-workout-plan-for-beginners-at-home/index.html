Basic Workout Plan For Beginners At Home

Written by Daniel Sep 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a beginner who wants to start working out but doesn't know where to begin? Do you feel overwhelmed by the idea of going to a gym and working out in front of others? You're not alone. Starting a workout routine can be intimidating, but it doesn't have to be. With a basic workout plan for beginners at home, you can get started on your fitness journey in the comfort of your own home.

Starting a workout routine can be difficult, especially if you're new to working out. You may not know what exercises to do or how to do them correctly. You may also feel self-conscious about your body and your ability to perform the exercises. These are all common pain points that beginners face when starting a workout routine.

The target of this article is to provide you with a basic workout plan for beginners at home. This plan will help you get started on your fitness journey and will give you the confidence you need to continue working out. What is a Basic Workout Plan for Beginners at Home?

When I first started working out, I was intimidated by the idea of going to a gym. I didn't know what exercises to do or how to do them correctly. That's why I started doing basic exercises at home. A basic workout plan for beginners at home consists of exercises that are easy to do and require little to no equipment.

One of the best exercises to do at home is bodyweight squats. This exercise targets your legs, glutes, and core. To do a bodyweight squat, stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and your arms at your sides. Lower your body as if you're sitting down in a chair, keeping your chest up and your back straight. Once your thighs are parallel to the ground, stand back up.

How to Do a Push-Up

Another great exercise to do at home is push-ups. Push-ups target your chest, triceps, and shoulders. To do a push-up, start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart. Lower your body until your chest touches the ground, then push back up to the starting position.

Other Exercises to Include in Your Basic Workout Plan

In addition to bodyweight squats and push-ups, there are several other exercises you can include in your basic workout plan for beginners at home. These include:

  • Plank
  • Jumping jacks
  • Lunges
  • Mountain climbers
  • Crunches

These exercises target different areas of your body and can be done with little to no equipment.

How to Create a Basic Workout Plan for Beginners at Home

Creating a basic workout plan for beginners at home is easy. Start by choosing a few exercises from the list above. Aim to do each exercise for 30 seconds to one minute, then rest for 30 seconds to one minute between exercises. Repeat the circuit two to three times, depending on your fitness level.

Benefits of a Basic Workout Plan for Beginners at Home

One of the biggest benefits of a basic workout plan for beginners at home is convenience. You don't need to go to a gym or have any special equipment to get started. You can do these exercises in the comfort of your own home. Another benefit is that these exercises are easy to modify. If an exercise is too difficult, you can modify it to make it easier. If it's too easy, you can modify it to make it harder.

Conclusion of Basic Workout Plan for Beginners at Home

A basic workout plan for beginners at home is a great way to get started on your fitness journey. These exercises are easy to do and require little to no equipment. By following this plan, you can build confidence and improve your overall health and fitness.
