Free Best Exercise Apps For Android For Beginner

Written by Eveline Nov 23, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the best exercise apps for android to help you stay fit and healthy? Look no further! In this article, we'll explore the top exercise apps for android that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

Staying fit and healthy is a priority for many people, but finding the time and motivation to exercise can be challenging. This is where exercise apps come in handy. With the right app, you can easily track your progress, get personalized workout plans, and stay motivated to reach your fitness goals.

Best Exercise Apps for Android

Here are some of the best exercise apps for android that can help you stay on top of your fitness game:

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a great app for people who want to get fit at home. The app offers over 190 free workouts, ranging from yoga to HIIT, which can be customized to your fitness level and preferences. The app also offers personalized training plans and allows you to track your progress over time.

2. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a popular app for tracking your diet and exercise. The app allows you to log your meals, track your calorie intake, and set personalized goals for weight loss or muscle gain. The app also integrates with other fitness apps, such as Fitbit and Garmin, to provide a comprehensive view of your health and fitness.

3. FitOn

FitOn is a free app that offers a variety of workout classes, including yoga, Pilates, and cardio. The app also offers personalized workout plans and allows you to track your progress over time. FitOn is great for people who are looking for a workout app that offers a variety of options.

4. 7 Minute Workout

7 Minute Workout is a great app for people who are short on time but still want to get a good workout in. The app offers quick, high-intensity workouts that can be done in just seven minutes. The app also offers personalized workout plans and allows you to track your progress over time.

Personal Experience with Best Exercise Apps for Android

Personally, I have used Nike Training Club and MyFitnessPal for my fitness journey. Nike Training Club has been great for me because it offers a variety of workouts that I can do at home with minimal equipment. MyFitnessPal has been great for tracking my meals and making sure that I am staying within my calorie goals. Both apps have helped me stay on track and motivated to reach my fitness goals.

Tips for Choosing the Best Exercise App for Android

When choosing the best exercise app for android, it's important to consider your fitness goals and preferences. Some apps offer personalized training plans, while others offer a variety of workout classes. Here are some tips to help you choose the best exercise app for android:

1. Consider your fitness goals

Do you want to lose weight, gain muscle, or improve your overall fitness? Consider your goals and choose an app that aligns with them.

2. Look for personalized features

Some apps offer personalized training plans and workouts based on your fitness level and preferences. Look for apps that offer these features if you want a more customized workout experience.

3. Check user reviews

Reading user reviews can give you an idea of how effective an app is and whether it's worth downloading.

4. Consider app compatibility

Make sure the app is compatible with your android device and that it doesn't require additional equipment that you don't have.

Question and Answer

Q: Are exercise apps for android free?

A: Some exercise apps for android are free, while others require a subscription or one-time payment to access all features.

Q: Can exercise apps for android help me lose weight?

A: Yes, exercise apps for android can help you lose weight by providing personalized workout plans and tracking your progress over time.

Q: Are exercise apps for android suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, many exercise apps for android offer workouts for beginners and allow you to customize the intensity and duration of each workout.

Q: Can exercise apps for android replace a personal trainer?

A: Exercise apps for android can provide personalized training plans and workouts, but they cannot replace the expertise and guidance of a personal trainer.

Conclusion of Best Exercise Apps for Android

Choosing the best exercise apps for android can help you stay on track and motivated to reach your fitness goals. Consider your goals and preferences when choosing an app, and don't be afraid to try out different ones until you find the perfect fit. With the right exercise app, you can achieve your fitness goals and live a healthier, happier life.
