Weekly Gym Workout Plan Free A Beginner s Guide

Written by Aprilia Aug 30, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you a beginner in the gym world and looking for a weekly gym workout plan that is both effective and free? Look no further! It can be challenging to figure out where to start when it comes to working out, especially if you don't have the luxury of hiring a personal trainer. But don't worry, we've got you covered with a weekly gym workout plan that is perfect for beginners.

The target of this article is to provide a free and effective weekly gym workout plan for beginners. We will also cover related keywords such as gym workout plan, weekly workout plan, and free workout plan.

To summarize, this article will provide you with a comprehensive and free weekly gym workout plan that is perfect for beginners. We will cover exercises that target different muscle groups and provide tips on how to perform them correctly. Additionally, we will touch upon the benefits of having a weekly gym workout plan and how it can help you achieve your fitness goals.

What is a Weekly Gym Workout Plan?

Before we dive into the workout plan itself, let's first understand what a weekly gym workout plan is. Simply put, it is a plan that outlines the exercises you will perform each week. Having a weekly gym workout plan is beneficial as it keeps you on track and helps you achieve your fitness goals. It also allows you to see your progress over time and make adjustments accordingly.

Personally, when I started going to the gym, I didn't have a plan and ended up wandering around aimlessly. I would do a few bicep curls here and there and hop on the treadmill for a bit. But I quickly realized that this approach wasn't effective and wasn't helping me achieve my fitness goals. That's when I decided to create a weekly gym workout plan for myself.

Benefits of Having a Weekly Gym Workout Plan

Having a weekly gym workout plan has many benefits, including:

1. Accountability: When you have a plan, you are more likely to stick to it and hold yourself accountable for your progress.

2. Efficiency: A weekly gym workout plan allows you to make the most of your time at the gym by focusing on exercises that target specific muscle groups.

3. Progress Tracking: A weekly gym workout plan helps you track your progress over time and make adjustments as needed.

4. Goal Achievement: Having a plan in place helps you work towards achieving your fitness goals.

How to Create a Weekly Gym Workout Plan

Creating a weekly gym workout plan may seem daunting, but it's actually quite simple. Here are some tips to help you get started:

1. Determine your fitness goals: Before creating your plan, determine what your fitness goals are. Do you want to lose weight, build muscle, or increase your endurance?

2. Choose exercises that target different muscle groups: When creating your plan, make sure to choose exercises that target different muscle groups. This will help you avoid overworking certain muscles and prevent injury.

3. Schedule your workouts: Decide which days and times you will be going to the gym and schedule your workouts accordingly.

4. Start slowly: If you're new to working out, start slowly and gradually increase the intensity of your workouts.

Sample Weekly Gym Workout Plan

Here is a sample weekly gym workout plan for beginners:

Monday: Upper Body (Chest, Back, Shoulders)

1. Bench Press: 3 sets of 12 reps

2. Lat Pulldowns: 3 sets of 12 reps

3. Shoulder Press: 3 sets of 12 reps

4. Dumbbell Flyes: 3 sets of 12 reps

Tuesday: Rest Day

Wednesday: Lower Body (Legs, Glutes)

1. Squats: 3 sets of 12 reps

2. Deadlifts: 3 sets of 12 reps

3. Leg Press: 3 sets of 12 reps

Thursday: Rest Day

Friday: Full Body

1. Pull-Ups: 3 sets of 12 reps

2. Push-Ups: 3 sets of 12 reps

3. Lunges: 3 sets of 12 reps

Saturday & Sunday: Rest Days

Remember, this is just a sample plan and can be adjusted to fit your fitness goals and schedule.

Question and Answer:

Q: Do I need to go to the gym every day?

A: No, it's important to give your body rest days to recover. A weekly gym workout plan typically includes 3-4 gym days per week.

Q: Can I create my own workout plan?

A: Yes, creating your own workout plan can be beneficial as it can be tailored to your specific fitness goals and schedule.

Q: How long should each workout be?

A: A typical gym workout should last around 1 hour.

Q: What should I do if I feel pain during a workout?

A: If you feel pain during a workout, stop immediately and seek medical attention if necessary.

Conclusion of Weekly Gym Workout Plan Free

A weekly gym workout plan is an essential tool for beginners looking to achieve their fitness goals. It provides structure, accountability, and helps you track your progress over time. By following the tips and sample plan provided in this article, you'll be well on your way to a healthier and happier you.
