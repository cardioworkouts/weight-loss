Best Exercise To Lose Weight With Hypothyroidism

Written by Petter Aug 15, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to lose weight despite hitting the gym regularly and following a strict diet plan? If you have hypothyroidism, it might be the culprit behind your weight gain. Hypothyroidism is a condition where your thyroid gland does not produce enough thyroid hormone. The lack of thyroid hormone slows down your metabolism, making it difficult for you to lose weight. However, the good news is that certain exercises can help you lose weight even with hypothyroidism.

If you have hypothyroidism, you might experience fatigue, joint pain, and muscle weakness, making it challenging to perform high-intensity workouts. You might also have difficulty losing weight despite having a healthy diet and exercising regularly. This can be frustrating and demotivating, making it essential to find exercises that work for your body and health condition.

The best exercise to lose weight with hypothyroidism is low-impact aerobic exercises that are gentle on your joints and muscles. These exercises can help you burn calories, improve your cardiovascular health, and boost your metabolism. Some of the best exercise to lose weight with hypothyroidism include:

1. Walking

Walking is a low-impact exercise that you can do anywhere, anytime. It does not require any equipment, making it a cost-effective way to stay active. Walking can help you burn calories, improve your cardiovascular health, and strengthen your muscles. You can start with a 20-minute walk every day and gradually increase your walking time as your fitness levels improve.

2. Swimming

Swimming is an excellent low-impact exercise that can help you burn calories and improve your cardiovascular health. It is also gentle on your joints, making it ideal for people with hypothyroidism. Swimming can help you tone your muscles and improve your flexibility. Start with a few laps in the pool and gradually increase your swimming time.

3. Yoga

Yoga is a low-impact exercise that can help you lose weight and improve your overall health. It can help you reduce stress, improve your flexibility, and strengthen your muscles. Yoga poses such as the Sun Salutation, Warrior Pose, and Downward-Facing Dog can help you burn calories and boost your metabolism.

4. Cycling

Cycling is another low-impact exercise that can help you lose weight and improve your cardiovascular health. It is a fun way to stay active and explore your surroundings. Cycling can help you burn calories, improve your endurance, and strengthen your leg muscles.

By incorporating these low-impact exercises into your fitness routine, you can lose weight even with hypothyroidism. However, it is important to consult your doctor before starting any exercise program, especially if you have any medical conditions.

Personal Experience

As someone with hypothyroidism, I struggled to lose weight for years despite following a strict diet and exercising regularly. However, I found that low-impact exercises such as walking and yoga helped me lose weight and improve my overall health. These exercises were gentle on my joints and muscles, making it easier for me to stay active without experiencing any pain or discomfort.

Tips for Exercising with Hypothyroidism

Here are some tips for exercising with hypothyroidism:

1. Start Slow

Start with low-impact exercises and gradually increase your exercise intensity and duration as your fitness level improves.

2. Listen to Your Body

Pay attention to your body and stop exercising if you experience any pain or discomfort.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water before, during, and after exercise to stay hydrated.

4. Get Enough Rest

Get enough rest and sleep to help your body recover and repair after exercise.

Question and Answer

Q: Can strength training help me lose weight with hypothyroidism?

A: Yes, strength training can help you build muscle, which can boost your metabolism and help you burn calories even at rest.

Q: How often should I exercise to lose weight with hypothyroidism?

A: Aim to exercise for at least 30 minutes a day, five days a week. However, it is important to consult your doctor before starting any exercise program.

Q: Can I do high-intensity workouts with hypothyroidism?

A: High-intensity workouts can be challenging for people with hypothyroidism, as they can cause fatigue and muscle weakness. It is essential to find exercises that work for your body and health condition.

Q: How long will it take to see results from exercising with hypothyroidism?

A: It might take longer to see results from exercising with hypothyroidism compared to people without the condition. However, staying consistent with your exercise routine and following a healthy diet can help you achieve your weight loss goals.

Conclusion of Best Exercise to Lose Weight with Hypothyroidism

Exercising with hypothyroidism can be challenging, but incorporating low-impact exercises such as walking, swimming, yoga, and cycling can help you lose weight and improve your overall health. It is essential to consult your doctor before starting any exercise program and to listen to your body to avoid any pain or discomfort. By staying consistent with your exercise routine and following a healthy diet, you can achieve your weight loss goals even with hypothyroidism.
