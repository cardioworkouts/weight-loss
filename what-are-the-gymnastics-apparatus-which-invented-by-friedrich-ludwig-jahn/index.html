It is used by male gymnasts to perform various exercises that require strength and agility. The gymnast swings back and forth on the bar and performs various maneuvers such as flips and twists.

The Parallel Bars

The parallel bars are a set of two bars that are parallel to each other and are used by male gymnasts. The gymnasts use the parallel bars to perform various exercises such as swings, dips, and handstands.

The Pommel Horse

The pommel horse is an apparatus that is used by male gymnasts. It is a padded horse with two handles, and the gymnast uses the handles to perform various maneuvers such as circles and flairs.

The Vaulting Horse

The vaulting horse is an apparatus that is used by male and female gymnasts. It is a padded horse that is used for vaulting exercises. The gymnast runs towards the horse, jumps onto it, and performs various maneuvers such as twists and flips.

The Balance Beam

The balance beam is an apparatus that is used by female gymnasts. It is a narrow beam that is raised above the ground. The gymnast performs various exercises such as jumps, turns, and acrobatic moves on the beam.

The Rings

The rings are an apparatus that is used by male gymnasts. They are two suspended rings that are used for various exercises such as swings, handstands, and flips.
