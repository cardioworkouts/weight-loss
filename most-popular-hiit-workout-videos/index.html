Most Popular Hiit Workout Videos A Tutorial

Written by Oliver Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a way to get in shape but don't have a lot of time to devote to exercise? HIIT, or high-intensity interval training, might be the solution you're looking for. HIIT workouts are designed to be short but intense, with bursts of high-intensity exercise followed by periods of rest. Many people find that HIIT is an effective way to burn fat, build muscle, and improve cardiovascular health. In this tutorial, we'll explore some of the most popular HIIT workout videos available online and help you find the one that's right for you.

Pain Points

For many people, finding the motivation to exercise can be a challenge. It can be especially difficult if you have a busy schedule or if you're not sure where to start. HIIT workouts can help overcome these obstacles by providing a quick, effective workout that can be done from the comfort of your own home. However, with so many HIIT workout videos available online, it can be overwhelming to know which one to choose. Additionally, some workouts may be too intense for beginners, while others may not provide enough of a challenge for more advanced exercisers.

Target and Most Popular HIIT Workout Videos

The target of this tutorial is to help you find the most popular HIIT workout videos that are suitable for your fitness level and goals. Here are some of the most popular HIIT workout videos available online:

  • Insanity
  • P90X
  • Tabata
  • HIIT with Adriene
  • Beachbody on Demand
  • BodyBoss Method
  • Jillian Michaels: 30 Day Shred
  • The Fitness Marshall
  • Kickboxing HIIT Workout by FitnessBlender
  • Shaun T's Focus T25

These videos vary in length, intensity, and style, so it's important to find the one that's right for you. Some are designed for beginners, while others are more suitable for advanced exercisers. Some focus on strength training, while others focus on cardio. Below, we'll take a closer look at some of the most popular HIIT workout videos and what they have to offer.

Insanity

One of the most popular HIIT workout videos is Insanity, which is designed to be a 60-day program that includes a combination of cardio and strength training. The workouts are intense, with high-impact moves such as burpees, squats, and jumping jacks. The program is led by Shaun T and includes a nutrition plan to help you achieve your fitness goals. However, this program is not recommended for beginners or those with joint issues.

P90X

P90X is another popular HIIT workout program that includes a combination of cardio, strength training, and yoga. The program is designed to be a 90-day program that includes 12 different workouts. The workouts are led by Tony Horton and include a nutrition plan to help you achieve your fitness goals. Like Insanity, this program is not recommended for beginners or those with joint issues.

HIIT with Adriene

If you're looking for a more beginner-friendly HIIT workout, HIIT with Adriene might be a good option. This program includes a series of short, 20-minute workouts that are designed to be low-impact and suitable for all fitness levels. The workouts are led by Adriene Mishler, a popular yoga instructor, and include modifications for those who need them.

Tabata

Tabata is a type of HIIT workout that involves 20 seconds of high-intensity exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated for a total of 4 minutes. This type of workout can be done with a variety of exercises, including bodyweight exercises, cardio, or weights. There are many Tabata workout videos available online, including some that are suitable for beginners.

Conclusion

In conclusion, HIIT workouts can be a great way to get in shape, burn fat, and improve your overall health. However, with so many HIIT workout videos available online, it's important to find the one that's right for you. Whether you're a beginner or an advanced exerciser, there's a HIIT workout out there that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

FAQs

1. What is the best HIIT workout video for beginners?

HIIT with Adriene is a great option for beginners, as the workouts are short, low-impact, and include modifications for those who need them.

2. Are there any HIIT workout videos that don't require equipment?

Yes, many HIIT workout videos can be done with just your bodyweight. Tabata workouts, in particular, are often done with no equipment.

3. How often should I do a HIIT workout?

It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week, with at least one day of rest in between workouts.

4. Can I do a HIIT workout if I have joint issues?

It's important to talk to your doctor before starting any new exercise program, especially if you have joint issues. Some HIIT workouts may be too intense for those with joint issues, so it's important to find one that's suitable for your needs.
