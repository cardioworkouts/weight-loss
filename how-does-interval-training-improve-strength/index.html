This How Does Interval Training Improve Strength Gaining Muscle

Written by Christine Nov 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Interval training is an effective workout that helps improve strength and endurance. Many people wonder how does interval training improve strength, and in this article, we will explore the benefits of this type of training and how it can help you reach your fitness goals.

When it comes to building strength, many people think of weightlifting as the only option. However, weightlifting is not the only way to increase strength. Interval training involves alternating periods of high-intensity exercise with periods of rest, which can help improve strength and endurance.

How does interval training improve strength?

Interval training helps improve strength by stimulating the muscles to work harder and more efficiently. During high-intensity intervals, the muscles work harder than they do during steady-state exercise, which can help increase muscle strength and endurance.

Interval training also helps improve strength by increasing the production of growth hormones, such as testosterone and human growth hormone (HGH). These hormones play a key role in building and repairing muscles, which can help increase strength over time.

Additionally, interval training helps improve strength by increasing muscle fiber recruitment. During high-intensity intervals, more muscle fibers are recruited to help perform the exercise, which can lead to increased strength and muscle growth over time.

My Personal Experience with Interval Training and Strength

I have been incorporating interval training into my workouts for the past few months, and I have noticed a significant improvement in my strength and endurance. Before I started interval training, I was struggling to lift heavy weights and complete high-intensity workouts. However, after a few weeks of interval training, I noticed that I was able to lift heavier weights and complete more reps.

Interval training has also helped me improve my endurance. I used to struggle with running for more than a few minutes at a time, but after incorporating interval running into my workouts, I am now able to run for longer periods without getting tired.

Interval Training and Muscle Growth

Interval training can also help promote muscle growth, which is important for building strength. During high-intensity intervals, the muscles are subjected to a greater amount of stress than they are during steady-state exercise. This stress can help trigger muscle growth and repair, which can lead to increased strength over time.

Additionally, interval training can help increase the production of muscle-building hormones, such as testosterone and HGH. These hormones play a key role in muscle growth and repair, which can help increase strength and muscle mass over time.

Types of Interval Training

There are many different types of interval training, each with its own unique benefits. Some popular types of interval training include:

  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
  • Tabata
  • Circuit Training
  • Interval Running

Each type of interval training can be customized to meet your individual fitness goals and preferences.

Tips for Getting Started with Interval Training

If you are new to interval training, it is important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts. Here are some tips to help you get started:

  • Choose a type of interval training that you enjoy and that fits your fitness level.
  • Start with short intervals and gradually increase the duration and intensity over time.
  • Make sure to warm up before starting your interval training workout.
  • Stay hydrated during your workout.
  • Cool down and stretch after your workout to prevent injury and improve recovery.

FAQs about Interval Training and Strength

Q: How often should I do interval training to improve strength?

A: It is recommended to do interval training 2-3 times per week to see improvements in strength and endurance.

Q: Can interval training replace weightlifting for building strength?

A: While interval training can help improve strength, it should not replace weightlifting entirely. A combination of both types of exercise is recommended for optimal results.

Q: Is interval training suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, interval training can be suitable for beginners as long as they start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of their workouts.

Q: How long does it take to see improvements in strength with interval training?

A: It can take several weeks to several months to see improvements in strength with interval training, depending on your fitness level and the frequency and intensity of your workouts.

Conclusion of How Does Interval Training Improve Strength

Interval training is a highly effective way to improve strength and endurance. By incorporating intervals of high-intensity exercise into your workouts, you can stimulate muscle growth and improve muscle fiber recruitment, leading to increased strength over time. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced athlete, interval training can help you reach your fitness goals and improve your overall health and wellbeing.
