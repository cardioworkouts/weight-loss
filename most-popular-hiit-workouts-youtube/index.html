Get Fit With The Most Popular Hiit Workouts On Youtube

Written by Eveline Jun 22, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old workout routine and ready for a change? Have you heard of HIIT workouts but don't know where to start? Look no further than the most popular HIIT workouts on YouTube.

HIIT, or High-Intensity Interval Training, is a popular workout method that alternates between high-intensity exercises and periods of rest or low-intensity exercises. It's known for being efficient and effective, but can also be intimidating for beginners.

But fear not, the most popular HIIT workouts on YouTube cater to all levels of fitness and offer a variety of exercises to keep things interesting. Plus, they're free and can be done from the comfort of your own home.

In this article, we'll dive into the world of the most popular HIIT workouts on YouTube and give you a rundown of what to expect.

The Body Coach TV

One of the most popular HIIT workouts on YouTube comes from The Body Coach TV. Hosted by Joe Wicks, this channel offers a variety of quick and effective HIIT workouts for all fitness levels. The workouts range from 15 to 30 minutes and include exercises such as squats, lunges, and burpees.

Personally, I love The Body Coach TV for its upbeat and motivational atmosphere. Joe Wicks is a great coach and encourages you to push yourself while also reminding you to listen to your body.

Popsugar Fitness

Popsugar Fitness is another popular YouTube channel that offers a variety of HIIT workouts. What sets this channel apart is the diversity of workouts, which include dance cardio, kickboxing, and even barre.

One of my favorite Popsugar Fitness HIIT workouts is a 30-minute cardio and strength training routine. It's challenging but also fun and keeps me engaged throughout the entire workout.

Blogilates

While Blogilates is primarily known for its Pilates workouts, the channel also offers HIIT routines that are worth checking out. The workouts are typically 10-15 minutes long and incorporate Pilates-inspired moves for a full-body workout.

If you're someone who enjoys Pilates but wants to incorporate more cardio into your routine, Blogilates' HIIT workouts are a great option.

How to Get Started

If you're new to HIIT workouts, it's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity. Begin with shorter workouts and focus on proper form before pushing yourself too hard.

It's also important to listen to your body and take breaks when needed. HIIT workouts can be intense and it's okay to take a breather or modify an exercise if it's too challenging.

Q&A

Q: How often should I do HIIT workouts?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between. This allows your body to recover and prevents injury.

Q: Can HIIT workouts help me lose weight?

A: Yes, HIIT workouts are great for weight loss because they burn a lot of calories in a short amount of time. However, it's important to pair exercise with a healthy diet for optimal results.

Q: Do I need any equipment for HIIT workouts?

A: While some workouts may require equipment such as dumbbells or resistance bands, many HIIT workouts on YouTube can be done with just your bodyweight.

Q: Can HIIT workouts be done at any fitness level?

A: Yes, HIIT workouts can be modified to fit any fitness level. Beginners should start with shorter workouts and focus on proper form before increasing the intensity.

Conclusion of Most Popular HIIT Workouts on YouTube

The most popular HIIT workouts on YouTube offer a fun and effective way to get in shape. From The Body Coach TV to Popsugar Fitness and Blogilates, there's a variety of workouts to choose from that cater to all levels of fitness.

Remember to start slow, listen to your body, and have fun. With dedication and consistency, you'll be on your way to a healthier, happier you.
