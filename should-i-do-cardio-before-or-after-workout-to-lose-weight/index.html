Should I Do Cardio Before Or After Workout To Lose Weight

Written by Christine Jun 29, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you someone who is trying to lose weight but is not sure whether to do cardio before or after your workout? This can be a confusing topic, but it's important to know what works best for your body. In this post, we will discuss the benefits and drawbacks of doing cardio before or after your workout, and hopefully help you make an informed decision.

When it comes to losing weight, there are different approaches to take. Some people prefer to do cardio before their workout to warm up their body and get their heart rate up. Others prefer to do cardio after their workout to burn more calories and fat. It's important to know that both methods have their benefits and drawbacks, and it ultimately comes down to personal preference and what works best for your body.

If your main goal is to lose weight, doing cardio after your workout may be more effective. This is because when you do cardio after your workout, your body has already used up its stored glycogen, which means it will start burning fat for energy instead. This can help you burn more calories and lose weight faster. However, if you do cardio before your workout, you may burn more calories during your workout, which can be beneficial if your goal is to increase your overall calorie burn.

In summary, whether you choose to do cardio before or after your workout depends on your personal preference and weight loss goals. Both methods have their benefits and drawbacks, and it's important to find what works best for your body.

Benefits of Doing Cardio Before Your Workout

Personally, I prefer to do cardio before my workout because it helps me warm up my body and get my heart rate up. It also helps me get in the right mindset for my workout and gives me a burst of energy. Additionally, doing cardio before my workout helps me burn more calories during my workout, which is beneficial for weight loss.

Benefits of Doing Cardio After Your Workout

On the other hand, doing cardio after your workout can be more effective for weight loss because it helps you burn more fat. When you do cardio after your workout, your body has already used up its stored glycogen, which means it will start burning fat for energy instead. This can help you burn more calories and lose weight faster.

How to Incorporate Cardio into Your Workout

If you're not sure whether to do cardio before or after your workout, you can incorporate both methods into your workout routine. For example, you can do a quick 10-15 minute cardio session before your workout to warm up your body and get your heart rate up. Then, after your workout, you can do a longer cardio session to burn more fat and calories.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your Cardio Workout

To get the most out of your cardio workout, it's important to choose an activity that you enjoy and that challenges you. This can be running, cycling, swimming, or any other activity that gets your heart rate up. Additionally, it's important to vary your cardio routine to prevent boredom and to challenge your body. You can do this by changing up the duration, intensity, and type of cardio you do.

Question and Answer

Q: How long should I do cardio for to lose weight?

A: It's recommended to do at least 30 minutes of cardio per day to lose weight. However, the duration and intensity of your cardio workout depends on your fitness level and weight loss goals.

Q: Is it better to do cardio on an empty stomach?

A: It's a personal preference, but some studies suggest that doing cardio on an empty stomach can help you burn more fat. However, it's important to listen to your body and fuel yourself properly before any workout.

Q: Can I do cardio every day?

A: Yes, you can do cardio every day, but it's important to listen to your body and not overdo it. It's recommended to take rest days and to vary your cardio routine to prevent boredom and overuse injuries.

Q: Should I do high-intensity or low-intensity cardio to lose weight?

A: Both high-intensity and low-intensity cardio can be effective for weight loss. High-intensity cardio can help you burn more calories in a shorter amount of time, while low-intensity cardio can help you burn more fat over a longer period of time. It's important to find what works best for your body and fitness level.

Conclusion of Should I Do Cardio Before or After Workout to Lose Weight

In conclusion, whether you choose to do cardio before or after your workout depends on your personal preference and weight loss goals. Both methods have their benefits and drawbacks, and it's important to find what works best for your body. Remember to listen to your body and fuel yourself properly before any workout.
