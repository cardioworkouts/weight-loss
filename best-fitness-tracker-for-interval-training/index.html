Best Fitness Tracker For Interval Training The Ultimate Guide

Written by Frank Nov 06, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for the best fitness tracker for interval training? Do you want to optimize your workouts and track your progress? Look no further because this ultimate guide will provide you with all the information you need.

Interval training is a popular workout technique that involves alternating high-intensity exercises with periods of rest or low-intensity exercises. This type of training can help you burn more calories, improve your cardiovascular health, and increase your endurance. However, it can be challenging to keep track of your heart rate, calorie burn, and other metrics without a reliable fitness tracker.

The best fitness tracker for interval training should have features such as heart rate monitoring, GPS tracking, and multi-sport modes. These features will allow you to monitor your progress, track your route, and record your different workout types. In this guide, we will review some of the best fitness trackers for interval training that are available on the market.

The Fitbit Charge 4

As a personal trainer, I have personally used and recommended the Fitbit Charge 4 to my clients for interval training. This fitness tracker has a built-in GPS, heart rate monitoring, and up to 20 different sport modes. It also has a feature called Active Zone Minutes that tracks your time spent in the fat burn, cardio, and peak heart rate zones. This feature is especially useful for interval training because it allows you to monitor your heart rate and adjust your workout intensity accordingly.

The Garmin Forerunner 945

The Garmin Forerunner 945 is another great fitness tracker for interval training. This watch has advanced features such as VO2 max tracking, training load focus, and recovery time. It also has a built-in GPS and multi-sport modes that allow you to track your different workouts. One of the unique features of this watch is the Body Battery Energy Monitor that measures your body's energy levels and suggests the best time for you to work out.

Other Considerations

When choosing the best fitness tracker for interval training, there are other factors to consider such as battery life, water resistance, and price. The Fitbit Charge 4 has a battery life of up to 7 days, is water-resistant up to 50 meters, and is priced at around $150. The Garmin Forerunner 945 has a battery life of up to 2 weeks, is water-resistant up to 50 meters, and is priced at around $600.

Tips for Using a Fitness Tracker for Interval Training

Now that you have chosen the best fitness tracker for interval training, here are some tips for using it effectively:

  • Set your goals: Before starting your workout, set your goals for the session. This will help you stay focused and motivated.
  • Use the heart rate monitoring feature: Monitor your heart rate throughout your workout to ensure that you are working in the right intensity zones.
  • Track your progress: Use the fitness tracker to track your progress over time. This will help you see your improvements and adjust your workouts accordingly.
  • Stay consistent: Consistency is key for interval training. Use your fitness tracker to stick to your workout schedule and stay on track.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use a fitness tracker for interval training without a heart rate monitor?

A: While it is possible to use a fitness tracker without a heart rate monitor for interval training, it is not recommended. The heart rate monitor is a key feature that allows you to monitor your heart rate and adjust your workout intensity accordingly.

Q: Can I wear my fitness tracker while swimming?

A: It depends on the fitness tracker's water resistance rating. Both the Fitbit Charge 4 and Garmin Forerunner 945 are water-resistant up to 50 meters, so you can wear them while swimming.

Q: How often should I charge my fitness tracker?

A: The battery life of a fitness tracker depends on the brand and model. The Fitbit Charge 4 has a battery life of up to 7 days, while the Garmin Forerunner 945 has a battery life of up to 2 weeks. It is recommended to charge your fitness tracker as often as needed to ensure that it is fully charged for your workouts.

Q: Can I use a fitness tracker for other types of workouts besides interval training?

A: Yes, most fitness trackers have multiple sport modes that allow you to track different types of workouts such as running, cycling, and swimming. Some fitness trackers also have gym and yoga modes.

Conclusion of Best Fitness Tracker for Interval Training

Choosing the best fitness tracker for interval training can make a significant difference in optimizing your workouts and tracking your progress. The Fitbit Charge 4 and Garmin Forerunner 945 are two of the best fitness trackers for interval training available on the market, but there are other options to consider as well. Remember to set your goals, use the heart rate monitoring feature, track your progress, stay consistent, and have fun!
