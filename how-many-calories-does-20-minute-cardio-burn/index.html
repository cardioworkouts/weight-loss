How Many Calories Does 20 Minute Cardio Burn

Written by Christine Jun 08, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to burn calories in a short amount of time? Do you want to know how many calories a 20 minute cardio session can burn? If so, you're in the right place. In this article, we'll explore the answer to the question of how many calories does 20 minute cardio burn, and provide tips on how to maximize your calorie burn during cardio workouts.

Cardio is one of the most popular forms of exercise for people looking to burn calories and improve their overall fitness. However, it can be challenging to know exactly how many calories you're burning during a cardio workout. Many factors can affect the number of calories you burn, including your weight, age, sex, and the type of cardio exercise you're doing.

So, how many calories does 20 minute cardio burn? On average, a 20 minute cardio session can burn anywhere from 100 to 300 calories, depending on the intensity of the workout. High-intensity cardio workouts, such as running or spinning, can burn more calories than low-intensity workouts, such as walking or yoga.

In summary, the number of calories you burn during a 20 minute cardio session can vary depending on several factors. To maximize your calorie burn, focus on high-intensity cardio workouts and incorporate interval training into your routine.

How to increase your calorie burn during a 20 minute cardio session

One way to increase your calorie burn during a 20 minute cardio session is to incorporate interval training into your workout. Interval training involves alternating periods of high-intensity exercise with periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. This type of training has been shown to increase calorie burn and improve cardiorespiratory fitness.

Another way to increase your calorie burn during cardio is to vary the type of exercise you do. For example, if you typically run on the treadmill, try switching to the elliptical or rowing machine. This will help prevent boredom and challenge your body in new ways.

The benefits of cardio exercise

Cardio exercise has numerous health benefits, including improved heart health, increased endurance, and weight loss. Regular cardio exercise can also help reduce your risk of chronic diseases such as diabetes, heart disease, and certain types of cancer.

How to track your calorie burn during a 20 minute cardio session

There are several ways to track your calorie burn during a 20 minute cardio session. One of the most popular methods is to use a fitness tracker or app that can estimate your calorie burn based on your age, weight, sex, and the type of exercise you're doing. Another option is to wear a heart rate monitor, which can provide a more accurate estimate of your calorie burn based on your heart rate.

Tips for getting the most out of your 20 minute cardio session

Here are some tips for getting the most out of your 20 minute cardio session:

- Start with a warm-up to prepare your body for exercise

- Choose a form of cardio you enjoy to make it more sustainable

- Incorporate interval training to increase your calorie burn

- Track your progress to stay motivated

Conclusion

In conclusion, a 20 minute cardio session can burn anywhere from 100 to 300 calories, depending on the intensity of the workout. To maximize your calorie burn, focus on high-intensity cardio workouts and incorporate interval training into your routine. Remember to track your progress and choose a form of cardio you enjoy to make it more sustainable.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do cardio to see results?

A: It is recommended that you do cardio at least three to four times per week to see results. However, the frequency and duration of your workouts will depend on your individual goals and fitness level.

Q: Is it better to do cardio before or after strength training?

A: It is generally recommended to do cardio after strength training, as this can help prevent fatigue and injury during your weightlifting workout. However, the best approach will depend on your individual goals and fitness level.

Q: How long should a cardio workout be?

A: The duration of a cardio workout will depend on your fitness level and goals. For beginners, a 20 to 30 minute workout can be sufficient, while more advanced exercisers may benefit from longer workouts of 45 to 60 minutes.

Q: How can I make cardio workouts more enjoyable?

A: To make cardio workouts more enjoyable, try incorporating music, working out with a friend, or trying new forms of exercise. You can also set goals and track your progress to stay motivated.
