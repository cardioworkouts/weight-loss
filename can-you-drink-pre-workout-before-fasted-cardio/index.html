Can You Drink Pre Workout Before Fasted Cardio

Written by Oliver Oct 17, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you someone who likes to workout on an empty stomach to burn more fat? Do you also like to take pre workout supplements to boost your energy and performance during your workout? If yes, then you must be wondering whether you can drink pre workout before fasted cardio or not.

The idea of drinking pre workout before fasted cardio can be confusing for many people. On one hand, you want to workout on an empty stomach to burn fat, but on the other hand, you want to take pre workout to improve your performance. So, what should you do?

The short answer is yes, you can drink pre workout before fasted cardio, but there are some things you need to keep in mind.

Before we dive into the details of whether you can drink pre workout before fasted cardio, let's first understand what pre workout supplements are and how they work.

Pre workout supplements are a combination of ingredients that are designed to enhance your performance during workouts. They usually contain caffeine, beta-alanine, creatine, and other ingredients that help improve energy, focus, and endurance. The goal of pre workout supplements is to help you push harder and get more out of your workouts.

Now that we understand what pre workout supplements are, let's talk about whether you can drink them before fasted cardio.

Personal Experience with Drinking Pre Workout Before Fasted Cardio

As a fitness enthusiast, I have tried both fasted cardio and pre workout supplements separately, but I was curious to see what would happen if I combined them. I decided to try drinking pre workout before fasted cardio to see if it would affect my performance and fat-burning ability.

What I found was that drinking pre workout before fasted cardio did help me perform better during my workouts. I had more energy and focus, and I was able to push myself harder than I would have been able to without the pre workout supplement. However, I did not notice any significant difference in my fat-burning ability.

Things to Keep in Mind When Drinking Pre Workout Before Fasted Cardio

While drinking pre workout before fasted cardio can help improve your performance, there are some things you need to keep in mind:

1. Start with a low dose: If you are new to pre workout supplements, start with a low dose to see how your body reacts. Some people may experience side effects such as jitters, nausea, and headaches if they take too much pre workout.

2. Stay hydrated: Pre workout supplements can dehydrate you, so make sure you drink plenty of water before, during, and after your workout.

3. Monitor your heart rate: Since pre workout supplements contain caffeine, they can increase your heart rate. Make sure you monitor your heart rate during your workout to ensure that it does not go too high.

4. Listen to your body: If you feel any discomfort or unusual symptoms after taking pre workout before fasted cardio, stop immediately and seek medical attention if necessary.

Can Pre Workout Supplements Break Your Fast?

One concern that many people have when it comes to drinking pre workout before fasted cardio is whether it will break their fast. The answer to this question depends on the type of pre workout supplement you are taking.

If your pre workout supplement contains calories, such as a pre workout shake, then it will break your fast. However, most pre workout supplements are calorie-free, so they will not break your fast.

How to Choose the Right Pre Workout Supplement for Fasted Cardio

If you decide to drink pre workout before fasted cardio, make sure you choose the right pre workout supplement. Here are some things to look for:

1. Caffeine content: Look for a pre workout supplement that contains caffeine, as it can help improve your energy and focus.

2. Beta-alanine content: Beta-alanine is an amino acid that can help improve endurance during high-intensity exercise.

3. Creatine content: Creatine is a supplement that can help improve strength and power output during exercise.

4. Calorie-free: Make sure your pre workout supplement is calorie-free so that it does not break your fast.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I drink pre workout before fasted cardio if I have never taken pre workout before?

A: Yes, you can drink pre workout before fasted cardio even if you have never taken pre workout before. However, it is recommended that you start with a low dose to see how your body reacts.

Q: Will drinking pre workout before fasted cardio help me burn more fat?

A: Drinking pre workout before fasted cardio may help improve your performance, but it may not necessarily help you burn more fat. Fat loss is primarily determined by creating a calorie deficit through diet and exercise.

Q: Can drinking pre workout before fasted cardio be harmful?

A: Drinking pre workout before fasted cardio can be harmful if you take too much or if you have any underlying medical conditions. It is important to start with a low dose and listen to your body.

Q: Should I drink pre workout before every fasted cardio workout?

A: It is not necessary to drink pre workout before every fasted cardio workout. You can use pre workout supplements as a tool to help improve your performance when you need an extra boost.

Conclusion of Can You Drink Pre Workout Before Fasted Cardio

Drinking pre workout before fasted cardio can help improve your performance during your workout, but it is important to start with a low dose, stay hydrated, monitor your heart rate, and listen to your body. Make sure you choose a pre workout supplement that is calorie-free, contains caffeine, beta-alanine, and creatine, and is safe for you to use. Remember, fat loss is primarily determined by creating a calorie deficit through diet and exercise, so drinking pre workout before fasted cardio may not necessarily help you burn more fat.
