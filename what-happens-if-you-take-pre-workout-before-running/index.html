What Happens If You Take Pre Workout Before Running

Written by Aprilia Aug 15, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you someone who likes to take pre workout supplements before your workout sessions? Do you also enjoy running as a part of your fitness routine? But have you ever wondered what happens when you take pre workout supplements before running? Keep reading to find out!

Pain Points of Taking Pre Workout Before Running

Taking pre workout supplements before running can cause several issues such as jitters, nausea, headaches, and increased heart rate. These issues can not only be uncomfortable but can also negatively impact your performance and overall health.

Answering the Target of What Happens If You Take Pre Workout Before Running

Pre workout supplements are designed to boost your energy levels and help you perform better during your workouts. They contain ingredients like caffeine, creatine, and beta-alanine which work together to increase your endurance, strength, and focus. However, when you take pre workout supplements before running, the effects can be different.

Running is a cardiovascular exercise that requires a lot of endurance and stamina. Taking pre workout supplements can increase your heart rate, which can make you feel like you're running out of breath sooner than usual. It can also cause jitters and nausea, which can hinder your performance and make you feel uncomfortable.

Additionally, pre workout supplements can cause dehydration, which is the last thing you want when running. If you're not hydrated enough, it can cause muscle cramps, fatigue, and dizziness, which can put a halt to your running routine.

Summary of What Happens If You Take Pre Workout Before Running

Increased Heart Rate and Its Effects

When you take pre workout supplements before running, the caffeine content in them can cause your heart rate to increase, which can make you feel like you're running out of breath sooner than usual. This can cause your performance to suffer, and you may not be able to run as far or as fast as you normally would. It can also put a strain on your heart, which can be dangerous if you have pre-existing heart conditions.

Personal Experience: I remember taking pre workout supplements before a morning run and feeling my heart race so fast that I had to stop midway. It was scary, and I learned my lesson to never take pre workout before running again.

Dehydration and Its Effects

Running already causes your body to lose fluids, so taking pre workout supplements before running can dehydrate you even further. This can cause muscle cramps, fatigue, and dizziness, which can negatively impact your performance and make you feel uncomfortable. It's crucial to stay hydrated before, during, and after your runs, especially if you're taking pre workout supplements.

Personal Experience: I once took pre workout supplements before a long-distance run without drinking enough water, and I ended up feeling dizzy and nauseous halfway through. It was a terrible experience, and I learned the importance of staying hydrated.

Effects on Performance

Taking pre workout supplements before running can have mixed effects on your performance. While it can increase your energy levels and focus, it can also cause jitters, nausea, and increased heart rate, which can negatively impact your performance. It's essential to listen to your body and understand how pre workout supplements affect you before deciding to take them before running.

How to Safely Take Pre Workout Before Running

If you still want to take pre workout supplements before running, here are some tips to do it safely:

  • Drink plenty of water before, during, and after your runs to stay hydrated.
  • Start with a low dosage of pre workout supplements and gradually increase it over time.
  • Wait at least 30 minutes after taking pre workout supplements before starting your run.
  • Listen to your body and stop if you experience any discomfort or negative side effects.

Conclusion of What Happens If You Take Pre Workout Before Running

Taking pre workout supplements before running can have both positive and negative effects on your performance and health. It's essential to understand how pre workout supplements affect you and listen to your body before deciding to take them before running. With the right precautions, you can safely take pre workout supplements and optimize your performance during your runs.

Question and Answer

Q: Can pre workout supplements boost my running performance?

A: Yes, pre workout supplements can boost your running performance by increasing your energy levels and focus. However, it's important to understand how they affect you before deciding to take them before running.

Q: Can pre workout supplements cause dehydration?

A: Yes, pre workout supplements can cause dehydration, especially if you're not drinking enough water. It's crucial to stay hydrated before, during, and after your runs.

Q: Can pre workout supplements cause muscle cramps?

A: Yes, pre workout supplements can cause muscle cramps if you're not hydrated enough. It's important to drink plenty of water and stay hydrated to prevent muscle cramps.

Q: Is it safe to take pre workout supplements before running?

A: It can be safe to take pre workout supplements before running if you take the right precautions, such as starting with a low dosage and staying hydrated. It's also important to listen to your body and stop if you experience any discomfort or negative side effects.
