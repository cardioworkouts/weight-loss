Best High Intensity Interval Training At Home A Complete Guide

Written by Bardi Jun 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you someone who wants to get a good workout in, but doesn't have the time or money to go to the gym? Do you want to burn a lot of calories in a short amount of time? If so, high intensity interval training (HIIT) at home might be the perfect solution for you!

HIIT workouts are known for their ability to burn a lot of calories in a short amount of time. They are also great for building endurance and increasing your metabolism. However, many people are intimidated by the idea of doing HIIT workouts at home. They worry that they won't be able to do the exercises correctly or that they won't have the motivation to push themselves.

In this article, we will provide you with everything you need to know to start doing the best high intensity interval training at home. We will cover the benefits of HIIT, the best exercises to do, and how to create your own HIIT workout plan.

What is High Intensity Interval Training?

High intensity interval training, or HIIT for short, is a type of workout that involves short bursts of high-intensity exercise followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. The idea is to push yourself to your limit during the high-intensity intervals, which will help you burn more calories and build more endurance than you would during a traditional cardio workout.

There are many different exercises that you can do during a HIIT workout, including running, jumping jacks, burpees, and squats. The key is to choose exercises that get your heart rate up and challenge your body.

My Personal Experience with HIIT at Home

Before I started doing HIIT workouts at home, I was struggling to find the time and motivation to exercise. I didn't want to spend money on a gym membership, and I found it hard to stick to a workout routine when I was exercising alone. However, once I discovered HIIT workouts, everything changed.

I started doing 20-minute HIIT workouts in my living room, and I was amazed at how much of a workout I could get in such a short amount of time. I loved the fact that I didn't need any equipment, and I could do the workouts whenever I had a spare 20 minutes. Plus, the high-intensity intervals made me feel like I was really pushing myself and getting a good workout.

The Benefits of HIIT at Home

One of the biggest benefits of doing HIIT workouts at home is that they are incredibly efficient. You can get a great workout in just 20-30 minutes, which is perfect for people who are short on time. Additionally, because you don't need any equipment, you can do the workouts anywhere and anytime.

Another benefit of HIIT workouts is that they are great for burning fat and building endurance. The high-intensity intervals help you burn more calories than you would during a traditional cardio workout, while the periods of rest or low-intensity exercise allow you to catch your breath and recover.

The Best Exercises for HIIT at Home

There are many different exercises that you can do during a HIIT workout at home, but some of the most effective ones include:

  • Jumping jacks
  • Burpees
  • Mountain climbers
  • High knees
  • Squats
  • Lunges
  • Push-ups
  • Plank

These exercises are great for getting your heart rate up and challenging your body. You can do each exercise for 30-60 seconds, followed by 10-20 seconds of rest or low-intensity exercise.

Creating Your Own HIIT Workout Plan

If you want to start doing HIIT workouts at home, the first step is to create your own workout plan. Here's how:

  1. Choose 4-6 exercises that you enjoy and that challenge your body.
  2. Decide how long you want to work out for (20-30 minutes is a good place to start).
  3. Set a timer for your high-intensity intervals and your rest periods.
  4. Do each exercise for the desired amount of time, followed by your rest period.
  5. Repeat the circuit 2-3 times for a complete workout.

Remember, the key to a successful HIIT workout is to push yourself during the high-intensity intervals. If you're not challenging yourself, you won't see the full benefits of the workout.

Question and Answer

Q: How many times a week should I do HIIT workouts at home?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times a week, with at least one day of rest in between each workout.

Q: Do I need any equipment to do HIIT workouts at home?

A: No, you don't need any equipment to do HIIT workouts at home. However, you may want to invest in a yoga mat for comfort during floor exercises.

Q: Can beginners do HIIT workouts at home?

A: Yes, beginners can do HIIT workouts at home. However, it's important to start slow and gradually increase the intensity of your workouts over time.

Q: Can HIIT workouts at home help me lose weight?

A: Yes, HIIT workouts at home can be a great way to lose weight. They are high-intensity and burn a lot of calories, which can help you lose fat and build muscle.

Conclusion of Best High Intensity Interval Training at Home

High intensity interval training at home is a great way to get a good workout in without spending a lot of time or money. By choosing the right exercises and creating a workout plan that challenges you, you can burn a lot of calories and build endurance in just 20-30 minutes a day. So why not give it a try and see how it can transform your fitness routine?
