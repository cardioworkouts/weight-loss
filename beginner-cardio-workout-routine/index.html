Beginner Cardio Workout Routine A Step By Step Guide

Written by Robby Oct 11, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a beginner looking to start a cardio workout routine but not sure where to begin? Do you feel intimidated by the gym and unsure of what exercises to do? Don't worry, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll provide you with a step-by-step guide to help you get started on your cardio fitness journey.

The Target of Beginner Cardio Workout Routine

The target of a beginner cardio workout routine is to help you improve your cardiovascular health and fitness level. Cardiovascular exercises increase your heart rate and breathing rate, which strengthens your heart and lungs. It also helps to burn calories, improve circulation, and reduce the risk of chronic diseases such as obesity, diabetes, and heart disease.

Before you start any exercise routine, it's important to consult with your doctor to ensure that you are healthy enough to exercise.

My Personal Experience with Beginner Cardio Workout Routine

When I first started my cardio workout routine, I was intimidated by the gym and unsure of what exercises to do. I decided to start with walking on the treadmill for 30 minutes a day, three days a week. As I got more comfortable, I increased my speed and incline. After a few weeks, I added in some cycling and rowing. Now, I do a mix of cardio exercises and feel stronger and healthier than ever before.

Remember, the key is to start slow and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts.

Types of Cardio Exercises for Beginners

There are many different types of cardio exercises that you can do as a beginner. Some of the most popular ones include:

  • Walking
  • Running
  • Cycling
  • Swimming
  • Rowing
  • Dancing

Choose an exercise that you enjoy and that fits your fitness level. Start with 10-15 minutes a day and gradually increase the duration and intensity of your workouts.

Tips for a Successful Beginner Cardio Workout Routine

Here are some tips to help you succeed in your beginner cardio workout routine:

  • Wear comfortable clothing and shoes
  • Stay hydrated by drinking water before, during, and after your workout
  • Warm up for 5-10 minutes before starting your workout
  • Cool down for 5-10 minutes after your workout
  • Track your progress by keeping a workout journal or using a fitness app

How to Stay Motivated with Beginner Cardio Workout Routine

Staying motivated is key to sticking with your cardio workout routine. Here are some tips to help you stay motivated:

  • Set achievable goals and track your progress
  • Find a workout buddy or join a fitness class
  • Switch up your routine to keep things interesting
  • Reward yourself for reaching your goals

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do cardio exercises as a beginner?

A: It's recommended that beginners aim for at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio exercise per week, spread out over several days. This can be broken down into 30 minutes of exercise, five days a week.

Q: What's the best time of day to do cardio exercises?

A: The best time of day to do cardio exercises is when you are most likely to stick to your routine. Some people prefer to exercise in the morning, while others prefer to exercise in the evening. Choose a time that works best for you.

Q: Do I need to invest in expensive equipment to do cardio exercises?

A: No, you don't need to invest in expensive equipment to do cardio exercises. Walking, running, cycling, and dancing are all affordable and effective ways to get your heart rate up.

Q: Can I do cardio exercises if I have a chronic health condition?

A: It's important to consult with your doctor before starting any exercise routine, especially if you have a chronic health condition. Your doctor can help you determine what types of exercises are safe and appropriate for you.

Conclusion of Beginner Cardio Workout Routine

Starting a cardio workout routine can be intimidating, but with the right mindset and approach, it can be a fun and rewarding experience. Remember to start slow, choose exercises that you enjoy, and stay motivated by setting achievable goals and tracking your progress. Before you know it, you'll be on your way to a healthier, stronger you.
