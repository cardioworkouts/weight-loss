The Best Fitness Music 2020 A Guide To Pump Up Your Workout Routine

Written by Eveline Nov 19, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of working out in silence? Do you find yourself struggling to stay motivated during your fitness routine? Look no further than the best fitness music of 2020 to help you power through your workouts and achieve your fitness goals.

Music has been scientifically proven to enhance athletic performance and increase motivation. The right playlist can push you to run faster, lift heavier, and work harder. But with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to choose the perfect workout soundtrack. That's where we come in.

In this guide, we will explore the best fitness music 2020 has to offer, including the top artists, genres, and playlists to help you reach your fitness peak. Whether you prefer high-energy pop hits or intense EDM beats, we have you covered.

Cardio: The Target of Best Fitness Music 2020

Cardio workouts such as running, cycling, and dancing require a high-energy soundtrack to keep you moving and motivated. The best fitness music for cardio includes fast-paced beats and catchy lyrics to help you push through the burn.

Personally, my go-to cardio playlist includes songs like "Blinding Lights" by The Weeknd, "Physical" by Dua Lipa, and "Adrenaline" by Zeds Dead. These songs have the perfect combination of tempo and energy to keep me going during my workouts.

Strength Training: The Target of Best Fitness Music 2020

Strength training requires a different type of energy than cardio. The best fitness music for strength training includes heavy bass lines and intense lyrics to help you power through your reps.

Some of my favorite strength training songs include "Lose Control" by Meduza, Becky Hill & GOODBOYS, "POWER" by Kanye West, and "Till I Collapse" by Eminem. These songs get me in the zone and help me lift heavier weights than I thought possible.

Yoga and Pilates: The Target of Best Fitness Music 2020

For yoga and Pilates, the best fitness music is calming and soothing, with a focus on relaxation and meditation. The right playlist can help you center your mind and find your inner peace.

Some of the best yoga and Pilates songs include "Weightless" by Marconi Union, "Bloom" by ODESZA, and "Namaste" by Yoga Pop Ups. These songs provide the perfect background for your practice, helping you find your flow and connect with your breath.

How to Create Your Perfect Workout Playlist

While there are plenty of pre-made workout playlists out there, creating your own personalized playlist can be even more effective. Here are some tips for creating the perfect workout soundtrack:

  • Choose songs with a tempo that matches your workout intensity
  • Include songs with motivational lyrics that inspire you
  • Add in some feel-good songs to boost your mood
  • Don't be afraid to mix genres and artists for a unique sound

Conclusion of Best Fitness Music 2020

Music can be a powerful tool in your fitness journey, providing motivation, energy, and a sense of accomplishment. By incorporating the best fitness music 2020 has to offer into your workouts, you can push yourself to new heights and achieve your goals.

Question and Answer

Q: Can listening to music improve my athletic performance?

A: Yes, studies have shown that music can enhance athletic performance by increasing motivation and decreasing perceived exertion.

Q: What type of music is best for running?

A: Fast-paced songs with a tempo of around 150-180 beats per minute are ideal for running, as they match the natural pace of your stride.

Q: How can I find new workout music?

A: Streaming services like Spotify and Apple Music offer curated workout playlists, as well as personalized recommendations based on your listening habits.

Q: Is it better to listen to music or podcasts during a workout?

A: It ultimately depends on personal preference, but listening to music has been shown to increase motivation and performance more than podcasts or audiobooks.
