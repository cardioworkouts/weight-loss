Best Free Workout Apps For Weight Loss Iphone

Written by Oliver Oct 27, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you struggling to lose weight and looking for a way to stay on track with your fitness goals? Do you find it hard to find time to go to the gym or afford a personal trainer? Don't worry, there are many free workout apps available for your iPhone that can help you achieve your weight loss goals.

The target of this article is to provide you with the best free workout apps for weight loss iPhone. These apps will help you to stay motivated, track your progress, and provide you with customized workouts that fit your fitness level and goals.

In summary, this article will cover the best free workout apps for weight loss iPhone, their features, and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals.

1. MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a free app that helps you track your calorie intake, set weight loss goals and track your progress. The app has a database of over 6 million foods, including restaurant meals and grocery items. It also has a barcode scanner that allows you to easily track your food intake.

The app provides you with a personalized calorie goal based on your weight, age, height, and weight loss goals. MyFitnessPal also syncs with other fitness apps and devices, such as Fitbit, Apple Health, and Garmin, to provide you with a complete picture of your fitness progress.

2. Nike Training Club

The Nike Training Club app is a free app that provides you with over 185 free workouts that range from beginner to advanced levels. The app also provides you with personalized workout recommendations based on your fitness level and goals.

Each workout includes step-by-step instructions and video demonstrations to ensure you perform exercises correctly. The app also allows you to track your progress and set reminders for your workouts.

3. 7 Minute Workout

If you have a busy schedule and don't have time to spend hours at the gym, the 7 Minute Workout app is perfect for you. The app provides you with high-intensity interval training workouts that only take 7 minutes to complete.

The app includes 12 exercises that target all major muscle groups and can be done anywhere without any equipment. The app also has a voice coach that guides you through each exercise and tracks your progress.

4. FitOn

FitOn is a free app that provides you with personalized workout plans that fit your fitness level and goals. The app has a variety of workouts, including HIIT, cardio, and yoga, that range from 5 to 45 minutes.

The app also has a social community where you can connect with other users, participate in challenges, and receive support from certified trainers. FitOn also syncs with other fitness apps, such as Apple Health and Fitbit, to track your progress.

How to choose the best free workout app for weight loss iPhone?

When choosing the best free workout app for weight loss iPhone, consider your fitness level, goals, and preferences. Look for apps that provide you with personalized workouts, track your progress, and provide you with support and motivation.

5. SWEAT

SWEAT is a free app that provides you with personalized workout plans that fit your fitness level and goals. The app has a variety of workouts, including strength training, cardio, and yoga, that range from 20 to 60 minutes.

The app also has a social community where you can connect with other users, participate in challenges, and receive support from certified trainers. SWEAT also provides you with nutrition plans and recipes to support your weight loss goals.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?
A: Yes, these apps provide workouts for all fitness levels, including beginner workouts.

Q: Do these apps require any equipment?
A: Some of the apps provide equipment-free workouts, while others require basic equipment such as dumbbells or resistance bands.

Q: Can I track my progress with these apps?
A: Yes, all of these apps provide you with the ability to track your progress and set goals.

Q: Are these apps available on Android devices?
A: Yes, most of these apps are available on both iOS and Android devices.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Apps for Weight Loss iPhone

In conclusion, there are many free workout apps available for your iPhone that can help you achieve your weight loss goals. These apps provide you with personalized workouts, track your progress, and provide you with support and motivation. Choose the app that fits your preferences and fitness level, and start your weight loss journey today!
