Fitness Blender Fat Burning Cardio Workout with Relaxing Cool Down: Cardio Section

As someone who struggles to motivate myself to workout, I was pleasantly surprised by how much I enjoyed Fitness Blender's Fat Burning Cardio Workout with Relaxing Cool Down. The cardio section is intense but manageable, and the variety of movements keeps things interesting. I found myself getting lost in the workout and before I knew it, I had completed the entire cardio section.

The cardio section is divided into three parts, each consisting of four exercises that are repeated twice. The exercises include jumping jacks, high knees, squat jumps, and more. Each exercise is performed for 45 seconds, followed by a 15 second rest period. The three parts are separated by a 60 second active rest period, where you perform a low intensity exercise such as marching in place or jogging on the spot.

The HIIT format of the cardio section is designed to increase your heart rate and keep it elevated throughout the workout, which helps you to burn fat and improve your cardiovascular endurance. It's a challenging workout, but the short rest periods make it manageable even for beginners.

Fitness Blender Fat Burning Cardio Workout with Relaxing Cool Down: Cool Down

The cool down section of Fitness Blender's Fat Burning Cardio Workout with Relaxing Cool Down is a welcome relief after the intensity of the cardio section. I found the cool down to be incredibly relaxing and it helped me to de-stress after a long day.

The cool down includes a variety of stretches for different muscle groups, including the hamstrings, hips, and lower back. Each stretch is held for 20-30 seconds, allowing you to really sink into the stretch and feel the tension release from your muscles. The cool down also includes some deep breathing exercises, which help to calm your mind and reduce stress.
