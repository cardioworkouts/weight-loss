How To Properly Do Hiit Cardio A Beginner s Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 29, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of boring, monotonous cardio workouts that seem to drag on forever? High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT) cardio may be just what you need to spice up your routine and get the results you want. However, it's important to know how to properly do HIIT cardio to avoid injury and achieve maximum benefits.

If you've tried HIIT cardio before and found it too difficult or too easy, you may not have been doing it correctly. Many people make common mistakes when doing HIIT cardio, such as not warming up properly or pushing themselves too hard too soon. These mistakes can lead to injury or burnout, and may even prevent you from seeing results.

So, how do you properly do HIIT cardio? First, it's important to understand what HIIT is and what it involves.

HIIT is a type of cardio exercise that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest or low-intensity activity. For example, you might sprint for 30 seconds and then walk for 60 seconds, repeating this cycle for a set amount of time. HIIT workouts can be done with various forms of cardio, including running, cycling, and jumping jacks.

To properly do HIIT cardio, follow these tips:

Start Slow

If you're new to HIIT cardio, start slow and gradually increase your intensity over time. Begin with shorter intervals and longer rest periods, and gradually work your way up to longer intervals and shorter rest periods. This will help your body adjust to the demands of HIIT cardio and prevent injury.

Warm Up

Before starting any workout, it's important to warm up properly. This can include light cardio, such as jogging or jumping jacks, and dynamic stretching to prepare your muscles for the workout ahead. Spend at least 5-10 minutes warming up before starting your HIIT workout.

Listen to Your Body

HIIT cardio is challenging, and it's normal to feel tired and out of breath during your workout. However, it's important to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard. If you feel dizzy, lightheaded, or experience any pain or discomfort, stop your workout and rest.

Stay Hydrated

Drink plenty of water before, during, and after your HIIT workout to stay hydrated. Dehydration can lead to fatigue, cramps, and other health issues, so it's important to drink water regularly throughout your workout.

Personal Experience with HIIT Cardio

When I first started doing HIIT cardio, I found it incredibly challenging. I would feel out of breath and exhausted after just a few minutes, and often felt like giving up. However, as I continued to work on my technique and gradually increase my intensity, I began to see results. Not only did I start to feel stronger and more energized during my workouts, but I also noticed improvements in my overall fitness level.

If you're new to HIIT cardio, I recommend starting with shorter intervals and longer rest periods, and gradually working your way up to longer intervals and shorter rest periods. Remember to warm up properly and listen to your body, and don't hesitate to take breaks if you need to.

Benefits of HIIT Cardio

HIIT cardio offers a variety of benefits for your physical and mental health. Some of the key benefits include:

  • Burns more calories than traditional cardio
  • Improves cardiovascular health
  • Increases muscle strength and endurance
  • Boosts metabolism
  • Reduces stress and anxiety

How Often Should You Do HIIT Cardio?

The frequency of your HIIT cardio workouts will depend on your fitness goals and current fitness level. Generally, it's recommended to do HIIT cardio 1-3 times per week, with at least one day of rest in between workouts. However, it's important to listen to your body and not overdo it.

How Long Should Your HIIT Workout Be?

A typical HIIT workout can range from 10-30 minutes, depending on your fitness level and the intensity of your intervals. It's important to keep your intervals short and intense to get the most benefits from your workout.

Conclusion of How to Properly Do HIIT Cardio

HIIT cardio can be a fun and effective way to improve your fitness level and achieve your weight loss goals. However, it's important to do it properly to avoid injury and burnout. Remember to start slow, warm up properly, listen to your body, stay hydrated, and gradually increase your intensity over time. With these tips in mind, you can start reaping the benefits of HIIT cardio today!
