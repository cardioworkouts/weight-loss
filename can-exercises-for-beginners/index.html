How to Do Can Exercises for Beginners

Here are some simple can exercises that you can do at home:

Bicep curls: Hold a can of food in each hand and stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Bend your elbows and bring the cans towards your shoulders, then lower them back down.

Tricep extensions: Hold a can of food in each hand and stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Raise your arms above your head, then bend your elbows and lower the cans behind your head. Raise them back up above your head.

Squats: Hold a can of food in each hand and stand with your feet shoulder-width apart. Bend your knees and lower yourself down as if you're sitting in a chair, then stand back up.
