Best Free Workout Timer App For Iphone A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Aug 29, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you tired of constantly checking the clock during your workout? Do you struggle with pacing yourself and keeping track of your progress? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with these pain points when it comes to working out. Fortunately, there's a solution: using a workout timer app.

If you're looking for the best free workout timer app for iPhone, you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll discuss the top options and help you choose the one that's right for you.

First and foremost, a workout timer app is designed to help you manage your time during your workouts. It can help you pace yourself, keep track of your progress, and stay motivated. Many apps also come with pre-programmed workouts or allow you to customize your own.

In this article, we'll cover the best free workout timer apps for iPhone, including their features, benefits, and drawbacks. We'll also provide tips on how to use them effectively.

The Best Free Workout Timer App for iPhone: Interval Timer

After testing out several options, we've determined that the best free workout timer app for iPhone is Interval Timer. This app is easy to use, customizable, and comes with a range of features to help you get the most out of your workouts.

Personally, I've been using Interval Timer for several months now and have found it to be incredibly helpful. I appreciate the flexibility it provides, allowing me to create and save custom intervals for different types of workouts. I also love that it allows me to label each interval, so I can easily keep track of what I'm doing.

One potential downside of Interval Timer is that it can be a bit overwhelming at first. There are a lot of options to choose from, and it can take some time to figure out how to use them all effectively. However, once you get the hang of it, the app is incredibly useful.

Other Options to Consider

While we believe that Interval Timer is the best free workout timer app for iPhone, there are several other options worth considering:

Seconds Pro

Seconds Pro is another popular option for workout timer apps. It's known for its simplicity and ease of use. However, some users have reported that the app can be buggy and may crash from time to time.

MyWOD

MyWOD is a workout timer app specifically designed for CrossFit workouts. It comes with pre-programmed workouts and allows you to track your progress over time. However, it's not as customizable as some of the other options on this list.

Tabata Stopwatch Pro

Tabata Stopwatch Pro is a timer app designed specifically for Tabata workouts. It comes with pre-programmed workouts and allows you to create custom workouts as well. However, some users have reported that the app can be difficult to navigate.

How to Use a Workout Timer App Effectively

While using a workout timer app can be incredibly helpful, it's important to use it effectively. Here are some tips to help you get the most out of your app:

1. Know Your Goals

Before you start using a workout timer app, it's important to know what you're trying to achieve. Are you trying to build muscle? Improve your endurance? Lose weight? Once you know your goals, you can use your app to create workouts that will help you achieve them.

2. Customize Your Intervals

Most workout timer apps allow you to customize your intervals. Take advantage of this feature to create workouts that are tailored to your needs. For example, if you're trying to build strength, you might want to create intervals that allow you to lift heavy weights for shorter periods of time.

3. Stay Focused

When you're using a workout timer app, it can be tempting to check your phone or get distracted by other things. However, it's important to stay focused on your workout. Use your app to keep yourself accountable and stay on track.

4. Track Your Progress

Finally, use your workout timer app to track your progress over time. This will help you see how far you've come and stay motivated to keep going.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I use a workout timer app for any type of workout?

A: Yes, most workout timer apps can be customized to work with any type of workout.

Q: Can I use a workout timer app without a gym membership?

A: Absolutely! Many workout timer apps allow you to create workouts that can be done at home or outside.

Q: Do I need to have a lot of experience with working out to use a timer app?

A: No, workout timer apps are designed to be user-friendly and accessible for people of all fitness levels.

Q: Are there any downsides to using a workout timer app?

A: One potential downside is that it can be easy to get distracted by your phone or the app itself. However, if you use the app effectively and stay focused on your workout, this shouldn't be a problem.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Timer App for iPhone

Using a workout timer app can be a game-changer when it comes to achieving your fitness goals. Whether you're looking to build muscle, improve your endurance, or lose weight, a timer app can help you stay on track and see real progress over time.

We believe that the best free workout timer app for iPhone is Interval Timer, thanks to its flexibility and range of features. However, there are several other options worth considering as well. Ultimately, the key is to find an app that works for you and use it effectively to achieve your fitness goals.
