Should You Do Cardio After Weights To Lose Weight

Written by Robby Jun 11, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to lose weight even after doing cardio and weight lifting? Are you confused about whether you should do cardio after weights to lose weight? If yes, then you are not alone. Many people struggle to lose weight even after following a strict diet and workout routine. In this post, we will discuss whether you should do cardio after weights to lose weight or not.

If you are hitting the gym regularly and not seeing results, it can be frustrating. You may feel like you are wasting your time and effort. You may also feel confused about whether you should do cardio after weights to lose weight or not. The truth is that there is no one-size-fits-all answer to this question. It depends on your fitness goals, body type, and workout routine.

If your goal is to lose weight, then doing cardio after weights can be an effective way to burn more calories. When you lift weights, your body burns calories to repair and build muscle. However, cardio can help you burn additional calories and improve your overall fitness level. It can also help you burn fat and lose weight.

In summary, doing cardio after weights can help you lose weight and improve your fitness level. However, it is important to keep in mind that everyone's body is different, and what works for one person may not work for another. It is important to listen to your body and adjust your workout routine accordingly.

My Personal Experience

When I first started my fitness journey, I was confused about whether I should do cardio after weights to lose weight. I tried different workout routines, but nothing seemed to work. Then I started doing cardio after weights, and I noticed a significant difference in my weight loss. I was able to burn more calories and lose weight faster. However, I also realized that it is important to listen to my body and adjust my workout routine accordingly.

Benefits of Doing Cardio After Weights to Lose Weight

1. Burns more calories: Doing cardio after weights can help you burn more calories and lose weight faster.

2. Improves cardiovascular health: Cardio can help improve your heart health and reduce the risk of heart disease.

3. Increases endurance: Doing cardio regularly can help you build endurance and improve your overall fitness level.

How to Do Cardio After Weights to Lose Weight

If you want to do cardio after weights to lose weight, there are a few things you should keep in mind:

1. Start slow: If you are new to cardio, start slow and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workout.

2. Mix it up: To avoid boredom and prevent injury, mix up your cardio routine. You can try running, cycling, swimming, or any other cardio exercise that you enjoy.

3. Fuel your body: To perform your best during cardio, make sure you fuel your body with nutritious food and plenty of water.

My Personal Experience

When I started doing cardio after weights, I had to adjust my workout routine to make sure I was fueling my body properly. I started eating more whole foods and drinking more water. I also started mixing up my cardio routine to keep things interesting.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do cardio before weights to lose weight?

A: Yes, you can do cardio before weights to lose weight. However, doing weights before cardio can help you burn more fat and lose weight faster.

Q: How much cardio should I do after weights to lose weight?

A: The amount of cardio you should do after weights depends on your fitness level and goals. It is recommended to do at least 30 minutes of cardio after weights to see results.

Q: What type of cardio is best for weight loss?

A: Any type of cardio can help you lose weight. However, high-intensity interval training (HIIT) is a popular cardio workout that has been shown to be effective for weight loss.

Q: Can I skip weights and do only cardio to lose weight?

A: While cardio can help you lose weight, weight lifting is also important for building muscle and improving your overall fitness level. It is recommended to incorporate both cardio and weight lifting into your workout routine.

Conclusion of Should You Do Cardio After Weights to Lose Weight

In conclusion, doing cardio after weights can be an effective way to lose weight and improve your overall fitness level. However, it is important to listen to your body and adjust your workout routine accordingly. Remember to start slow, mix up your cardio routine, and fuel your body with nutritious food and plenty of water. By following these tips, you can achieve your fitness goals and live a healthier life.
