How Much Time Per Day Should I Exercise

Written by Petter Jun 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Do you ever wonder how much time per day you should exercise to maintain good health? With so many different opinions out there, it can be hard to know what the right answer is. Maybe you're looking to lose weight or build muscle, or maybe you just want to stay healthy and active. Whatever your goals may be, it's important to understand how much exercise you should be doing each day to achieve them.

Many people struggle with the question of how much time per day they should exercise. Some worry that they will not have enough time to fit in a workout, while others may feel that they do not have enough energy or motivation to exercise regularly. Whatever your concerns may be, it's important to remember that every little bit counts when it comes to staying active and healthy.

The American Heart Association recommends at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 75 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per week, or a combination of both. This equates to about 30 minutes of exercise per day, five days a week. However, if you're looking to lose weight or build muscle, you may need to increase your exercise duration or intensity.

In summary, the amount of time per day you should exercise depends on your goals and your current fitness level. If you're just starting out, aim for 30 minutes of exercise per day, five days a week, and gradually increase the duration and intensity as you become more fit. If you're looking to lose weight or build muscle, you may need to do more exercise per day or increase the intensity of your workouts.

Personal Experience

I used to struggle with finding the time and motivation to exercise regularly. However, I found that by starting small and gradually increasing the duration and intensity of my workouts, I was able to make exercise a regular part of my routine. I now aim for 45 minutes of exercise per day, five days a week, and I feel healthier and more energized as a result.

The Importance of Consistency

Consistency is key when it comes to exercise. It's important to make exercise a regular part of your routine and to find activities that you enjoy. This will help you stay motivated and committed to your fitness goals.

The Benefits of Exercise

Exercise has numerous benefits for both your physical and mental health. It can help you maintain a healthy weight, reduce your risk of chronic diseases, improve your mood and cognitive function, and increase your overall quality of life.

Tips for Staying Motivated

Staying motivated to exercise can be challenging, especially when life gets busy. Here are some tips to help you stay on track:

  • Set realistic goals
  • Find an exercise buddy
  • Mix up your workouts
  • Reward yourself for reaching milestones

Personal Experience

I found that setting realistic goals and rewarding myself for reaching milestones helped me stay motivated to exercise. I also enjoy mixing up my workouts by trying new activities such as hiking or yoga, which keeps things interesting and fun.

Question and Answer

Q: How much time per day should I exercise if I want to lose weight?

A: If you're looking to lose weight, you may need to do more exercise per day or increase the intensity of your workouts. Aim for at least 60 minutes of moderate-intensity aerobic activity or 30 minutes of vigorous-intensity aerobic activity per day, in addition to strength training exercises.

Q: Can I exercise too much?

A: Yes, it is possible to exercise too much, especially if you're not giving your body enough time to rest and recover. It's important to listen to your body and take rest days when needed.

Q: Should I do cardio or strength training?

A: Both cardio and strength training are important for overall health and fitness. Aim for a combination of both throughout the week.

Q: Can I break up my exercise into smaller chunks throughout the day?

A: Yes, you can break up your exercise into smaller chunks throughout the day if that works better for your schedule. Just be sure to aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise per day.

Conclusion

So, how much time per day should you exercise? The answer depends on your goals and current fitness level. Aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise per day, five days a week, and gradually increase the duration and intensity as you become more fit. Remember to stay consistent, mix up your workouts, and listen to your body. With a little bit of effort and dedication, you can achieve your fitness goals and enjoy the many benefits of exercise.
