How Many Calories Will I Burn If I Workout For 2 Hours

Are you someone who is looking to lose weight or gain muscle? Do you often wonder how many calories you burn during your workout sessions? Well, you are not alone. Many people have the same question in mind. Knowing how many calories you burn during your workout can help you plan your diet and fitness goals accordingly.

So, how many calories will you burn if you workout for 2 hours? The answer to this question depends on various factors such as your age, weight, height, gender, and the type of exercise you are doing. However, on average, a person can burn anywhere between 500-1000 calories during a 2-hour workout session.

Factors That Affect the Number of Calories Burned During a 2-Hour Workout Session

As mentioned earlier, the number of calories burned during a 2-hour workout session depends on various factors. Let's take a closer look at these factors:

1. Age

Your age plays a crucial role in determining the number of calories you burn during a 2-hour workout session. As you grow older, your metabolism slows down, and you burn fewer calories. Therefore, an older person may burn fewer calories than a younger person during a 2-hour workout session.

2. Weight

The more you weigh, the more calories you burn during a 2-hour workout session. This is because it takes more energy to move a heavier body. So, if you weigh more, you will burn more calories during your workout.

3. Height

Your height also affects the number of calories you burn during a 2-hour workout session. Taller people have more muscle mass, which means they burn more calories than shorter people during a workout.

4. Gender

Men tend to burn more calories than women during a 2-hour workout session. This is because men generally have more muscle mass and less body fat than women.

5. Type of Exercise

The type of exercise you do also determines the number of calories you burn during a 2-hour workout session. Cardiovascular exercises such as running, cycling, and swimming burn more calories than strength training exercises such as weightlifting.

My Personal Experience

As someone who loves to workout, I can tell you that a 2-hour workout session is no joke. I remember the first time I did a 2-hour workout, and I was completely exhausted by the end of it. However, I felt accomplished and proud of myself for pushing through.

During my 2-hour workout sessions, I usually do a combination of cardiovascular exercises such as running, cycling, and rowing, along with strength training exercises such as weightlifting and bodyweight exercises. Depending on the intensity of my workout, I burn anywhere between 600-800 calories during a 2-hour workout session.

Tips to Burn More Calories During a 2-Hour Workout Session

If you want to burn more calories during your 2-hour workout session, here are some tips that can help:

1. Increase the Intensity of Your Workout

The higher the intensity of your workout, the more calories you burn. So, try to increase the intensity of your workout by doing high-intensity interval training (HIIT) or adding weights to your exercises.

2. Mix Cardio and Strength Training

Combining cardiovascular exercises with strength training can help you burn more calories during your 2-hour workout session. This is because strength training helps build muscle, which in turn increases your metabolism and helps you burn more calories throughout the day.

3. Stay Hydrated

Drinking water before, during, and after your workout can help you burn more calories. This is because water helps regulate your body temperature, which in turn helps you burn more calories.

4. Get Enough Rest

Make sure you get enough rest between your workout sessions. This will help your body recover and build muscle, which in turn helps you burn more calories during your next workout.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1. Will I burn more calories if I workout for longer than 2 hours?

A. Not necessarily. Your body can only burn a certain number of calories during a workout session. After a certain point, working out for longer periods may not yield any additional benefits.

Q2. How can I track the number of calories I burn during my 2-hour workout session?

A. You can use a fitness tracker or an app to track the number of calories you burn during your workout session.

Q3. Do I need to eat more if I burn more calories during my 2-hour workout session?

A. Yes, you need to eat more to fuel your body after a 2-hour workout session. Make sure to eat a healthy meal that includes protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

Q4. Can I burn more calories by working out every day?

A. Yes, working out every day can help you burn more calories. However, make sure to give your body enough rest between your workout sessions to avoid injury.

Conclusion of How Many Calories Will I Burn If I Workout for 2 Hours

So, how many calories will you burn if you workout for 2 hours? As we have seen in this article, the answer to this question depends on various factors such as your age, weight, height, gender, and the type of exercise you are doing. On average, a person can burn anywhere between 500-1000 calories during a 2-hour workout session. However, it is important to remember that the number of calories burned during a workout session is just one part of the equation for achieving your fitness goals. A healthy diet and lifestyle are also important factors in achieving your goals.
