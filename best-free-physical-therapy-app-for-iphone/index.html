If you're looking for a free physical therapy app for iPhone, you're in the right place. Our top choice for this category is the PT Pal Pro app. This app is designed to help you manage your pain and improve your mobility with a range of exercises and resources.

In addition to PT Pal Pro, there are many other great free physical therapy apps available for iPhone users. These include Physical Therapy Exercises, Stretching & Flexibility, and My Injury Exercises.
