10 Best Free Exercise Apps Uk Your Ultimate Guide To Fitness

Written by Daniel Jul 09, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of expensive gym memberships and not having enough time to exercise? Best free exercise apps UK can be the solution you've been looking for! With the rise of technology, you can now get fit from the comfort of your own home using just your smartphone or tablet.

Whether you're a beginner or a fitness enthusiast, finding the right exercise app that fits your needs and lifestyle can be challenging. With so many options available, it's hard to know where to start.

In this article, we'll guide you through the best free exercise apps UK and help you choose the one that's right for you. We'll cover everything from workout routines and tracking progress to nutrition and mental health.

So, if you're ready to take your fitness journey to the next level, keep reading!

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a free exercise app that offers personalized meal plans and workout routines based on your fitness goals. It also provides a calorie and nutrition tracker, making it easier to stay on top of your diet.

I've been using MyFitnessPal for a year now, and it's been a game-changer for me. The app is easy to use, and I love how it tracks my progress and provides healthy meal options.

FitOn

FitOn is a free exercise app that offers a variety of workout routines, including HIIT, yoga, and strength training. It's perfect for those who don't have access to a gym or want to switch up their workout routine.

I love using FitOn because it offers a wide range of workouts, and I can do them from the comfort of my own home. The app also tracks my progress, which keeps me motivated to continue my fitness journey.

Sworkit

Sworkit is a free exercise app that offers personalized workout routines based on your fitness level and goals. It also provides video demonstrations of each exercise, making it easy to follow along.

I recently started using Sworkit, and I love how it offers a variety of workouts that I can do from anywhere. The app is also easy to use, and I love how it tracks my progress.

C25K

C25K is a free exercise app that offers a beginner-friendly running program, perfect for those who want to start running but don't know where to begin. It offers a gradual increase in running time and distance over eight weeks.

I started using C25K a few months ago, and it's been a great way to start running. The app is easy to use, and I love how it gradually increases my running time and distance.

Conclusion of Best Free Exercise Apps UK

With these best free exercise apps UK, there's no excuse not to get fit! Whether you want to start running, do yoga, or track your nutrition, there's an app out there for you. Remember, consistency is key, so find an app that fits your needs and lifestyle and stick to it. Happy sweating!

Question and Answer

Q: Are these free exercise apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, these free exercise apps offer beginner-friendly workouts and programs that are perfect for those who are just starting.

Q: Can I use these free exercise apps without any equipment?

A: Yes, many of these free exercise apps offer workouts that don't require any equipment. However, some workouts may require basic equipment such as dumbbells or a yoga mat.

Q: Are these free exercise apps available for both iOS and Android?

A: Yes, all of these free exercise apps are available for both iOS and Android.

Q: Do I need to pay for a premium subscription to access all features of these free exercise apps?

A: No, all of the free exercise apps we've listed offer a range of features without the need for a premium subscription. However, some apps may offer additional features for a fee.
