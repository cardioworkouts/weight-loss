How to Do the Best Fat Burning HIIT Treadmill Workout?

Here are the steps to do the best fat burning HIIT treadmill workout:

Step 1: Warm-Up

Start with a 5-minute warm-up at a slow pace. This will get your body ready for the workout.

Step 2: High-Intensity Interval

For the next 30 seconds, run as fast as you can. This is the high-intensity interval.

Step 3: Low-Intensity Interval

Next, slow down to a walk for the next 60 seconds. This is the low-intensity interval.

Step 4: Repeat

Repeat steps 2 and 3 for a total of 10-15 minutes. As you get fitter, you can increase the time to 20-30 minutes.
