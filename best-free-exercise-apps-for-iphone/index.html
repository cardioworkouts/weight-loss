Best Free Exercise Apps For Iphone

Written by William Nov 21, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you looking for a way to stay fit and healthy without leaving your home? Do you want to have your own personal fitness trainer in your pocket? If yes, then you need to check out the best free exercise apps for iPhone. These apps can help you achieve your fitness goals and stay motivated throughout your fitness journey.

When it comes to fitness, many people struggle with finding the time, motivation, and resources to stay consistent. Going to the gym can be time-consuming, expensive, and sometimes intimidating. This is where fitness apps come in handy. With just your iPhone, you can access a wide range of free exercise apps that can help you stay fit and healthy.

If you are looking for the best free exercise apps for iPhone, then you have come to the right place. In this article, we will share with you some of the best free exercise apps for iPhone that you can download right now.

In summary, the best free exercise apps for iPhone are designed to help you stay fit and healthy without leaving your home or spending a lot of money. These apps offer a wide range of features, including workout plans, personalized coaching, progress tracking, and more.

1. FitOn

FitOn is a free exercise app for iPhone that offers a wide range of workout classes, including yoga, Pilates, strength training, and cardio. The app is designed to help you achieve your fitness goals by providing personalized workout plans and coaching. With FitOn, you can also track your progress and connect with other users for motivation and support.

Personally, I have been using FitOn for a few months now, and I am impressed with the variety of workouts and the quality of coaching. The app is easy to use, and the workout videos are well-produced and engaging. I also like the fact that I can track my progress and see how far I have come in my fitness journey.

2. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is another popular free exercise app for iPhone that offers a wide range of workouts, including bodyweight, cardio, and yoga. The app is designed to provide personalized coaching and motivation to help you achieve your fitness goals. With Nike Training Club, you can also track your progress and share your achievements with other users.

I have used Nike Training Club in the past, and I like the fact that the app offers a wide range of workouts and coaching styles. The app also has a social aspect, which allows you to connect with other users and share your progress. Overall, Nike Training Club is a great free exercise app for iPhone.

3. Daily Burn

Daily Burn is a free exercise app for iPhone that offers a wide range of workout classes, including cardio, strength training, and yoga. The app is designed to provide personalized coaching and motivation to help you achieve your fitness goals. With Daily Burn, you can also track your progress and connect with other users for support and motivation.

I have not personally used Daily Burn, but I have heard good things about the app from friends who have tried it. The app is known for its high-quality workout videos and personalized coaching. If you are looking for a free exercise app for iPhone that offers a wide range of workouts, then Daily Burn is definitely worth checking out.

4. 7 Minute Workout

7 Minute Workout is a free exercise app for iPhone that offers quick and effective workouts that you can do anywhere, anytime. The app is designed to provide a high-intensity workout in just seven minutes. With 7 Minute Workout, you can also track your progress and set reminders to do your daily workout.

I have used 7 Minute Workout in the past, and I like the fact that the app offers quick and effective workouts that can be done in just seven minutes. The app is perfect for busy people who don't have a lot of time to exercise. Overall, 7 Minute Workout is a great free exercise app for iPhone.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all the apps mentioned in this article are free to download and use. However, some apps may offer premium features that require a subscription.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the app and the workout you choose. Some workouts require equipment, while others can be done with just your bodyweight. Make sure to check the workout description before starting.

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, these apps offer workouts for all fitness levels, including beginners. The apps also provide coaching and modifications to help you progress at your own pace.

Q: Can I use these apps offline?

A: It depends on the app. Some apps require an internet connection to stream the workout videos, while others allow you to download the videos for offline use.

Conclusion of Best Free Exercise Apps for iPhone

In conclusion, if you are looking for a way to stay fit and healthy without leaving your home or spending a lot of money, then you need to check out the best free exercise apps for iPhone. These apps offer a wide range of workouts, personalized coaching, progress tracking, and more. So, download one of these apps today and start your fitness journey!
