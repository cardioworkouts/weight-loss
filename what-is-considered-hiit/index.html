What Is Considered Hiit A Beginner s Guide

Written by Aprilia Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Have you ever heard of HIIT and wondered what it means? Do you feel stuck in your current workout routine and want to switch things up? High-Intensity Interval Training, or HIIT, might be the answer you're looking for. HIIT is a popular exercise method that promises to help you burn fat, lose weight, and improve your overall fitness level. But what is considered HIIT, and why is it so effective? Let's dive in and find out.

Pain Points of Traditional Workouts

Many people struggle to see results from traditional workouts such as jogging, cycling, or lifting weights. These workouts can be time-consuming, monotonous, and often don't provide enough challenge to the body. Additionally, traditional workouts may not be suitable for individuals with certain health conditions or injuries. As a result, many people give up on their fitness goals or fail to reach their full potential.

What is Considered HIIT?

HIIT involves alternating periods of high-intensity exercise with periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. For example, you might sprint for 30 seconds, then walk for 60 seconds, and repeat this cycle for a set amount of time. HIIT workouts can be done with a variety of exercises, including running, cycling, jumping jacks, or bodyweight exercises such as burpees or push-ups.

HIIT is considered a form of cardiovascular exercise, but it also has benefits for muscle strength and endurance. HIIT workouts are typically shorter than traditional workouts, lasting anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes. However, they can be more challenging and effective in burning calories and improving fitness.

Personal Experience with HIIT

As someone who struggled to see results from traditional workouts, I was skeptical of HIIT at first. However, after incorporating HIIT into my routine, I noticed a significant improvement in my fitness level and body composition. HIIT workouts challenged me in ways that traditional workouts never did, and I found them to be more enjoyable and motivating.

Furthermore, HIIT allowed me to fit in a challenging workout even on my busiest days, as they require less time to complete. I highly recommend giving HIIT a try for anyone looking to switch up their routine and see results.

The Benefits of HIIT

Aside from burning calories and improving fitness, HIIT has several other benefits. For example, HIIT can improve insulin sensitivity, which can help prevent diabetes and improve overall health. Additionally, HIIT can increase the production of human growth hormone (HGH), which can aid in muscle growth and repair. Finally, HIIT has been shown to improve cognitive function and reduce stress levels.

Types of HIIT Workouts

There are several types of HIIT workouts, each with their own unique benefits and challenges. Some popular types of HIIT workouts include:

  • Tabata: a type of HIIT workout that involves 20 seconds of high-intensity exercise followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated for a total of 4 minutes.
  • Circuit Training: a type of HIIT workout that involves moving quickly between different exercises with little to no rest in between.
  • EMOM (Every Minute on the Minute): a type of HIIT workout that involves doing a specific exercise for a set number of reps at the beginning of each minute, then resting for the remainder of the minute.

Tips for a Successful HIIT Workout

If you're new to HIIT, it's important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts. Additionally, be sure to warm up properly before beginning your workout and cool down properly afterwards. Finally, remember to listen to your body and rest when needed. HIIT is challenging, but it should never cause pain or injury.

FAQs about HIIT

Q: Can HIIT be done at home?

A: Yes, HIIT can be done at home with little to no equipment. Bodyweight exercises such as burpees, push-ups, and squats can be combined into a HIIT workout.

Q: How often should I do HIIT?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week, with at least one day of rest in between each workout.

Q: Can anyone do HIIT?

A: HIIT can be modified to suit individuals of all fitness levels and abilities. However, individuals with certain health conditions or injuries should consult with a doctor before beginning a HIIT workout.

Q: How long should a HIIT workout last?

A: HIIT workouts can last anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes, depending on the intensity and type of exercises included.

Conclusion of What is Considered HIIT

In conclusion, HIIT is a highly effective and efficient form of exercise that can help you burn fat, lose weight, and improve your overall fitness level. By incorporating HIIT into your routine, you can challenge your body in new ways and see results that traditional workouts may not provide. Remember to start slowly, listen to your body, and enjoy the process!
