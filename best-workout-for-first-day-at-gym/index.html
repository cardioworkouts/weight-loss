This Best Workout For First Day At Gym For Beginner

Written by Daniel Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you ready to hit the gym for the first time but feeling overwhelmed and unsure of what to do? Don't worry, we've got you covered with the best workout for your first day at the gym.

Starting a new workout routine can be daunting, especially if you're new to the gym atmosphere. You may feel self-conscious or intimidated by the equipment and other gym-goers, but remember that everyone has to start somewhere.

The best workout for your first day at the gym should be a full-body workout that focuses on compound exercises. Compound exercises work multiple muscle groups at once, making them the most efficient use of your time and energy.

In summary, the best workout for your first day at the gym should include compound exercises that work multiple muscle groups at once. This will give you the most bang for your buck and help you build a strong foundation for your fitness journey.

Bodyweight Exercises

One of the easiest and most effective ways to start your fitness journey is with bodyweight exercises. These exercises require nothing but your own body weight and can be done anywhere, including the gym.

My personal experience with bodyweight exercises was a game-changer for my fitness journey. I started with basic exercises like push-ups, squats, and lunges and gradually progressed to more advanced movements like pistol squats and handstand push-ups.

Bodyweight exercises are perfect for beginners because they can be modified to suit your fitness level. For example, if regular push-ups are too difficult, you can start with modified push-ups on your knees. As you get stronger, you can progress to regular push-ups and eventually more advanced variations.

Resistance Training

Resistance training is another great option for your first day at the gym. Resistance training involves using weights or resistance bands to build strength and muscle mass.

When I first started resistance training, I was intimidated by the weight room and the equipment. But once I learned the proper form and technique, I fell in love with the challenge and the results.

If you're new to resistance training, start with light weights and focus on proper form and technique. Don't worry about lifting heavy weights right away. Building a strong foundation is more important than lifting heavy weights.

Circuit Training

Circuit training is a combination of bodyweight exercises and resistance training that involves performing a series of exercises with little to no rest in between. Circuit training is a great way to get a full-body workout in a short amount of time.

When I first tried circuit training, I was skeptical because I didn't think I could handle the intensity. But once I started, I was hooked. Circuit training challenged me both physically and mentally and helped me build endurance and strength.

How to Get Started

Now that you know the best workout for your first day at the gym, it's time to get started. Here are a few tips to help you on your journey:

  • Start slow and focus on proper form and technique
  • Listen to your body and don't push yourself too hard
  • Don't compare yourself to others. Everyone has their own fitness journey
  • Find a workout buddy or hire a personal trainer for extra motivation and guidance

Frequently Asked Questions

What should I bring to the gym on my first day?

Make sure to bring a towel, water bottle, and comfortable workout clothes and shoes. If you're unsure about what to bring, call the gym ahead of time and ask for their recommendations.

How long should my first workout be?

Your first workout should be around 30-45 minutes. It's important not to overdo it on your first day and risk injury or burnout.

How often should I work out?

It's recommended to aim for at least three workouts per week, with at least one day of rest in between. As you progress, you can increase the frequency and intensity of your workouts.

What if I'm not sure how to use the equipment?

Don't be afraid to ask for help! Most gyms have personal trainers or staff members who can show you how to use the equipment properly and safely. You can also find instructional videos online or hire a personal trainer for extra guidance.

Conclusion of Best Workout for First Day at Gym

Starting a new fitness journey can be scary, but the best workout for your first day at the gym is a full-body workout that focuses on compound exercises. Whether you choose bodyweight exercises, resistance training, or circuit training, remember to start slow, focus on proper form and technique, and listen to your body. With time and dedication, you'll be on your way to a stronger, healthier you.
