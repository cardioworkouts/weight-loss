Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is one of the best workout routine app iOS out there, and for a good reason. This app offers over 185 workouts ranging from bodyweight exercises to full gym routines. The best part? The app is entirely free. The workouts are designed by world-class Nike trainers and athletes and come with step-by-step instructions and videos to ensure you are performing each exercise correctly.

Personally, I've been using the Nike Training Club app for over a year now, and it has transformed my fitness journey. The app offers a wide range of workouts, from 15-minute quickies to full 60-minute routines, making it easy to fit in a workout, no matter how busy your schedule is.

Fitbod

Fitbod is another popular workout routine app iOS that offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals, experience level, and available equipment. The app uses machine learning algorithms to create a unique workout plan that adapts to your progress over time. Fitbod also provides detailed performance metrics, making it easy to track your progress and see how far you've come.

I've personally used Fitbod, and I love how easy it is to use. The app offers a variety of workout plans, including bodyweight exercises and gym routines, making it perfect for anyone, no matter their fitness level or available equipment.

Aaptiv

If you're someone who struggles with motivation during a workout, Aaptiv may be the perfect workout routine app iOS for you. This app offers audio-guided workouts led by certified personal trainers, making it feel like you have a personal trainer in your pocket. The app offers a wide range of workouts, including running, strength training, and yoga, and new workouts are added each week.

Personally, I've used Aaptiv during my runs, and I love how motivating the audio-guided workouts are. The trainers offer encouragement and helpful tips throughout the workout, making it easy to stay motivated and push through the tough parts.

Question and Answer

Q: Are workout routine apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, workout routine apps are perfect for beginners. Most apps offer workouts for all fitness levels, and you can choose a plan that works for you.

Q: Do I need gym equipment to use a workout routine app?
