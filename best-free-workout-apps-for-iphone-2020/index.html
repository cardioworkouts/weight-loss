The Best Free Workout Apps for iPhone 2020: A Comprehensive Guide

Are you looking for an easy way to stay fit and healthy without leaving your house? With the increasing popularity of smartphones, there are now more workout apps than ever before. However, with so many options available, it can be overwhelming to choose the right one. In this article, we will guide you through the best free workout apps for iPhone 2020 and help you find the perfect fit for your lifestyle.

The target of this article is to provide you with a detailed overview of the best free workout apps for iPhone 2020. We understand that not everyone has the time, money, or motivation to go to the gym regularly, and that's where these apps come in handy. With the right workout app, you can easily set fitness goals, monitor your progress, and achieve your desired results without leaving your house.

In this article, we will discuss some of the best free workout apps for iPhone 2020 that are available in the App Store. We will also provide you with a brief overview of each app's features, strengths, and weaknesses. That way, you can make an informed decision and choose the app that best suits your needs.

1. Nike Training Club

When it comes to the best free workout apps for iPhone 2020, Nike Training Club is a top contender. This app has a vast collection of free workouts, including strength, endurance, yoga, and mobility. The workouts are designed by professional trainers and athletes, making them both effective and challenging.

Personally, I have been using Nike Training Club for over a year now, and I love it. The app is easy to use, and the workouts are customizable, so you can choose the duration, intensity, and equipment you have available. Additionally, the app has a social feature that allows you to connect with friends and track each other's progress.

2. FitOn

FitOn is another excellent option for those looking for the best free workout apps for iPhone 2020. This app offers a wide variety of free workouts, including dance, yoga, Pilates, and strength training. The workouts are led by celebrity trainers, making them both fun and effective.

What I love about FitOn is that it offers personalized meal plans and nutrition advice, making it a one-stop-shop for all your fitness needs. The app also has a social feature that allows you to connect with friends and join virtual workout classes.

3. MyFitnessPal

While MyFitnessPal is not technically a workout app, it is a useful tool for anyone looking to improve their fitness and overall health. This app allows you to set fitness goals, track your food intake, and monitor your progress over time. The app also has an extensive database of foods, making it easy to log your meals and keep track of your calorie intake.

Personally, I have been using MyFitnessPal for years, and it has helped me maintain a healthy weight and lifestyle. The app is easy to use, and the premium version offers even more features, including personalized meal plans and recipe ideas.

4. Yoga Studio

If you're looking for a specific type of workout, such as yoga, then Yoga Studio is the app for you. This app offers a wide variety of yoga classes, including beginner, intermediate, and advanced levels. The classes are led by certified yoga instructors, making them both safe and effective.

What I love about Yoga Studio is that it allows you to customize your classes based on your fitness level and personal preferences. The app also has a library of over 70 yoga and meditation classes, making it easy to find a workout that suits your needs.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Apps for iPhone 2020

In conclusion, there are many excellent options when it comes to the best free workout apps for iPhone 2020. Whether you're looking for strength training, yoga, or nutrition advice, there is an app out there that can help you achieve your fitness goals. The key is to find an app that suits your lifestyle and preferences, and that you enjoy using.

Question and Answer:

Q: Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, all of these apps offer beginner-level workouts and allow you to customize your workouts based on your fitness level.

Q: Do these apps offer personalized meal plans?

A: Yes, some of these apps, such as FitOn and MyFitnessPal, offer personalized meal plans and nutrition advice.

Q: Are these apps free to use?

A: Yes, all of the apps mentioned in this article offer free versions with limited features. However, some apps offer premium versions with additional features for a fee.

Q: Can I track my progress using these apps?

A: Yes, all of these apps allow you to track your progress and monitor your fitness goals over time.
