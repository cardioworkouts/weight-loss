The Ultimate Guide To Best Running Music 2021 Boost Your Workout With These Tunes

Written by Oliver Aug 11, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of the same old playlist when you hit the pavement for a run? Do you find yourself getting bored or losing motivation halfway through your workout? If so, it might be time to switch up your running music. In this article, we'll explore the best running music 2021 has to offer and how it can help you power through your next workout.

Let's face it, running can be tough. It's easy to get in your head and start to doubt yourself, especially when you're pushing your limits. That's where music comes in. Listening to the right tunes can help distract you from the pain, boost your mood, and keep you motivated to keep going. But finding the right running music can be a challenge. With so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to know where to start.

The target of the best running music 2021 is to provide you with a list of songs that will help you stay motivated, energized, and focused during your next run. We've scoured the internet and compiled a list of the top running tunes that are sure to take your workout to the next level. From upbeat pop hits to motivational rap anthems, we've got you covered.

In this article, we'll provide you with a comprehensive list of the best running music 2021 has to offer, explain the benefits of running with music, discuss how to create the perfect running playlist, and answer some common questions about running with music. So let's get started!

The Benefits of Running with Music

Before we dive into the best running music 2021 has to offer, let's take a moment to discuss the benefits of running with music. First and foremost, music can help distract you from the physical discomfort of running. When you're listening to your favorite tunes, you're less likely to focus on the burning in your legs or the tightness in your chest. This distraction can help you push through your workout and stay focused on your goals.

Additionally, music has been shown to have a positive effect on mood. Listening to upbeat, high-energy music can help boost your mood and give you the confidence you need to tackle that next hill or sprint to the finish line. Running with music can also help improve your performance by synchronizing your movements with the beat of the music. This synchronization can help you run more efficiently and reduce the risk of injury.

Creating the Perfect Running Playlist

Now that you know the benefits of running with music, it's time to create the perfect running playlist. The key to a great running playlist is variety. You want to include a mix of fast-paced, high-energy songs as well as slower, more mellow tunes. This variety will help keep you engaged and motivated throughout your entire workout.

Another important factor to consider when creating a running playlist is the tempo of the music. The ideal tempo for running is between 120 and 140 beats per minute. This tempo has been shown to synchronize with the average runner's stride and can help improve performance. There are many apps and websites available that can help you find songs with the perfect tempo for your workout.

The Top Running Songs of 2021

Now that you know how to create the perfect running playlist, let's take a look at the top running songs of 2021. These songs are sure to get you pumped up and motivated to tackle your next run.

1. "Levitating" by Dua Lipa

This upbeat pop hit is perfect for a high-energy warm-up or a fast-paced run. With a tempo of 103 beats per minute, it's the perfect song to get you moving.

2. "Blinding Lights" by The Weeknd

This catchy tune has a tempo of 171 beats per minute, making it the perfect song for a fast-paced run or sprint. The upbeat rhythm and catchy lyrics will keep you motivated and energized throughout your workout.

3. "Good Days" by SZA

If you're looking for a slower, more mellow tune for your cool-down, "Good Days" is the perfect choice. With a tempo of 60 beats per minute, it's the ideal song to help you wind down after a tough workout.

4. "Montero (Call Me By Your Name)" by Lil Nas X

This motivational rap anthem has a tempo of 178 beats per minute, making it the perfect song for a high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workout or a fast-paced run. The empowering lyrics and catchy beat will keep you motivated and energized throughout your entire workout.

Common Questions About Running with Music

Q: Is it safe to run with headphones?

A: While running with headphones can be a great way to stay motivated and energized during your workout, it's important to be aware of your surroundings. Make sure you're running in a safe area and always be aware of traffic and other potential hazards.

Q: Can running with music help me run faster?

A: Yes, running with music can help improve your performance by synchronizing your movements with the beat of the music. This synchronization can help you run more efficiently and reduce the risk of injury.

Q: What are some tips for creating the perfect running playlist?

A: When creating a running playlist, it's important to include a mix of fast-paced, high-energy songs as well as slower, more mellow tunes. You also want to consider the tempo of the music, aiming for songs between 120 and 140 beats per minute.

Q: What are the benefits of running with music?

A: Running with music can help distract you from the physical discomfort of running, boost your mood, and improve your performance by synchronizing your movements with the beat of the music.

Conclusion of Best Running Music 2021

Whether you're a seasoned runner or just starting out, the right running music can make all the difference. By creating the perfect playlist and incorporating some of the top running songs of 2021, you can stay motivated, energized, and focused throughout your entire workout. So plug in those headphones, hit play, and let the music carry you to the finish line!
