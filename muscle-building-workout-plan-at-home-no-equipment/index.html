Muscle Building Workout Plan At Home No Equipment A Step By Step Guide

Written by William Jun 15, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym and spending a lot of money on gym memberships and equipment? Do you want to build muscle but don't have the time or resources to go to a gym? Well, we have good news for you! You can build muscle at home without any equipment. Yes, you read that right! In this blog post, we will guide you through a muscle building workout plan at home no equipment.

Building muscle is not an easy task, and it requires a lot of hard work and dedication. Muscle building workout plan at home no equipment can be challenging, but it is possible. People often face pain points like lack of motivation, time, and equipment when trying to build muscles at home. But with our muscle building workout plan at home no equipment, you can overcome these pain points and achieve your fitness goals.

Muscle Building Workout Plan at Home No Equipment - What is it?

The muscle building workout plan at home no equipment is a workout routine that focuses on building muscle without using any equipment. This workout plan is perfect for people who want to build muscle but don't have access to a gym or equipment. The muscle building workout plan at home no equipment consists of exercises that use your body weight to build muscle.

In this workout plan, you will perform exercises like push-ups, squats, lunges, and planks. These exercises are known as compound exercises, which means they work multiple muscle groups at once. Compound exercises are great for building muscle because they stimulate a lot of muscle fibers in a short amount of time.

Why Choose the Muscle Building Workout Plan at Home No Equipment?

There are several reasons why you should choose the muscle building workout plan at home no equipment. Firstly, it is a cost-effective way to build muscle. You don't need to spend any money on gym memberships or equipment. Secondly, it is a time-efficient way to build muscle. You can perform these exercises at home, and you don't need to spend any time traveling to and from the gym.

Lastly, the muscle building workout plan at home no equipment is a great way to challenge yourself. These exercises can be challenging, and you can easily adjust the intensity to suit your fitness level. As you get stronger and fitter, you can increase the intensity of the exercises and challenge yourself even more.

How to Follow the Muscle Building Workout Plan at Home No Equipment?

The muscle building workout plan at home no equipment is a six-day workout routine that targets different muscle groups each day. The workout plan is as follows:

Day 1: Chest and Triceps

  • Push-ups - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Tricep dips - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Close grip push-ups - 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 2: Back and Biceps

  • Superman - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Chin-ups - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Hammer curls - 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 3: Legs

  • Squats - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Lunges - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Calf raises - 3 sets of 10 reps

Day 4: Shoulders and Abs

  • Pike push-ups - 3 sets of 10 reps
  • Plank - 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Side plank - 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 5: Cardio

  • Jumping jacks - 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • High knees - 3 sets of 30 seconds
  • Butt kicks - 3 sets of 30 seconds

Day 6: Rest Day

Remember to warm-up before each workout and cool down after each workout. You can also adjust the number of sets and reps to suit your fitness level.

Benefits of Muscle Building Workout Plan at Home No Equipment

The muscle building workout plan at home no equipment has several benefits. Firstly, it is a cost-effective way to build muscle. You don't need to spend any money on gym memberships or equipment. As you get stronger and fitter, you can increase the intensity of the exercises and challenge yourself even more.
