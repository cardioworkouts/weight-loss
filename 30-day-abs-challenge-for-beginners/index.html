The Exercises in 30 Day Abs Challenge for Beginners

The 30-day abs challenge for beginners includes a variety of exercises that are designed to target your entire core. These exercises include:

How to Do the 30 Day Abs Challenge for Beginners

To do the 30-day abs challenge for beginners, follow these simple steps:

  1. Find a comfortable spot to do your exercises, such as a yoga mat or carpeted floor.
  2. Set a timer for 10-15 minutes, depending on how much time you have.
  3. Do each exercise for 30 seconds, then rest for 10 seconds before moving on to the next exercise.
  4. After you have completed all of the exercises, rest for 30 seconds, then repeat the circuit two more times.
  5. Do the challenge every day for 30 days, gradually increasing the number of reps as you get stronger.
