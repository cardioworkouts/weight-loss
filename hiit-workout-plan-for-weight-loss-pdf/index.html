Question and Answer

Q: Is HIIT good for weight loss?

A: Yes, HIIT is an effective way to lose weight, as it increases your heart rate and burns calories.

Q: How often should I do HIIT workouts?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between.

Q: How long should a HIIT workout be?

A: A typical HIIT workout should last around 30 minutes, including warm-up and cool-down.

A: Yes, you can do HIIT workouts at home with little to no equipment. There are many HIIT workout videos available online that you can follow along with.
