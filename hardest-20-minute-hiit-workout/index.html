How to Perform Hardest 20 Minute HIIT Workout

The hardest 20 minute HIIT workout involves several exercises that target different muscle groups. Here's how to perform the workout:

1. Warm-up

Start with a 5-minute warm-up that includes dynamic stretching exercises. This will help to prepare your body for the workout and reduce the risk of injury.

2. High-Intensity Interval Training

Perform each exercise for 30 seconds, followed by a 10-second rest. Repeat each exercise for a total of 4 rounds before moving on to the next exercise. Rest for 1 minute between each exercise.

The exercises are:

  • Burpees
  • Mountain climbers
  • Jump squats
  • Push-ups

3. Cool-down

Finish with a 5-minute cool-down that includes static stretching exercises. This will help to reduce muscle soreness and improve flexibility.
