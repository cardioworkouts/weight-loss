How Many Calories Does 20 Minutes Of Abs Workout Burn

Written by Luffy Sep 12, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you looking to burn some extra calories and tone your abs at the same time? Maybe you're curious about how many calories a 20-minute abs workout can burn? Well, you've come to the right place! In this blog post, we'll explore how many calories a 20-minute abs workout can burn and give you some tips on how to maximize your results.

When it comes to burning calories, every little bit counts. If you're looking to lose weight or maintain your current weight, adding a 20-minute abs workout to your fitness routine can help you reach your goals. However, it's important to note that spot reduction is not possible, which means you can't just target your abs and expect to lose fat in that area only.

How Many Calories Does 20 Minutes of Abs Workout Burn?

The number of calories burned during a 20-minute abs workout can vary depending on several factors, including your weight, age, sex, and the intensity of your workout. On average, a person can burn anywhere from 80 to 150 calories during a 20-minute abs workout.

To maximize your calorie burn, it's important to keep your heart rate up and perform exercises that engage multiple muscle groups. This could include exercises like planks, mountain climbers, and bicycle crunches. It's also important to remember to take breaks as needed and to listen to your body.

My Personal Experience

As someone who regularly incorporates abs workouts into my fitness routine, I can attest to the fact that they can be a great way to burn some extra calories and tone your midsection. I typically aim for a 20-30 minute abs workout, which includes a mix of exercises targeting different areas of my abs.

While I don't track the exact number of calories burned during my workouts, I can feel my heart rate increase and my abs working hard. I also notice improvements in my overall strength and endurance over time, which is a great motivator to keep going.

Tips for Maximizing Your Results

If you're looking to maximize your calorie burn during a 20-minute abs workout, there are a few tips you can keep in mind:

1. Focus on Compound Exercises

Compound exercises engage multiple muscle groups at once, which can help you burn more calories in less time. Some examples of compound exercises for your abs include the plank, mountain climbers, and Russian twists.

2. Increase the Intensity

The more intense your workout, the more calories you'll burn. You can increase the intensity of your abs workout by adding weights, increasing the number of reps or sets, or shortening your rest periods.

Final Thoughts

A 20-minute abs workout can be a great way to burn some extra calories and tone your midsection. While the number of calories burned can vary based on several factors, it's important to remember that every little bit counts. By incorporating compound exercises and increasing the intensity of your workout, you can maximize your results and achieve your fitness goals.

Question and Answer

Q: Can you lose belly fat by doing 20-minute abs workouts?

A: While abs workouts can help tone your midsection and improve your overall fitness, spot reduction is not possible. To lose belly fat, you need to create a calorie deficit through a combination of diet and exercise.

Q: How often should I do 20-minute abs workouts?

A: It's recommended to do 20-minute abs workouts 2-3 times per week in addition to a well-rounded fitness routine that includes cardio and strength training.

Q: Are 20-minute abs workouts suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, 20-minute abs workouts can be suitable for beginners as long as they start with basic exercises and progress at their own pace. It's important to listen to your body and take breaks as needed.

Q: Do I need any equipment for a 20-minute abs workout?

A: No, you don't need any equipment for a 20-minute abs workout. However, adding weights or resistance bands can increase the intensity of your workout.

Conclusion of How Many Calories Does 20 Minutes of Abs Workout Burn

In conclusion, a 20-minute abs workout can be a great way to burn some extra calories and tone your midsection. By incorporating compound exercises and increasing the intensity of your workout, you can maximize your results and achieve your fitness goals. Remember to listen to your body and progress at your own pace, and most importantly, have fun!
