Written by Daniel Nov 27, 2023 · 6 min read

Are you someone who enjoys working out on an empty stomach? Or do you prefer to have a little something in your system before you hit the gym? Whatever your preference, one question that often comes up is whether or not it's okay to take pre workout before fasted cardio. In this post, we'll dive into this topic and explore the pros and cons of taking pre workout before fasted cardio.

For many people, fasted cardio is a great way to burn fat and lose weight. By exercising on an empty stomach, your body is forced to use stored fat as fuel, which can lead to more efficient fat loss. However, working out on an empty stomach can also lead to low energy levels, dizziness, and fatigue. This is where pre workout supplements come in.

The short answer is yes, you can take pre workout before fasted cardio. In fact, many people find that taking pre workout before fasted cardio can help increase energy levels and endurance, allowing them to work out more effectively. However, there are some things to keep in mind when it comes to taking pre workout before fasted cardio.

First and foremost, it's important to choose a pre workout supplement that is specifically designed for fasted cardio. Look for supplements that are low in calories and carbohydrates, as these can interfere with your body's ability to burn fat. Additionally, it's important to read the label and make sure that the supplement does not contain any ingredients that could cause stomach upset or other negative side effects.

In summary, taking pre workout before fasted cardio can be a great way to increase energy levels and endurance, allowing you to work out more effectively. However, it's important to choose the right supplement and read the label carefully to avoid any negative side effects.
