Q: Is it possible to do crossfit workouts at home without any equipment?

A: While some crossfit workouts do require equipment, there are many that can be done without any equipment at all. For example, bodyweight exercises such as push-ups, squats, and lunges can be done anywhere, without any equipment.

Q: How often should I do crossfit workouts?

A: It is recommended that you do crossfit workouts 3-4 times per week, with rest days in between. This will give your body time to recover and prevent injury.
