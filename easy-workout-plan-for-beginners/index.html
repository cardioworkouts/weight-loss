Easy Workout Plan For Beginners A Guide To Get Started

Written by Bardi Aug 25, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you one of those who want to start working out but don't know where to begin? Do you find yourself clueless about what exercises to do and how to do them? Starting a workout routine can be overwhelming, especially if you're a beginner. But don't worry; you're not alone in this. Many people struggle with the same issues when starting their fitness journey.

If you're struggling to come up with an easy workout plan for beginners or don't know where to start, this guide is for you. This post will help you with a step-by-step guide on how to create an easy workout plan for beginners to get you started.

First and foremost, the target of an easy workout plan for beginners is to ease you into the fitness routine while minimizing injury risks. The workouts should be simple, easy to follow, and require no equipment. The idea is to help you develop a habit of exercising regularly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of the workouts.

In summary, an easy workout plan for beginners should help you:

  • Start exercising regularly
  • Minimize the risk of injury
  • Keep the workouts simple and easy to follow
  • Gradually increase the intensity and duration of the workouts

Cardiovascular Exercise

Cardiovascular exercise is an essential part of any workout plan, especially for beginners. It helps improve heart health, burn calories, and improve overall fitness. Start with a simple and easy activity like walking or jogging for 20-30 minutes per session. You can gradually increase the duration and intensity of the workouts as you progress.

Personally, I started with a 20-minute walk around my neighborhood. It was easy, and I didn't have to worry about any complicated exercises. Once I got comfortable, I started jogging slowly for a few minutes, gradually increasing the duration and intensity.

Strength Training

Strength training helps build muscle, improve bone density, and boost metabolism. Start with bodyweight exercises like squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks. These exercises don't require any equipment and can be done at home. Try doing three sets of 10-12 repetitions for each exercise.

When I started strength training, I did three sets of 10 squats, lunges, push-ups, and planks. It was challenging at first, but I gradually increased the repetitions and added more exercises like crunches and tricep dips.

Flexibility Exercises

Flexibility exercises help improve mobility, reduce stiffness, and prevent injury. Start with simple stretches like neck rolls, shoulder stretches, and hamstring stretches. Hold each stretch for 15-30 seconds and repeat two to three times on each side.

Personally, I found flexibility exercises the most challenging as I was not very flexible. But I persisted and gradually improved my flexibility by doing simple stretches every day.

How to Create an Easy Workout Plan for Beginners

Creating an easy workout plan for beginners is not rocket science. Here are some steps you can follow:

  1. Set realistic goals
  2. Choose activities you enjoy
  3. Start with simple exercises
  4. Gradually increase the intensity and duration of the workouts
  5. Be consistent with your workouts
  6. Track your progress

Benefits of an Easy Workout Plan for Beginners

Starting your fitness journey with an easy workout plan for beginners has several benefits. It helps you build a habit of exercising regularly, improves your overall fitness, and boosts your confidence. It also minimizes the risk of injury and helps you avoid burnout.

Personally, I found that starting with an easy workout plan helped me build a habit of exercising regularly. It also helped me gain confidence and motivated me to push myself further.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should beginners exercise?

A: Beginners should aim to exercise at least three to four times a week to see results.

Q: How long should a beginner workout session be?

A: Beginners should aim for a 30-minute workout session, gradually increasing the duration as they progress.

Q: Do I need equipment for an easy workout plan for beginners?

A: No, an easy workout plan for beginners does not require any equipment. You can use your body weight for strength training exercises and do cardio exercises like walking or jogging.

Q: How long does it take to see results from an easy workout plan for beginners?

A: It depends on several factors like age, gender, and fitness level. However, you can expect to see noticeable results in four to six weeks if you're consistent with your workouts.

Conclusion of Easy Workout Plan for Beginners

Starting a fitness journey can be daunting, but an easy workout plan for beginners can make it simple and achievable. Remember to set realistic goals, choose activities you enjoy, start with simple exercises, and gradually increase the intensity and duration of the workouts. Be consistent, track your progress, and don't forget to have fun!
