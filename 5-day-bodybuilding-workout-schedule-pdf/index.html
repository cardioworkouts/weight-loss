The 5 day bodybuilding workout schedule PDF is a comprehensive workout plan that spans five days a week. The workout schedule also includes cardio and rest days to ensure your body gets proper recovery time.

How to Follow the 5 Day Bodybuilding Workout Schedule PDF

Here's a breakdown of the 5 day bodybuilding workout schedule PDF:

Day 1: Chest and Triceps

On day 1, you'll focus on chest and triceps exercises. This includes bench press, incline bench press, dumbbell flyes, tricep pushdowns, and skull crushers.

Day 2: Back and Biceps

On day 2, you'll focus on back and biceps exercises. This includes pull-ups, lat pulldowns, rows, bicep curls, and hammer curls.

Day 3: Legs and Abs

On day 3, you'll focus on leg and ab exercises. This includes squats, lunges, leg press, calf raises, and various ab exercises.

Day 4: Shoulders and Traps

On day 4, you'll focus on shoulder and trap exercises. This includes military press, lateral raises, front raises, rear delt flyes, and shrugs.

Day 5: Arms and Abs

On day 5, you'll focus on arm and ab exercises. This includes bicep curls, tricep pushdowns, hammer curls, various ab exercises, and cardio.
