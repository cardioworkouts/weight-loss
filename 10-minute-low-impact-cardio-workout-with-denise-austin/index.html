What Exercises are Included in Denise Austin's 10 Minute Low Impact Cardio Workout?

The workout includes a warm up, a series of cardio exercises, and a cool down. The cardio exercises include marching in place, side taps, knee lifts, and more. Each exercise is designed to get your heart rate up and burn calories while minimizing the impact on your joints.

How Do I Do Denise Austin's 10 Minute Low Impact Cardio Workout?

To do this workout, all you need is a small space and a willingness to move your body. Start with the warm up, then move on to the cardio exercises. Follow Denise's instructions and do each exercise for 30 seconds to 1 minute. Once you've completed all the exercises, finish with the cool down.

What are the Benefits of Denise Austin's 10 Minute Low Impact Cardio Workout?
