Fitness Blender Strength Training Bodyweight: Plyometric Exercises

Plyometric exercises are a great way to build power and explosiveness in your muscles. Plyometric exercises involve jumping and explosive movements that engage your fast-twitch muscle fibers.

Some examples of plyometric exercises that are included in the Fitness Blender Strength Training Bodyweight program include jump squats, burpees, and jump lunges. These exercises are challenging but can be modified to fit your fitness level.

If you want to build power and explosiveness in your muscles, try incorporating plyometric exercises into your workout routine.

Fitness Blender Strength Training Bodyweight: How to Modify Exercises

If you're new to strength training or have an injury that limits your mobility, you may need to modify some of the exercises in the Fitness Blender Strength Training Bodyweight program. Here are some tips for modifying exercises:

1. Reduce the range of motion: If an exercise requires a full range of motion that is too challenging for you, reduce the range of motion until you build up your strength and flexibility.

2. Use a chair or bench for support: If an exercise requires you to balance on one leg or arm, use a chair or bench for support until you build up your balance and stability.

3. Use a resistance band: If an exercise requires resistance that is too challenging for you, use a resistance band to provide additional resistance without adding weight.

By modifying exercises, you can still get a challenging workout without overexerting yourself or risking injury.
