What Is The Best Workout Dvd For Beginners

Written by Robby Nov 09, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a beginner looking for a way to get fit without going to a gym or hiring a personal trainer? Workout DVDs can be a great solution for you! They offer a variety of workouts that you can do in the comfort of your own home, at your own pace. But with so many options out there, it can be overwhelming to figure out what is the best workout DVD for beginners.

Starting a new workout routine can be intimidating, especially if you're not sure where to begin. You may be worried about not knowing the proper form, or struggling to keep up with the pace of the workout. It's important to find a workout DVD that is designed for beginners, so you can build a strong foundation and avoid injury.

The best workout DVD for beginners is one that offers a variety of workouts, including cardio, strength training, and stretching. It should also provide clear instructions and modifications for different fitness levels. Here are some top picks:

Jillian Michaels Beginner Shred

Jillian Michaels is a well-known fitness trainer, and her Beginner Shred DVD is a great choice for those just starting out. It includes three 20-minute workouts that focus on cardio, strength, and abs. Michaels provides clear instructions and modifications for each exercise, making it easy to follow along.

Zumba Fitness Incredible Results

If you're looking for a fun way to get in shape, Zumba Fitness Incredible Results may be the DVD for you. It includes six workouts that combine dance and cardio, with easy-to-follow steps. The DVD also includes a nutrition plan and workout calendar to help you stay on track.

Beachbody PiYo

PiYo is a combination of Pilates and yoga, designed to help you build strength and flexibility. Beachbody PiYo includes 10 workouts that range from 25 to 45 minutes, with modifications for different fitness levels. The DVD also includes a nutrition plan and workout calendar.

Leslie Sansone Walk at Home

Walking is a great low-impact exercise, and Leslie Sansone's Walk at Home DVDs make it easy to get your steps in. The DVDs include a variety of workouts, from one mile to five miles, with different intensity levels. Sansone provides encouragement and motivation throughout the workouts.

Question and Answer

Q: What should I look for when choosing a workout DVD for beginners?

A: Look for a DVD that includes a variety of workouts, clear instructions and modifications for different fitness levels, and a recommended workout schedule.

Q: Are there any workout DVDs that are specifically designed for older adults?

A: Yes, there are several workout DVDs that are designed for older adults, such as Gentle Yoga with Jane Adams and Chair Aerobics for Everyone with Melanie Toner.

Q: Can I lose weight with a workout DVD?

A: Yes, you can lose weight with a workout DVD, as long as you are following a healthy diet and doing the workouts consistently.

Q: How often should I do a workout DVD?

A: It's recommended to do a workout DVD at least three times a week, and to switch up the workouts to prevent boredom and plateauing.

Conclusion of What is the Best Workout DVD for Beginners

When it comes to finding the best workout DVD for beginners, it's important to choose one that is designed for your fitness level and offers a variety of workouts. Jillian Michaels Beginner Shred, Zumba Fitness Incredible Results, Beachbody PiYo, and Leslie Sansone Walk at Home are all great options to consider. Remember to stay consistent and listen to your body, and you'll be on your way to a healthier, happier you!
