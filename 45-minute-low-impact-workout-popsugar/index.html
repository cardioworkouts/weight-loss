The Benefits of a 45 Minute Low Impact Workout

As someone who has struggled with joint pain for years, I was hesitant to try high impact exercises. That's why I was so excited to discover the Popsugar 45 minute low impact workout. This workout has all the benefits of a high impact workout, without the pain and discomfort that often comes with it.

One of the biggest benefits of a low impact workout is that it is gentle on your joints. If you have an injury or chronic pain, high impact exercises can make your symptoms worse. Low impact exercises, on the other hand, are designed to be gentle on your body, while still providing a great workout.

Another benefit of a low impact workout is that it can be done by people of all fitness levels. Whether you are a beginner or an experienced athlete, you can modify the exercises to meet your needs. This means that you can challenge yourself without putting unnecessary stress on your body.

The Popsugar 45 Minute Low Impact Workout

The Popsugar 45 minute low impact workout is designed to provide a full body workout that will help you burn calories and build muscle, without putting stress on your joints. This workout includes a combination of cardio and strength training exercises, so you get the benefits of both types of exercises.

One of my favorite exercises in this workout is the side plank with leg lift. This exercise is great for targeting your core and your glutes, while also improving your balance. To do this exercise, start in a side plank position with your elbow on the ground and your feet stacked on top of each other. Lift your top leg up and hold for a few seconds, then lower it back down. Repeat on the other side.

Another great exercise in this workout is the squat to overhead press. This exercise targets your legs, glutes, shoulders, and arms, making it a great full body exercise. To do this exercise, start with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a weight in each hand. Squat down, then stand up and lift the weights above your head. Lower the weights back down and repeat.

How to Modify the Popsugar 45 Minute Low Impact Workout

If you are a beginner, or if you have an injury or chronic pain, you may need to modify some of the exercises in the Popsugar 45 minute low impact workout. Here are some modifications you can make:

- If you have knee pain, you can do a modified lunge instead of a regular lunge.

- If you have shoulder pain, you can do a modified push-up instead of a regular push-up.

- If you are a beginner, you can start with lighter weights or no weights at all.

How to Make the Most of Your Popsugar 45 Minute Low Impact Workout

If you want to get the most out of your Popsugar 45 minute low impact workout, here are some tips:

- Wear comfortable clothing and shoes that are appropriate for your workout.

- Focus on your form and technique to get the most benefit from each exercise.

Q: Can I do the Popsugar 45 minute low impact workout at home?
