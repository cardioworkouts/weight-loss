The Best Time To Do Hiit Workout A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Aprilia Oct 24, 2023 Â· 7 min read

Are you wondering what is the best time to do HIIT workout? Do you want to maximize your results and achieve your fitness goals? If you are struggling to find the right time to do your high-intensity interval training, you are not alone. Many people are unsure about when to schedule their HIIT sessions and how to fit them into their busy schedules.

HIIT workouts are intense and demanding, and they require a lot of energy and effort. Therefore, it is essential to choose the right time to do them to avoid burnout, fatigue, and injury. Additionally, the timing can affect your performance, motivation, and recovery, which can impact your overall fitness progress.

The best time to do HIIT workout depends on various factors, such as your goals, preferences, schedule, and health status. However, there are some general guidelines and recommendations that can help you determine the ideal time for your HIIT sessions.

In this article, we will answer the question of what is the best time to do HIIT workout and provide you with practical tips and insights to help you optimize your training and achieve your desired outcomes.

My Personal Experience with HIIT Workout Timing

As a fitness enthusiast and personal trainer, I have tried different approaches and schedules for my HIIT workouts over the years. I have experimented with morning, afternoon, and evening sessions, and I have found that each time has its advantages and disadvantages.

For instance, when I do HIIT workouts in the morning, I feel more energized, focused, and productive throughout the day. However, I also need to wake up earlier, eat a light meal or snack, and warm up properly, which can be challenging when I have a busy schedule or lack motivation.

On the other hand, when I do HIIT workouts in the evening, I feel more relaxed, relieved, and accomplished. I can also eat a substantial meal before the session, which can fuel my performance and recovery. However, I may also feel more fatigued, distracted, and sleepless after the workout, which can affect my sleep quality and mood.

Therefore, I have learned to listen to my body and adjust my HIIT workout timing based on my needs and goals. I have also advised my clients to do the same and find a schedule that works for them and their lifestyle.

The Best Time to Do HIIT Workout for Weight Loss

If your primary goal of doing HIIT workout is to lose weight, you may wonder what is the best time to do it. According to research, the best time to do HIIT workout for weight loss is in the morning, on an empty stomach.

When you do HIIT workout in the morning, your body has been fasting overnight, which can deplete your glycogen stores and increase your fat-burning capacity. Additionally, doing HIIT workout on an empty stomach can improve your insulin sensitivity, which can enhance your body's ability to use and store nutrients efficiently.

However, doing HIIT workout on an empty stomach may not be suitable for everyone, especially if you have low blood sugar, diabetes, or other health conditions. Therefore, it is essential to consult your doctor or a registered dietitian before trying this approach.

The Best Time to Do HIIT Workout for Muscle Building

If your primary goal of doing HIIT workout is to build muscle and strength, you may wonder what is the best time to do it. According to research, the best time to do HIIT workout for muscle building is in the afternoon or evening, after you have eaten a substantial meal.

When you do HIIT workout after a meal, your body has more energy and nutrients to support your performance and recovery. Additionally, doing HIIT workout later in the day can improve your muscle activation and endurance, which can enhance your muscle-building potential.

However, doing HIIT workout after a meal may also cause some discomfort, bloating, or indigestion. Therefore, it is essential to give yourself enough time to digest your food before starting your workout, and to choose foods that are easy to digest and low in fiber and fat.

Other Factors to Consider When Choosing the Best Time to Do HIIT Workout

Aside from your goals and preferences, there are other factors to consider when choosing the best time to do HIIT workout. These factors include:

  • Your sleep quality and quantity
  • Your stress level and mood
  • Your work or school schedule
  • Your access to equipment and facilities
  • Your fitness level and health status

For instance, if you have trouble sleeping or feel stressed, doing HIIT workout in the evening may not be the best option for you, as it can increase your cortisol levels and make it harder for you to relax and rest. Similarly, if you have a tight schedule or limited access to a gym, doing HIIT workout at home or outdoors may be more convenient and practical for you.

How to Optimize Your HIIT Workout Timing

To optimize your HIIT workout timing and get the most out of your training, you can follow these tips:

  • Experiment with different times and see how your body responds
  • Stay hydrated and eat a balanced diet to fuel your workouts
  • Warm up properly and cool down after your sessions
  • Listen to your body and adjust your timing accordingly
  • Get enough rest and recovery to avoid burnout and injury

Question and Answer

Q: Can I do HIIT workout every day?

A: It depends on your fitness level and goals. HIIT workouts are intense and demanding, and they require a lot of energy and effort. Therefore, it is recommended to do them 2-3 times a week, with at least 24 hours of rest between sessions. However, if you are an experienced athlete or fitness enthusiast, you may be able to do HIIT workouts more frequently, as long as you listen to your body and avoid overtraining.

Q: Should I do HIIT workout before or after weightlifting?

A: It depends on your goals and preferences. Some people prefer to do HIIT workouts before weightlifting to warm up and activate their muscles, while others prefer to do it after weightlifting to burn more calories and increase their metabolic rate. However, both approaches can be effective, as long as you give yourself enough time to recover and refuel between sessions.

Q: Can I do HIIT workout at night?

A: It depends on your body's response and your sleep quality. Some people may feel energized and alert after HIIT workouts at night, while others may feel too stimulated and have trouble falling asleep. Therefore, it is recommended to do HIIT workouts at least 2-3 hours before bedtime, and to avoid doing them too close to your sleep time.

Q: Can I do HIIT workout on an empty stomach?

A: It depends on your health status and goals. Doing HIIT workout on an empty stomach can increase your fat-burning capacity and improve your insulin sensitivity. However, it can also cause low blood sugar, dizziness, or fainting, especially if you have diabetes or other health conditions. Therefore, it is essential to consult your doctor or a registered dietitian before trying this approach.

Conclusion of What is the Best Time to Do HIIT Workout

In conclusion, the best time to do HIIT workout depends on your goals, preferences, and lifestyle. Whether you choose to do it in the morning, afternoon, or evening, make sure to listen to your body and adjust your timing based on your needs and feedback. By doing so, you can optimize your training, avoid burnout and injury, and achieve your desired outcomes. Remember to stay hydrated, eat a balanced diet, warm up properly, and cool down after your sessions to maximize your results and enjoy your fitness journey.
