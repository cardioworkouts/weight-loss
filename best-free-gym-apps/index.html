Best Free Gym Apps For Your Workouts A Beginner s Guide

Written by William Oct 27, 2023 · 4 min read

Going to the gym can be intimidating, especially if you're new to working out. It can be hard to know where to start, what exercises to do, and how to stay motivated. Fortunately, there are plenty of free gym apps that can help you reach your fitness goals.

If you're looking for a way to make your workouts more effective and enjoyable, free gym apps are a great place to start. These apps can provide you with workout plans, track your progress, and connect you with other fitness enthusiasts.

So, what are the best free gym apps out there? Here are some of our favorites:

MyFitnessPal

MyFitnessPal is a free app that can help you track your calorie intake, monitor your progress, and connect with other users. It also has a database of over 6 million foods, so you can easily log what you're eating and make sure you're staying on track with your nutrition goals.

Personally, I've been using MyFitnessPal for a while now and it's been a game-changer for me. It's easy to use and has helped me stay accountable with my nutrition.

Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is a free app that offers a variety of workout videos and programs. You can filter workouts by duration, difficulty, and body focus, so it's easy to find something that fits your needs. The app also has a calendar feature that helps you plan your workouts for the week.

I've used Fitness Blender in the past and have found it to be a great resource for at-home workouts. The videos are high-quality and the variety of workouts is impressive.

C25K

If you're looking to start running but don't know where to begin, C25K is a great app to try. It's a free program that gradually builds you up to running a 5K over the course of 8 weeks. The app includes audio cues to tell you when to walk and run, so you don't have to keep track of time yourself.

I used C25K when I first started running and found it to be a great way to ease into the sport. The program is challenging but doable, and it's satisfying to see your progress over the 8 weeks.

FitOn

If you're looking for a free app that offers live and on-demand workouts, FitOn is a great option. The app has a variety of workouts to choose from, including HIIT, yoga, and strength training. You can filter workouts by time, difficulty, and equipment, so it's easy to find something that fits your needs.

I've used FitOn for a few months now and have found it to be a great resource for at-home workouts. The live workouts are a fun way to exercise with others, and the on-demand workouts are convenient for when you can't make it to a live class.

Question and Answer

Q: Are free gym apps effective?

A: Yes, free gym apps can be very effective. They can provide you with workout plans, track your progress, and connect you with other fitness enthusiasts.

Q: Can I use free gym apps instead of going to the gym?

A: It depends on your fitness goals. If you're looking to build muscle or increase your strength, you may need to use gym equipment. However, if you're looking to improve your overall fitness, free gym apps can be a great resource.

Q: Are there any downsides to using free gym apps?

A: One potential downside is that you may not have access to a personal trainer or coach to help you with your form or answer your questions. However, many free gym apps have forums or communities where you can connect with other users for support.

Q: Can I use free gym apps if I'm a beginner?

A: Absolutely! Many free gym apps are designed with beginners in mind and offer workouts that are appropriate for all fitness levels.

Conclusion of Best Free Gym Apps

Free gym apps are a great resource for anyone looking to improve their fitness. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced gym-goer, there's an app out there that can help you reach your goals. So, download a few of our favorites and start working towards a healthier, happier you!
