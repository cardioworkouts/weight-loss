Best Aerobic Exercise Apps You Need To Try

Written by Oliver Aug 04, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of following the same workout routine every day? Are you looking for an effective way to burn calories and improve your cardiovascular health? If so, aerobic exercises are the perfect solution for you. However, finding the right aerobic exercise app can be a daunting task. With so many options out there, it can be tough to determine which one is worth your time and money.

If you are looking for the best aerobic exercise apps that can help you achieve your fitness goals, then you have come to the right place. This blog post will outline the top aerobic exercise apps available in the market today.

Here are some of the best aerobic exercise apps that you must try:

1. Nike Training Club

If you are looking for a free aerobic exercise app that offers a variety of workout routines, then Nike Training Club is perfect for you. This app offers more than 185 free workouts that can help you stay fit and healthy. You can choose from different categories such as strength, endurance, and mobility to customize your workout routine. The app also offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals.

Personally, I have been using Nike Training Club for the past six months, and it has helped me stay motivated and consistent with my workout routine. The app offers a variety of workout routines that keep me engaged and challenged.

2. Aaptiv

Aaptiv is a subscription-based aerobic exercise app that offers audio-based workout routines. The app offers more than 2,500 workouts that are designed by certified personal trainers. The app offers a variety of workout categories such as running, cycling, strength training, and yoga. The app also offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals.

I have been using Aaptiv for the past three months, and I must say that it has been a game-changer for me. The audio-based workout routines keep me engaged and motivated throughout the session.

3. FitOn

FitOn is a free aerobic exercise app that offers a variety of workout routines. The app offers more than 700 workout routines that are designed by celebrity trainers. You can choose from different categories such as dance, yoga, and HIIT to customize your workout routine. The app also offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals.

I have tried FitOn for a few weeks, and I must say that it is an excellent app for people who are looking for a variety of workout options.

Why Are These Apps Effective?

These aerobic exercise apps are effective because they offer a variety of workout routines that can help you stay motivated and consistent with your fitness goals. The apps also offer personalized workout plans that are designed based on your fitness level and goals.

How to Choose the Right Aerobic Exercise App?

Choosing the right aerobic exercise app can be a challenging task. Here are some factors that you must consider before choosing an app:

- Type of workout routines offered by the app

- Personalized workout plans offered by the app

- User interface and ease of use

- Cost of the app

4. 7 Minute Workout

7 Minute Workout is a free aerobic exercise app that offers high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workout routines. The app offers more than 200 workout routines that are designed to be completed in seven minutes. The app also offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals.

I have tried 7 Minute Workout for a few weeks, and I must say that it is an excellent app for people who are looking for a quick and effective workout routine.

Question and Answer:

Q. Are these apps suitable for beginners?

A. Yes, these apps offer personalized workout plans that are designed based on your fitness level and goals. They are suitable for beginners as well as advanced users.

Q. Do these apps offer a free trial?

A. Yes, some of these apps offer a free trial. You can try the app for a specific period before deciding to subscribe.

Q. Can I use these apps without any equipment?

A. Yes, most of these apps offer workout routines that can be completed without any equipment. However, some workout routines may require dumbbells or resistance bands.

Q. Are these apps available for both Android and iOS?

A. Yes, these apps are available for both Android and iOS.

Conclusion of Best Aerobic Exercise Apps:

In conclusion, aerobic exercise apps are an excellent way to stay fit and healthy. The above-mentioned apps offer a variety of workout routines that can help you achieve your fitness goals. Before choosing an app, make sure to consider the factors mentioned above to choose the right app for you.
