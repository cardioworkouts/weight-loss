What Is The Best Hiit Timer App A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 17, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of constantly checking your watch or phone while doing HIIT workouts? Do you struggle to keep track of your intervals and rest periods? If so, then you need the best HIIT timer app to take your workouts to the next level.

HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) is an effective and popular form of exercise that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. However, keeping track of your intervals can be challenging, especially if you're a beginner or new to HIIT workouts.

The best HIIT timer app is a mobile application that helps you time your intervals and rest periods during your HIIT workouts. It can also provide additional features such as customized workouts, progress tracking, and motivational cues.

In this article, we'll explore what is the best HIIT timer app and provide you with a comprehensive guide to help you choose the right app for your needs.

My Personal Experience with HIIT Timer Apps

As a fitness enthusiast and someone who frequently does HIIT workouts, I've tried several HIIT timer apps over the years. Some have been great, while others have been frustrating and hard to use.

One of my favorite HIIT timer apps is the Tabata Timer: Interval Timer app, which is available for both iOS and Android devices. It's easy to use, customizable, and provides clear audio and visual cues for your intervals and rest periods. Plus, it's free to download and use.

Factors to Consider When Choosing the Best HIIT Timer App

When choosing the best HIIT timer app for your needs, there are several factors to consider:

Customization Options

Look for an app that allows you to customize your intervals, rest periods, and workout duration. This can help you tailor your workouts to your fitness level and goals.

User-Friendliness

Choose an app that is easy to use and navigate. You don't want to waste time trying to figure out how to use the app during your workout.

Audio and Visual Cues

Find an app that provides clear audio and visual cues for your intervals and rest periods. This can help you stay on track and motivated during your workout.

Additional Features

Consider whether you want an app that provides additional features such as progress tracking, motivational cues, or the ability to save and share your workouts.

Top HIIT Timer Apps

Here are some of the best HIIT timer apps to consider:

1. Tabata Timer: Interval Timer

This app is simple, customizable, and provides clear audio and visual cues for your intervals and rest periods. It's free to download and use, and available for both iOS and Android devices.

2. Seconds Pro Interval Timer

This app is highly customizable and allows you to create complex interval workouts. It also provides audio and visual cues, progress tracking, and the ability to save and share your workouts. There is a one-time fee of $4.99 to download and use the app, and it's available for both iOS and Android devices.

3. Aaptiv

This app provides customized audio workouts led by certified personal trainers. You can choose from a variety of workouts, including HIIT, and the app will time your intervals and rest periods for you. It also provides progress tracking and motivational cues. There is a monthly fee to use the app, and it's available for both iOS and Android devices.

4. Nike Training Club

This app provides a variety of workouts, including HIIT, led by Nike Master Trainers. The app will time your intervals and rest periods for you, and provide progress tracking and motivational cues. It's free to download and use, and available for both iOS and Android devices.

Question and Answer

Q: What is the best free HIIT timer app?
A: Tabata Timer: Interval Timer is one of the best free HIIT timer apps.

Q: What is the best HIIT timer app for customization options?
A: Seconds Pro Interval Timer is highly customizable and allows you to create complex interval workouts.

Q: What is the best HIIT timer app for audio workouts?
A: Aaptiv provides customized audio workouts led by certified personal trainers.

Q: What is the best free workout app?
A: Nike Training Club provides a variety of free workouts, including HIIT, led by Nike Master Trainers.

Conclusion of What is the Best HIIT Timer App

Choosing the best HIIT timer app can help you take your workouts to the next level. Consider factors such as customization options, user-friendliness, audio and visual cues, and additional features when choosing the right app for your needs. Some of the top HIIT timer apps to consider include Tabata Timer: Interval Timer, Seconds Pro Interval Timer, Aaptiv, and Nike Training Club.
