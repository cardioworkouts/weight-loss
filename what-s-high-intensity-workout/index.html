What Is High Intensity Workout

Written by Robby Aug 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of your regular gym routine and want to spice it up with something new? High Intensity Workout might be the answer you're looking for. It's a type of exercise that involves short bursts of intense activity followed by rest periods. It's not for the faint-hearted but can be a great way to get in shape quickly.

Do you find yourself spending hours at the gym without seeing any significant changes in your body? Or maybe you're looking for a workout that challenges you both physically and mentally? High Intensity Workout might just be what you need to switch things up.

High Intensity Workout, or HIIT, is a type of workout that involves short periods of intense exercise followed by rest periods. The goal is to increase your heart rate and metabolism, which can lead to improved fitness and weight loss. HIIT workouts can be done with a variety of exercises such as running, cycling, or bodyweight exercises.

In summary, High Intensity Workout is a type of exercise that involves short periods of intense activity followed by rest periods. It can help improve your fitness and aid in weight loss.

Benefits of High Intensity Workout

As mentioned earlier, High Intensity Workout can help improve your fitness and aid in weight loss. But that's not all, here are some other benefits of HIIT:

1. Time-efficient: HIIT workouts are short and intense, usually lasting between 10-30 minutes. This makes it a great option for those who have busy schedules but still want to get a good workout in.

2. Burns more calories: Due to the intense nature of the workout, HIIT can help you burn more calories in a shorter amount of time compared to steady-state cardio.

3. Boosts metabolism: HIIT can increase your metabolism for up to 48 hours post-workout, meaning you'll continue to burn calories even after you've finished exercising.

4. Can be done anywhere: You don't need a gym membership to do High Intensity Workout. It can be done at home, in a park, or even in a hotel room if you're traveling.

Personally, I've experienced all of these benefits from doing HIIT workouts. I used to spend hours at the gym doing steady-state cardio but never saw any significant changes in my body. Once I switched to High Intensity Workout, I noticed a difference in my fitness level and saw changes in my body composition within a few weeks.

How to do High Intensity Workout?

Now that you know what High Intensity Workout is and its benefits, you might be wondering how to get started. Here are some tips:

1. Choose your exercise: HIIT can be done with a variety of exercises such as running, cycling, or bodyweight exercises. Choose an exercise that you enjoy and can perform at a high intensity.

2. Warm-up: Before starting your HIIT workout, make sure to warm up properly. This can include jogging in place, jumping jacks or doing some dynamic stretches.

3. Work and rest periods: During your HIIT workout, you'll alternate between short periods of intense exercise and rest periods. A common ratio is 20 seconds of work followed by 10 seconds of rest, repeated for 8 rounds.

4. Cool down: After completing your HIIT workout, make sure to cool down properly. This can include walking or doing some static stretches.

By following these tips, you can get started with High Intensity Workout and experience its benefits.

FAQs

Q: Is High Intensity Workout suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, High Intensity Workout can be suitable for beginners. However, it's important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts. It's also recommended to consult with a trainer or physician before starting any new exercise program.

Q: Can I do High Intensity Workout every day?

A: It's not recommended to do High Intensity Workout every day as your body needs time to recover. It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times a week with rest days in between.

Q: Do I need any equipment to do High Intensity Workout?

A: No, you don't need any equipment to do High Intensity Workout. It can be done with just your bodyweight. However, you can also incorporate equipment such as dumbbells or resistance bands to increase the intensity of your workouts.

Q: How long should a High Intensity Workout session last?

A: A High Intensity Workout session can last between 10-30 minutes depending on your fitness level and the exercises you choose to do.

Conclusion of what's High Intensity Workout

High Intensity Workout is a type of exercise that involves short periods of intense activity followed by rest periods. It can help improve your fitness, aid in weight loss and has many other benefits. By following the tips mentioned above, you can get started with High Intensity Workout and experience its benefits for yourself.
