1. Nike Training Club

I recently started using the Nike Training Club app, and I must say, it's one of the best free strength training apps for iPhone out there. The app offers a range of workouts that can be customized to your fitness level and goals, with options for bodyweight workouts, yoga, and more.

The app also features a range of guided workouts led by professional trainers, with personalized recommendations based on your fitness level and goals. You can also track your progress and earn rewards for completing workouts, which helps to keep you motivated and on track.

2. Fitbod

The Fitbod app is another great option for anyone looking for the best free strength training apps for iPhone. The app offers a range of customized workouts based on your fitness level and goals, with options for weightlifting, bodyweight exercises, and more.

One of the best things about the Fitbod app is that it uses artificial intelligence to create personalized workouts based on your past performance and goals. This means that each workout is tailored to your specific needs, which helps to keep you engaged and motivated.

3. JEFIT

The JEFIT app is a popular choice for anyone looking for the best free strength training apps for iPhone. The app offers a range of workouts and exercises that can be customized to your fitness level and goals, with options for weightlifting, bodyweight exercises, and more.

One of the best things about the JEFIT app is that it offers a range of tracking tools, including a workout log, progress tracker, and more. This helps to keep you motivated and on track, and allows you to see your progress over time.

4. StrongLifts 5x5

The StrongLifts 5x5 app is a great option for anyone looking to build strength and muscle. The app offers a range of workouts that focus on the 5x5 training method, which involves lifting heavy weights for five sets of five reps.

The app also features a range of tracking tools, including a workout log and progress tracker, which helps to keep you motivated and on track. Additionally, the app offers a range of instructional videos and tips to help you perfect your form and technique.

5. 7 Minute Workout

The 7 Minute Workout app is a great option for anyone looking for a quick and efficient workout that can be done anywhere, anytime. The app offers a range of bodyweight exercises that can be completed in just seven minutes, making it perfect for anyone with a busy schedule.
