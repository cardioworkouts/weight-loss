Best Bollywood Workout Songs Get Your Groove On

Written by Petter Jul 05, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for a fun and effective way to get fit? Do you want to add some Bollywood flavor to your workout routine? Look no further than the best Bollywood workout songs!

Working out can be a drag sometimes, but music can make all the difference. A good workout playlist can motivate you, keep you energized, and even distract you from any discomfort you may be feeling. But with so many songs out there, it can be hard to know where to start. That's where the best Bollywood workout songs come in!

The best Bollywood workout songs are a collection of high-energy tracks that feature catchy beats, fun lyrics, and plenty of opportunities to dance. Whether you're a seasoned pro or a beginner, these songs will help you get your heart rate up, burn calories, and have a blast while doing it.

Top 5 Best Bollywood Workout Songs

As a Bollywood lover and fitness enthusiast, I've tried and tested a variety of Bollywood songs for my workout. Here are my top five picks:

1. "Dhating Naach" from the movie Phata Poster Nikhla Hero

This song has a fast-paced beat and catchy lyrics that make you want to move. The chorus is especially great for high-intensity workouts like cardio or HIIT.

2. "Balam Pichkari" from the movie Yeh Jawaani Hai Deewani

A fun and upbeat track, "Balam Pichkari" is perfect for dance workouts. The song's playful vibe will put a smile on your face and keep you energized throughout your workout.

3. "Malhari" from the movie Bajirao Mastani

"Malhari" is a powerful and intense song that's great for strength training or any workout that requires you to push yourself. The song's energetic beats and powerful vocals will motivate you to keep going.

4. "Tattad Tattad" from the movie Ram-Leela

A high-energy track with a strong beat, "Tattad Tattad" is perfect for any high-intensity workout. The song's fast pace and catchy lyrics will keep you moving and motivated.

5. "Chammak Challo" from the movie Ra.One

A classic Bollywood song, "Chammak Challo" is perfect for dance workouts or any workout that requires you to move your body. The song's upbeat tempo and catchy chorus will have you singing along while you sweat.

Why Choose Bollywood Songs for Your Workout?

Bollywood music is known for its high energy, catchy beats, and infectious rhythms. It's the perfect music to get you moving and keep you motivated throughout your workout. Plus, Bollywood songs often feature a mix of Hindi and English lyrics, making them accessible to a wide range of listeners.

Another great thing about Bollywood songs is their versatility. Whether you're looking for a high-intensity workout or a more low-key routine, there's a Bollywood song out there for you. From fast-paced tracks like "Dhating Naach" to more mellow songs like "Tum Hi Ho" from the movie Aashiqui 2, there's something for everyone.

The Benefits of Working Out with Music

Working out with music has been shown to have numerous benefits. Studies have found that listening to music during exercise can:

  • Boost your mood
  • Reduce feelings of fatigue
  • Increase endurance
  • Improve overall performance

How to Create Your Own Bollywood Workout Playlist

If you're not sure where to start with your Bollywood workout playlist, here are a few tips:

  • Choose songs with a fast tempo and strong beat
  • Look for songs with catchy lyrics that you can sing along to
  • Include a mix of high-intensity tracks and more mellow songs for a well-rounded workout
  • Consider adding songs from your favorite Bollywood movies or artists

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How many songs should I include in my Bollywood workout playlist?

A: It depends on the length of your workout, but aim for at least 10-15 songs to keep things interesting.

2. Can I use Bollywood songs for any type of workout?

A: Yes! Bollywood songs are versatile and can be used for a variety of workouts, from high-intensity cardio to yoga and stretching.

3. Do I need to understand Hindi to enjoy Bollywood songs?

A: Not at all! Many Bollywood songs feature a mix of Hindi and English lyrics, making them accessible to a wider audience.

4. Can I find Bollywood workout playlists on streaming platforms like Spotify?

A: Yes! Many users have created their own Bollywood workout playlists on platforms like Spotify and Apple Music. You can also find pre-made playlists with popular Bollywood workout songs.

Conclusion of Best Bollywood Workout Songs

Working out doesn't have to be a chore. With the best Bollywood workout songs, you can have fun, stay motivated, and get fit all at the same time. So put on your dancing shoes, crank up the music, and get ready to get your groove on!
