20 Minute Cardio Workout For Beginners A Step By Step Tutorial

Written by William Aug 14, 2023 Â· 5 min read

Are you looking for a quick and effective way to get in shape? If you're a beginner, cardio workouts can be a great way to start. In just 20 minutes a day, you can burn calories, improve your cardiovascular health, and boost your mood. But where do you begin?

Starting a new workout routine can be daunting, especially if you're not sure where to start. You may be worried about getting injured, feeling self-conscious, or not seeing results fast enough. These are all common concerns, but with the right guidance, you can overcome them.

In this tutorial, we'll walk you through a 20 minute cardio workout for beginners. We'll cover the benefits of cardio, how to get started, and what to expect from your first few workouts. By the end of this tutorial, you'll have the knowledge and confidence you need to start your cardio journey.

So, let's get started with the basics.

The Target of 20 Minute Cardio Workout for Beginners

The target of a 20 minute cardio workout for beginners is to get your heart rate up and your body moving. The goal is to perform exercises that get your blood pumping and your breathing rate up. This will help you burn calories, improve your cardiovascular health, and build endurance.

One of the best things about cardio workouts is that you can do them anywhere, with little to no equipment. This makes them a great option for beginners who may not have access to a gym or expensive equipment.

Now, let's dive into some specific exercises you can do in your 20 minute cardio workout.

Exercises for 20 Minute Cardio Workout for Beginners

Before we get started with the exercises, it's important to warm up. Spend 5 minutes doing some light cardio, such as marching in place or walking around your room. This will help prepare your body for the workout.

Once you're warmed up, you can start with the following exercises:

1. Jumping Jacks

Stand with your feet together and your arms at your sides. Jump your feet out to the sides while raising your arms above your head. Jump your feet back together while lowering your arms to your sides. Repeat for 30 seconds.

2. High Knees

Stand with your feet hip-width apart. Lift your right knee up to your chest, then quickly switch to your left knee. Continue alternating knees as quickly as you can for 30 seconds.

3. Butt Kicks

Stand with your feet hip-width apart. Lift your right heel towards your butt, then quickly switch to your left heel. Continue alternating heels as quickly as you can for 30 seconds.

4. Mountain Climbers

Start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your feet together. Bring your right knee towards your chest, then quickly switch to your left knee. Continue alternating knees as quickly as you can for 30 seconds.

5. Squat Jumps

Stand with your feet hip-width apart. Lower your body into a squat position, then jump up as high as you can. Land softly back into the squat position and repeat for 30 seconds.

Once you've completed all five exercises, rest for 30 seconds, then repeat the circuit two more times. This will give you a total of 20 minutes of cardio.

Tips for 20 Minute Cardio Workout for Beginners

Here are some tips to keep in mind as you start your cardio journey:

1. Start slow and gradually increase your intensity.

Don't push yourself too hard too fast. It's important to start slow and gradually increase your intensity over time. This will help prevent injury and burnout.

2. Listen to your body.

If you feel pain or discomfort during a workout, stop immediately. It's important to listen to your body and not push yourself beyond your limits.

3. Stay hydrated.

Drink plenty of water before, during, and after your workout. This will help keep your body hydrated and prevent cramps.

4. Have fun!

Cardio workouts can be a lot of fun, especially if you find exercises you enjoy. Experiment with different exercises and find what works best for you.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do cardio workouts?

A: It's recommended to do at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per week. This can be broken up into 20-30 minute sessions, 5 days a week.

Q: Do I need any equipment for a 20 minute cardio workout?

A: No, you don't need any equipment for a 20 minute cardio workout. However, you may want to invest in a good pair of athletic shoes for support.

Q: Can I do cardio workouts if I have joint pain?

A: If you have joint pain, it's important to talk to your doctor before starting any new workout routine. They may recommend low-impact exercises, such as swimming or biking.

Q: How long will it take to see results from cardio workouts?

A: Results will vary based on your fitness level, diet, and other factors. However, you can expect to see improvements in your cardiovascular health, endurance, and mood within a few weeks of starting a cardio routine.

Conclusion of 20 Minute Cardio Workout for Beginners

A 20 minute cardio workout for beginners is a great way to get started on your fitness journey. By following the exercises and tips in this tutorial, you can improve your cardiovascular health, burn calories, and boost your mood in just 20 minutes a day. Remember to start slow, listen to your body, and have fun!
