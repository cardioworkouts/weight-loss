In house training is a type of training program that is conducted within the organization's premises. It is specifically designed to meet the needs of the company and its employees. This type of training is becoming increasingly popular among companies of all sizes and industries because it is cost-effective and convenient.

So, what is the target of apa itu in house training? The target is to provide employees with the necessary skills and knowledge to perform their job functions effectively. This can include anything from technical skills to soft skills like communication and teamwork.

The benefits of in house training are numerous. For one, it allows companies to tailor the training to their specific needs. This means that employees can learn skills and knowledge that are directly applicable to their job roles. Additionally, in house training is much more cost-effective than sending employees to external training programs. Finally, in house training is much more convenient for employees since they do not have to travel offsite, which can be time-consuming and expensive.

There are several types of in house training programs that companies can implement.
