60 Tips Short Intense Cardio Workouts For Beginner

Written by Frank Jun 27, 2023 · 4 min read

In this post, we will be discussing the benefits of short intense cardio workouts and how they can help you achieve your fitness goals without taking up too much of your time.

Are you someone who struggles to find time to work out or simply hates spending hours at the gym? Do you want to improve your cardiovascular health but find traditional cardio workouts tedious and boring? If so, short intense cardio workouts might just be the solution you have been looking for.

What are short intense cardio workouts?

Short intense cardio workouts are high-intensity interval training (HIIT) workouts that consist of short bursts of intense exercise followed by brief periods of rest. These workouts typically last anywhere from 10 to 30 minutes and can be done with little to no equipment.

The goal of these workouts is to get your heart rate up quickly and keep it elevated throughout the workout, resulting in a more effective cardio workout in less time.

My personal experience with short intense cardio workouts

As someone who hates spending hours at the gym, I have found short intense cardio workouts to be a game-changer in my fitness routine. Not only do they help me save time, but they also make my cardio workouts more enjoyable.

One of my go-to short intense cardio workouts is a 20-minute HIIT workout that consists of 30 seconds of jumping jacks, 30 seconds of mountain climbers, 30 seconds of burpees, and 30 seconds of rest. I repeat this circuit for a total of 20 minutes, and by the end, I am drenched in sweat and feel like I have gotten a great workout.

The benefits of short intense cardio workouts

Short intense cardio workouts have several benefits, including:

1. Increased fat burning

Short intense cardio workouts have been shown to increase fat burning and improve metabolic health.

2. Improved cardiovascular health

Short intense cardio workouts can help improve cardiovascular health by increasing your heart rate and improving blood flow.

3. Time-efficient

Short intense cardio workouts are a time-efficient way to improve your fitness and achieve your fitness goals without spending hours at the gym.

4. Versatile

Short intense cardio workouts can be done anywhere with little to no equipment, making them a versatile option for anyone looking to improve their fitness.

How to get started with short intense cardio workouts

If you are new to short intense cardio workouts, it is essential to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts over time.

Some tips for getting started include:

1. Choose a workout that suits your fitness level

Start with a workout that is challenging but doable for your fitness level. As you get stronger, you can increase the intensity and duration of your workouts.

2. Warm up before your workout

It is important to warm up before your workout to prevent injury and prepare your body for the intense exercise to come. Some good warm-up exercises include jumping jacks, high knees, and lunges.

3. Incorporate strength training

Strength training can help improve your overall fitness and make your short intense cardio workouts more effective. Consider incorporating bodyweight exercises like push-ups, squats, and lunges into your workouts.

Conclusion of short intense cardio workouts

Short intense cardio workouts are a time-efficient and effective way to improve your fitness and achieve your fitness goals without spending hours at the gym. With a little bit of planning and dedication, you can incorporate these workouts into your fitness routine and start seeing the benefits in no time.

Question and Answer

Q: How often should I do short intense cardio workouts?

A: It is recommended to do short intense cardio workouts 2-3 times a week, with rest days in between.

Q: Can short intense cardio workouts help me lose weight?

A: Yes, short intense cardio workouts can help you lose weight by increasing fat burning and improving metabolic health.

Q: Do I need equipment to do short intense cardio workouts?

A: No, short intense cardio workouts can be done with little to no equipment. Bodyweight exercises like jumping jacks, burpees, and mountain climbers are effective options.

Q: Are short intense cardio workouts suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, short intense cardio workouts can be modified to suit any fitness level. It is important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts over time.
