Get Fit With The Most Popular Hiit Workout On Youtube

Written by Frank Sep 30, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old workout routine? Do you want to get fit but don't have a lot of time? High-intensity interval training (HIIT) might be the solution you're looking for! HIIT is a type of workout that alternates between short bursts of intense exercise and periods of rest or low-intensity exercise. It's a great way to burn fat, build muscle, and improve cardiovascular fitness.

However, with so many HIIT workouts available on YouTube, it can be overwhelming to find the right one for you. You want a workout that is challenging but not too difficult, and that targets your specific goals.

Fortunately, we've done the research for you and found the most popular HIIT workout on YouTube. This workout has over 5 million views and is loved by fitness enthusiasts all over the world. It's called the "7 Minute HIIT Workout" by Fitness Blender.

In this article, we'll take a closer look at the 7 Minute HIIT Workout and why it's so popular. We'll also explore the benefits of HIIT and give you some tips on how to get the most out of your workout.

The 7 Minute HIIT Workout

The 7 Minute HIIT Workout is a series of 12 exercises that you perform for 30 seconds each, with a 10-second break in between. The exercises are designed to target all major muscle groups and get your heart rate up. They include jumping jacks, squats, lunges, push-ups, and more.

One of the reasons this workout is so popular is that it only takes 7 minutes to complete. This makes it perfect for people who are short on time or who don't want to spend hours at the gym. However, don't let the short duration fool you – this workout is intense and will leave you feeling the burn.

Another reason people love the 7 Minute HIIT Workout is that it requires no equipment. You can do it anywhere, anytime, without any fancy gym equipment. All you need is a bit of space and some motivation.

Personally, I have been doing the 7 Minute HIIT Workout for a few weeks now, and I can already see improvements in my fitness level. I feel stronger, more energized, and more confident. Plus, it's a great way to start my day and get my blood pumping!

The Benefits of HIIT

HIIT has many benefits, including:

  • Burning fat and building muscle
  • Improving cardiovascular fitness
  • Boosting metabolism
  • Increasing energy levels
  • Reducing stress

HIIT is also a great way to break through a fitness plateau. If you've been doing the same workout routine for months and aren't seeing any results, adding HIIT to your routine can help you push past your limits and achieve your fitness goals.

How to Get the Most Out of Your HIIT Workout

If you want to get the most out of your HIIT workout, here are some tips:

  • Warm up before you start. This will help prevent injury and prepare your body for the workout.
  • Focus on proper form. This will help you avoid injury and ensure that you're targeting the right muscles.
  • Push yourself, but listen to your body. It's important to challenge yourself during a HIIT workout, but it's also important to know your limits. If something feels too difficult, take a break or modify the exercise.
  • Cool down after the workout. This will help your body recover and prevent injury.

FAQs About HIIT

Q: Is HIIT suitable for beginners?

A: Yes, HIIT can be modified to suit any fitness level. However, if you're new to exercise, it's important to start slowly and gradually increase the intensity.

Q: How often should I do HIIT?

A: It's recommended to do HIIT workouts 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between. This will give your body time to recover and prevent injury.

Q: Can I do HIIT every day?

A: It's not recommended to do HIIT every day, as it can put a lot of stress on your body. It's important to give your body time to recover between workouts.

Q: What should I eat before and after a HIIT workout?

A: It's important to fuel your body with the right nutrients before and after a workout. Before a workout, eat a small meal or snack that is high in carbohydrates and protein. After a workout, eat a meal that is high in protein and carbohydrates to help your body recover.

Conclusion

The 7 Minute HIIT Workout by Fitness Blender is the most popular HIIT workout on YouTube for a reason. It's short, effective, and requires no equipment. Plus, it's a great way to get fit and improve your overall health. By following the tips in this article, you can get the most out of your HIIT workout and achieve your fitness goals.
