Weekly Bodybuilding Workout Plan A Beginner s Guide

Written by Daniel Jun 21, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you ready to take your bodybuilding journey to the next level? If so, then you've come to the right place. In this article, we'll be discussing everything you need to know about creating a weekly bodybuilding workout plan. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced gym-goer, this guide will help you achieve your fitness goals.

Starting a new workout routine can be intimidating, especially if you're not sure where to begin. You may be worried about not seeing results, or you may not know how to properly structure your workouts. These are common concerns that many people face when starting a new fitness journey.

The primary target of a weekly bodybuilding workout plan is to build muscle, increase strength, and improve overall fitness. A well-structured workout plan can help you achieve these goals by targeting specific muscle groups and incorporating a variety of exercises.

In summary, a weekly bodybuilding workout plan is a structured routine that targets specific muscle groups and incorporates a variety of exercises. Its primary goal is to build muscle, increase strength, and improve overall fitness.

What should be included in a weekly bodybuilding workout plan?

When creating a weekly bodybuilding workout plan, there are several key components that you should consider including:

1. Resistance Training: This includes exercises that use weights, resistance bands, or bodyweight to build muscle and increase strength.

2. Cardiovascular Exercise: This includes exercises that increase your heart rate, such as running, cycling, or swimming.

3. Rest Days: It's important to include rest days in your weekly workout plan to allow your muscles time to recover and grow.

4. Proper Nutrition: Building muscle requires proper nutrition, including a balance of protein, carbohydrates, and healthy fats.

Personally, I follow a weekly bodybuilding workout plan that includes four resistance training days, two cardiovascular exercise days, and one rest day. I also make sure to eat a balanced diet that includes plenty of protein and complex carbohydrates.

How do I structure my resistance training workouts?

When structuring your resistance training workouts, it's important to target specific muscle groups and incorporate a variety of exercises. Here's an example of how you can structure your resistance training workouts:

Day 1: Chest and Triceps

  • Bench Press
  • Incline Bench Press
  • Dumbbell Flyes
  • Tricep Pushdowns
  • Skull Crushers

Day 2: Back and Biceps

  • Deadlifts
  • Lat Pulldowns
  • Seated Rows
  • Bicep Curls
  • Hammer Curls

Day 3: Legs

  • Squats
  • Leg Press
  • Lunges
  • Calf Raises

Day 4: Shoulders and Abs

  • Shoulder Press
  • Side Lateral Raises
  • Rear Delt Flyes
  • Planks
  • Crunches

It's important to note that you should always warm up before starting your workouts and cool down afterwards. You should also adjust the weight and reps based on your individual fitness level and goals.

How do I incorporate cardiovascular exercise?

Incorporating cardiovascular exercise into your weekly bodybuilding workout plan can help increase your endurance and improve your overall fitness. Here are a few examples of how you can add cardio to your routine:

  • Running or jogging
  • Cycling
  • Swimming
  • Jumping rope
  • Rowing

You can either dedicate specific days for cardiovascular exercise or incorporate it into your resistance training days as a warm-up or cool-down. Aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio per session.

How do I track my progress?

Tracking your progress is an important part of any fitness journey. Here are a few ways you can track your progress:

  • Take progress photos to visually track your muscle growth
  • Keep a workout journal to track your weight, reps, and sets
  • Use a fitness app or wearable device to track your workouts and progress
  • Get regular body composition tests to track changes in your body fat and muscle mass

Conclusion of weekly bodybuilding workout plan

A weekly bodybuilding workout plan can help you achieve your fitness goals by targeting specific muscle groups and incorporating a variety of exercises. Remember to include resistance training, cardiovascular exercise, rest days, and proper nutrition in your plan. By following a structured routine and tracking your progress, you can build muscle, increase strength, and improve your overall fitness.
