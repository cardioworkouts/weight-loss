Get Fit And Groove With The Best Dance Cardio Apps

Written by Petter Nov 02, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you tired of the same old cardio routine that makes you dread your workout? Do you want to add some fun and excitement to your fitness regimen? One great solution is dance cardio, which not only gets your heart rate up but also allows you to bust a move and express yourself. However, not everyone has access to a dance class or wants to pay for one. That's where dance cardio apps come in handy, offering a convenient and affordable way to sweat it out and shake it off.

If you're on the hunt for the best dance cardio apps, you're probably looking for a few things. First, you want a variety of dance styles and music genres to keep things interesting and challenging. Second, you want clear instructions and demonstrations to follow along with, especially if you're new to dance or fitness. Third, you want a customizable experience that fits your schedule, skill level, and goals. Finally, you want a user-friendly interface and affordable pricing, if not free.

So, without further ado, let's dive into some of the best dance cardio apps that meet these criteria and more.

1. Dance Fitness with Jessica

I first discovered Dance Fitness with Jessica on YouTube, where she has a huge following for her high-energy and easy-to-follow dance workouts. She offers a range of routines, from hip hop and Latin to pop and country, and incorporates cardio, toning, and even yoga moves. Her app, available on iOS and Android, features exclusive content, personalized meal plans, and a community of dancers to connect with. The app is free to download but requires a subscription to access all the videos.

2. Aaptiv

If you prefer a more audio-based workout, Aaptiv might be your jam. This app offers a variety of guided workouts, including dance cardio, that are led by certified trainers and set to upbeat music. You can choose your preferred duration, difficulty, and music genre, and the app will create a personalized playlist for you. The app also tracks your progress and offers challenges to keep you motivated. Aaptiv is available on iOS and Android and offers a 7-day free trial before requiring a subscription.

3. Zumba Fitness

Zumba is one of the most popular dance fitness programs out there, and their app brings the party to your phone. You can access thousands of Zumba routines, from classic to new, and learn from world-renowned instructors. The app also offers a progress tracker, customized playlists, and virtual classes with other dancers. You can download the app for free on iOS and Android but need a subscription to unlock all the features.

4. FitOn

FitOn is a comprehensive fitness app that includes dance cardio as one of its many options. You can choose from a variety of dance styles, such as Bollywood, hip hop, and jazz, and follow along with top trainers. The app also offers personalized meal plans, mindfulness exercises, and challenges to help you reach your fitness goals. You can download the app for free on iOS and Android but need a subscription to access all the content.

Question and Answer

Q: Can dance cardio help with weight loss?

A: Yes, dance cardio can be an effective way to burn calories and shed pounds, especially when combined with a healthy diet and other forms of exercise.

Q: Do I need any equipment for dance cardio?

A: Most dance cardio routines don't require any equipment, although you may want to wear supportive shoes and comfortable clothes that allow for movement.

Q: Can beginners do dance cardio?

A: Absolutely! Many dance cardio apps and videos cater to beginners and offer modifications for different skill levels. Just start with a lower intensity and gradually increase as you feel comfortable.

Q: How often should I do dance cardio?

A: It depends on your fitness level and goals, but aim for at least 30 minutes of moderate-intensity cardio, such as dance, most days of the week. You can also mix and match with other forms of exercise, such as strength training and stretching.

Conclusion of Best Dance Cardio Apps

Dance cardio apps are a fun and effective way to get your heart pumping and your body moving. Whether you prefer hip hop, Latin, or Bollywood, there's a dance style and app out there for you. Just remember to choose an app that fits your needs and preferences, and don't be afraid to try new things and challenge yourself.
