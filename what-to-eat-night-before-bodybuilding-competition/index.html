What To Eat Night Before Bodybuilding Competition

Written by Christine Jun 29, 2023 Â· 3 min read

Are you a bodybuilder preparing for a competition and wondering what to eat the night before? Your diet plays a crucial role in your performance on competition day, and the night before is no exception. In this tutorial, we will discuss what foods to eat and avoid for optimal results.

Why is the Night Before Competition Important?

The night before competition is crucial as it provides your body with the necessary nutrients required for optimal performance on the big day. Eating the right foods can help you feel energized, reduce inflammation, and enhance muscle definition. On the other hand, consuming the wrong foods can lead to bloating, gas, indigestion, and other digestive issues.

What to Eat Night Before Bodybuilding Competition

Here are some food options to consider for your pre-competition meal:

Protein

Protein is essential for muscle growth and recovery. Opt for lean protein sources such as chicken, turkey, fish, and egg whites. Protein powders are also a convenient option, but be sure to choose a high-quality brand that is low in sugar and additives.

Complex Carbohydrates

Complex carbohydrates provide your body with sustained energy throughout the day. Good sources include brown rice, quinoa, sweet potato, and oatmeal. Avoid refined carbohydrates such as white bread, pasta, and sugary snacks.

Personal Experience

As a bodybuilder, I always make sure to consume a balanced meal of protein and complex carbohydrates the night before a competition. My go-to meal is grilled chicken with brown rice and steamed vegetables. I avoid high-fat foods, dairy, and alcohol, as they tend to cause bloating and discomfort.

What to Avoid Night Before Bodybuilding Competition

Avoid the following foods the night before competition:

Fatty Foods

Fatty foods take longer to digest and can cause bloating and discomfort. Avoid foods such as pizza, fried foods, and fatty cuts of meat.

Dairy Products

Dairy products can cause digestive issues such as gas and bloating. Avoid milk, cheese, and ice cream.

How to Prepare the Night Before Bodybuilding Competition

Preparing your meals the night before can save you time and ensure that you have the necessary foods on hand. Cook your meals in advance and portion them out in Tupperware containers. This will prevent you from reaching for unhealthy snacks or fast food on competition day.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I eat fruits the night before a competition?

A: While fruits are a healthy food choice, they can cause digestive issues for some individuals. If you choose to eat fruits, stick to low-fiber options such as bananas or apples.

Q: Is it okay to drink coffee the night before competition?

A: It is best to avoid coffee the night before competition as it can cause dehydration and disrupt your sleep.

Q: Can I drink alcohol the night before competition?

A: Alcohol causes dehydration and can lead to bloating and water retention. It is best to avoid alcohol the night before competition.

Q: Should I eat a large meal or small meals the night before competition?

A: It is best to consume small, frequent meals the night before competition to prevent bloating and promote digestion.

Conclusion of What to Eat Night Before Bodybuilding Competition

Preparing for a bodybuilding competition requires discipline and dedication, especially when it comes to your diet. Consuming the right foods the night before can help you feel energized, reduce inflammation, and enhance muscle definition. Avoiding the wrong foods can prevent digestive issues and discomfort. Follow these tips to prepare for your next competition and achieve optimal results.
