When Is The Best Time To Do Hiit Cardio

Written by Christine Nov 10, 2023 · 5 min read

Are you trying to get the most out of your HIIT (High-Intensity Interval Training) cardio workouts but not sure when the best time to do them is? You're not alone! Many people struggle with this question, and the answer isn't as clear-cut as you might think.

The best time to do HIIT cardio depends on your goals, schedule, and personal preferences. Some people prefer to do their cardio first thing in the morning, while others like to do it later in the day. However, there are some general guidelines that can help you determine the optimal time for your HIIT cardio sessions.

First and foremost, the best time to do HIIT cardio is when you can commit to it consistently. Consistency is key when it comes to seeing results from any fitness routine, including HIIT cardio. If you're not a morning person, forcing yourself to do cardio early in the morning may not be sustainable for the long term. Similarly, if you're too tired at the end of the day, it may not be the best time for you to do HIIT.

In general, many fitness experts recommend doing HIIT cardio in the morning, on an empty stomach. This is because your body has been in a fasted state overnight, which means it has depleted its glycogen stores and is more likely to burn fat for fuel during your workout. However, if you prefer to eat something before your workout, make sure it's a light meal that won't weigh you down or cause digestive issues during your HIIT session.

The Benefits of Doing HIIT Cardio in the Morning

Personally, I've found that doing my HIIT cardio in the morning works best for me. It helps me start my day off on the right foot, boosts my energy levels, and helps me stay focused throughout the day. Plus, I don't have to worry about fitting it in later in the day when unexpected events may arise.

Doing HIIT cardio in the morning also helps me stay consistent with my workouts. I'm less likely to skip a workout if I do it first thing in the morning before the day gets away from me. Plus, it sets a positive tone for my day and helps me make healthier choices throughout the day, such as eating nutritious foods and staying hydrated.

The Benefits of Doing HIIT Cardio in the Evening

While many fitness experts recommend doing HIIT cardio in the morning, others argue that doing it in the evening can be just as effective. For example, if you're someone who struggles to get going in the morning, doing HIIT cardio later in the day when you're more awake and alert may be more beneficial for you.

Additionally, doing HIIT cardio in the evening can help you unwind after a long day at work, release stress, and improve your sleep quality. Just make sure you don't do your workout too close to bedtime, as it can make it harder to fall asleep.

What About Doing HIIT Cardio After Weight Training?

If you're someone who likes to lift weights and do cardio, you may be wondering if there's an optimal time to do each. While there's no one-size-fits-all answer, many fitness experts recommend doing weight training before cardio.

This is because weight training can deplete your glycogen stores, which means your body will be more likely to burn fat for fuel during your HIIT cardio session. Additionally, doing HIIT cardio after weight training can help you burn more calories overall and increase your metabolic rate, which means you'll continue to burn calories even after your workout is over.

Tips for Getting the Most Out of Your HIIT Cardio Workouts

No matter when you decide to do your HIIT cardio, there are some tips that can help you get the most out of your workouts:

  1. Make sure you warm up before your workout to prevent injury and improve performance.
  2. Stay hydrated before, during, and after your workout.
  3. Focus on your form and technique to maximize your results and avoid injury.
  4. Gradually increase the intensity and duration of your workouts over time to avoid plateauing.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it better to do HIIT cardio before or after eating?

A: It's generally recommended to do HIIT cardio on an empty stomach, but if you prefer to eat something before your workout, make sure it's a light meal that won't weigh you down or cause digestive issues during your HIIT session.

Q: Can HIIT cardio be done every day?

A: It's generally not recommended to do HIIT cardio every day, as it can be hard on your body and increase your risk of injury. Aim to do it 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between to allow your body to recover.

Q: How long should a HIIT cardio workout be?

A: A typical HIIT workout lasts anywhere from 10-30 minutes, depending on your fitness level and the intensity of your workout. It's important to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard, especially if you're new to HIIT.

Q: Can HIIT cardio be done at home?

A: Absolutely! HIIT cardio can be done anywhere, including at home with no equipment necessary. There are many online resources and videos available that can guide you through an effective HIIT workout at home.

Conclusion of When is the Best Time to Do HIIT Cardio

Ultimately, the best time to do HIIT cardio is when you can commit to it consistently and when it works best for your schedule and personal preferences. Whether you prefer to do it in the morning, evening, or after weight training, the most important thing is to stay consistent and listen to your body. By following these tips and guidelines, you can get the most out of your HIIT cardio workouts and reach your fitness goals.
