Foods that are high in fat, such as fast food, fried foods and processed snacks, should be avoided after running. These foods can be difficult to digest and can lead to stomach discomfort and digestive issues.

Sugary Foods

Sugary foods, such as candy, soda and baked goods, should also be avoided after running. These foods can cause a spike in blood sugar levels, which can lead to a crash later on. They can also contribute to weight gain and other health issues.
