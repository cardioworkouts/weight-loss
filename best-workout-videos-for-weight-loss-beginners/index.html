Best Workout Videos For Weight Loss Beginners

Written by Oliver Jun 16, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you a beginner looking for the best workout videos to lose weight? Do you want to get fit but don't know where to start? Look no further! In this article, we will discuss the best workout videos for weight loss beginners that will help you achieve your fitness goals.

Pain Points

Starting a weight loss journey can be intimidating, especially if you are a beginner. You might not know where to start or what exercises to do. You might not have the motivation to go to the gym or hire a personal trainer. These are common pain points that most beginners face when trying to lose weight.

Best Workout Videos for Weight Loss Beginners

The following are the best workout videos for weight loss beginners:

1. Fitness Blender

Fitness Blender is a popular YouTube channel that offers a wide range of workout videos for beginners. They have videos that focus on cardio, strength training, and HIIT workouts. Their workouts are easy to follow and require little to no equipment.

2. Yoga with Adriene

If you prefer a more low-impact workout, Yoga with Adriene is a great option. Her YouTube channel offers a variety of yoga videos for all levels, including beginners. Yoga is great for weight loss, as it can help reduce stress and improve flexibility.

3. Jillian Michaels

Jillian Michaels is a well-known fitness trainer who has several workout videos for beginners. Her videos focus on strength training and cardio, and are designed to help you lose weight and tone your body.

4. POPSUGAR Fitness

POPSUGAR Fitness is another popular YouTube channel that offers a variety of workout videos for beginners. They have videos that focus on dance workouts, cardio, and strength training. Their workouts are fun and easy to follow, making them perfect for beginners.

My Personal Experience

When I first started my weight loss journey, I was intimidated by the thought of going to a gym or hiring a personal trainer. That's when I discovered Fitness Blender on YouTube. Their workouts were easy to follow and required little to no equipment. I was able to lose 10 pounds in just a few weeks by following their workouts consistently.

Tips for Beginners

Here are some tips for beginners who are just starting their weight loss journey:

1. Start Slow

Don't try to do too much too soon. Start with simple exercises and gradually work your way up to more intense workouts.

2. Find a Workout Buddy

Working out with a friend can help keep you motivated and accountable. You can also encourage each other and push each other to do your best.

3. Set Realistic Goals

Set achievable goals for yourself, such as losing a certain amount of weight or being able to run a certain distance. This will help keep you motivated and focused.

4. Don't Give Up

Remember that weight loss is a journey, and it takes time and effort. Don't give up if you don't see results right away. Keep pushing yourself and stay consistent with your workouts and diet.

Question and Answer

1. Can I lose weight by just doing yoga?

Yes, yoga can help you lose weight, especially if you practice regularly and follow a healthy diet.

2. How often should I do strength training?

You should aim to do strength training at least two to three times a week for best results.

3. What should I eat before a workout?

You should eat a light meal or snack that contains carbohydrates and protein, such as a banana with peanut butter or a small bowl of oatmeal with fruit.

4. How can I stay motivated to workout?

Try to find a workout buddy, set achievable goals, and reward yourself after each workout. You can also try listening to music or watching motivational videos to stay motivated.

Conclusion

If you are a beginner looking to lose weight, the best workout videos for weight loss beginners are Fitness Blender, Yoga with Adriene, Jillian Michaels, and POPSUGAR Fitness. Remember to start slow, find a workout buddy, set realistic goals, and stay consistent with your workouts and diet. With hard work and dedication, you can achieve your fitness goals and live a healthier, happier life.
