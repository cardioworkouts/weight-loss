The Best Free Workout Routine Apps You Need To Try Today

Written by Robby Aug 25, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you struggling to stay motivated and consistent with your workout routine? Do you find it hard to keep track of your progress and set achievable goals? If so, you're not alone. Many people face these challenges when it comes to working out. Fortunately, there's a solution - free workout routine apps!

With the rise of technology, there's an app for everything, and working out is no exception. There are countless free workout routine apps available on both iOS and Android, designed to help you stay on track with your fitness goals. These apps offer everything from personalized workout plans to tracking your progress and providing motivation and accountability.

If you're looking for the best free workout routine apps, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll share some of the top apps you need to try today, along with their unique features and benefits.

1. Nike Training Club

As one of the most popular workout apps on the market, Nike Training Club offers a variety of free workouts for all fitness levels. The app features over 185+ workouts led by Nike Master Trainers, along with personalized training plans tailored to your goals and fitness level. Nike Training Club also allows you to track your progress and set reminders to help you stay consistent.

Personally, I've been using Nike Training Club for a few months now, and I love how easy it is to use. The app offers a variety of workouts, from yoga to strength training, and the videos are easy to follow along with. I also appreciate the personalized training plans, which help me stay on track with my fitness goals.

2. FitOn

If you're looking for a workout app that offers a community feel, FitOn is the app for you. The app features live and on-demand workouts led by top trainers, along with a social community where you can connect with other users and share your progress. FitOn also offers personalized workout plans and challenges to help you stay motivated.

Personally, I've found FitOn to be a great way to stay motivated and connected with others. The live workouts are a fun way to switch up my routine, and I love being able to connect with other users and share my progress.

3. 7 Minute Workout

Short on time? No problem. With the 7 Minute Workout app, you can get a full-body workout in just seven minutes. The app features 12 exercises that can be done anywhere, without any equipment. The app also allows you to customize your workout plan and set reminders to help you stay consistent.

I've used the 7 Minute Workout app on days when I'm short on time or traveling, and it's a great way to squeeze in a quick workout. The exercises are easy to follow, and the app allows you to customize your workout plan to fit your needs.

4. Daily Burn

If you're looking for a more personalized approach to your workout routine, Daily Burn is the app for you. The app offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness level and goals, along with live and on-demand workouts led by certified trainers. Daily Burn also offers nutrition guidance and support to help you achieve your goals.

Personally, I've found Daily Burn to be a great way to stay on track with my fitness goals. The personalized workout plans have helped me stay consistent, and I appreciate the nutrition guidance and support offered by the app.

Question and Answer

Q: Are these apps really free?

A: Yes, all of the apps mentioned in this article are free to download and use. Some may offer premium features for a fee, but the basic features are free.

Q: Do I need any equipment to use these apps?

A: It depends on the app. Some, like the 7 Minute Workout, require no equipment, while others may require weights or other equipment. Be sure to read the app description before downloading to see what equipment is needed.

Q: Do these apps offer beginner-friendly workouts?

A: Yes, all of the apps mentioned in this article offer workouts for all fitness levels, including beginners.

Q: Can I use these apps if I have a specific fitness goal?

A: Yes, many of these apps offer personalized workout plans tailored to your goals, whether it's weight loss, strength training, or improving overall fitness.

Conclusion of Best Free Workout Routine Apps

Working out doesn't have to be a chore, and with the help of these free workout routine apps, it can be a fun and rewarding experience. Whether you're short on time, looking for a community feel, or need a personalized approach, there's an app for you. So why not give one (or all) of these apps a try and see how they can take your fitness routine to the next level?
