What Are The Three Types Of Anaerobic Exercises

Written by Bardi Jun 30, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you looking for ways to build muscle, increase strength, and improve overall fitness? If so, then anaerobic exercises may be just what you need. Anaerobic exercises are a type of physical activity that focus on short bursts of intense activity followed by periods of rest. In this article, we will explore what are the three types of anaerobic exercises and how they can benefit your body.

If you are someone who wants to get in shape, lose weight, or increase muscle mass, then you may have heard about anaerobic exercises. However, you may not be aware of the different types of anaerobic exercises and how they can benefit your body. This lack of knowledge can make it difficult to choose the right exercises for your fitness goals.

What Are the Three Types of Anaerobic Exercises?

The three types of anaerobic exercises are:

  • High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)
  • Resistance Training
  • Plyometric Training

Each of these types of anaerobic exercises has its unique benefits and can help you achieve different fitness goals. Let's take a closer look at each type of exercise.

High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT)

HIIT is a type of anaerobic exercise that involves short bursts of high-intensity activity followed by periods of rest. This type of exercise is excellent for improving cardiovascular health, burning calories, and building endurance. HIIT can also help you lose weight, reduce body fat, and increase muscle mass.

Personally, I love doing HIIT workouts because they are fast-paced, challenging, and effective. I usually do a 30-minute HIIT workout, which involves a series of exercises like burpees, squats, push-ups, and mountain climbers. I perform each exercise for 30 seconds and rest for 10 seconds between each one. This workout leaves me feeling exhausted but accomplished.

Resistance Training

Resistance training is a type of anaerobic exercise that involves using weights or resistance bands to build strength and muscle. This type of exercise is excellent for increasing bone density, improving posture, and burning calories. Resistance training can also help you lose weight, reduce body fat, and increase muscle mass.

I have been doing resistance training for years, and it has helped me build muscle, increase strength, and improve my overall fitness. My favorite resistance exercises are squats, deadlifts, and bench press. I usually do three sets of each exercise, with 8-12 reps per set.

Plyometric Training

Plyometric training is a type of anaerobic exercise that involves explosive movements like jumping, hopping, and bounding. This type of exercise is excellent for increasing power, speed, and agility. Plyometric training can also help you burn calories, lose weight, and increase muscle mass.

Personally, I find plyometric training to be challenging but fun. I usually do plyometric exercises like box jumps, jump squats, and jump lunges. I perform each exercise for 30 seconds and rest for 10 seconds between each one.

How Do Anaerobic Exercises Benefit Your Body?

Anaerobic exercises offer many benefits to your body, including:

  • Building muscle mass
  • Increase strength
  • Boosting metabolism
  • Improving cardiovascular health
  • Burning calories
  • Reducing body fat
  • Increasing bone density
  • Improving posture
  • Increasing power, speed, and agility

How to Perform Anaerobic Exercises Safely?

To perform anaerobic exercises safely, you should:

  • Warm-up properly before exercising
  • Start with low weights and gradually increase over time
  • Use proper form and technique to avoid injury
  • Rest for at least 24-48 hours between workouts
  • Stay hydrated
  • Listen to your body and stop if you feel any pain or discomfort

Conclusion of What Are the Three Types of Anaerobic Exercises

Anaerobic exercises are an excellent way to build muscle, increase strength, and improve overall fitness. The three types of anaerobic exercises are High-Intensity Interval Training (HIIT), Resistance Training, and Plyometric Training. Each type of exercise offers unique benefits and can help you achieve different fitness goals. By incorporating anaerobic exercises into your workout routine, you can enjoy numerous health benefits and improve your quality of life.
