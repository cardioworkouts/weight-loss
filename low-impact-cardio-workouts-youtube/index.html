Low Impact Cardio Workouts On Youtube A Beginner s Guide

Written by Bardi Oct 28, 2023 Â· 6 min read

Are you looking for a way to get fit without putting too much pressure on your joints? Do you struggle with high impact workouts that leave you feeling exhausted and in pain? If so, you're not alone. Many people struggle with finding a form of exercise that is both effective and gentle on their body. Luckily, there is a solution: low impact cardio workouts on YouTube.

Low impact cardio workouts on YouTube are designed to be gentle on your joints while still providing a challenging workout. Unlike high impact workouts, which involve jumping, running, and other movements that can put strain on your knees, ankles, and hips, low impact workouts involve movements that keep at least one foot on the ground at all times. This makes them a great option for people with joint issues, as well as beginners who are just starting to get into fitness.

So what exactly are low impact cardio workouts on YouTube, and how do they work? In this article, we'll dive into everything you need to know about this popular form of exercise, including its benefits, the best workouts to try, and some tips for getting started.

To start, let's recap what we know so far. Low impact cardio workouts on YouTube are a great option for people who want to get fit without putting too much pressure on their joints. These workouts involve movements that keep at least one foot on the ground at all times, making them a good choice for beginners and those with joint issues.

The Benefits of Low Impact Cardio Workouts on YouTube

Before we get into the nitty-gritty of low impact cardio workouts on YouTube, let's take a moment to talk about the benefits. First and foremost, low impact cardio workouts are great for your heart and lungs. By getting your heart rate up and challenging your cardiovascular system, these workouts can help improve your overall cardiovascular health and reduce your risk of heart disease, stroke, and other chronic conditions.

In addition to the cardiovascular benefits, low impact cardio workouts on YouTube can also help you build strength, improve your balance and coordination, and increase your flexibility. They can also be a great way to relieve stress and improve your mood, thanks to the endorphins (feel-good chemicals) that are released during exercise.

My Personal Experience with Low Impact Cardio Workouts on YouTube

I've been doing low impact cardio workouts on YouTube for about six months now, and I have to say, I'm hooked. As someone who has struggled with knee pain in the past, I was hesitant to try high impact workouts like running or jumping jacks. But with low impact workouts, I've been able to get a great workout without any pain or discomfort.

My favorite low impact cardio workout on YouTube is a dance-based workout that involves lots of fun, easy-to-follow moves. I love how it gets my heart rate up without feeling like a chore, and the music always puts me in a good mood. Plus, I've noticed that my endurance and flexibility have improved since I started doing these workouts regularly.

The Best Low Impact Cardio Workouts on YouTube

Now that you know the benefits and have heard about my personal experience, let's talk about the best low impact cardio workouts on YouTube. There are hundreds of options out there, so it can be overwhelming to know where to start. Here are a few of my favorites:

1. Dance Cardio

If you love to dance, you'll love dance cardio workouts on YouTube. These workouts are usually set to upbeat music and involve lots of fun, easy-to-follow moves that will get your heart rate up and your body moving.

2. Low Impact HIIT

HIIT (high-intensity interval training) workouts are a great way to get a challenging workout in a short amount of time. But if you're looking for a low impact option, try a low impact HIIT workout on YouTube. These workouts involve short bursts of high-intensity exercise followed by periods of rest, and can be modified to be low impact.

3. Walking Workouts

Walking may not seem like a "workout," but it can be a great way to get moving and get your heart rate up. Try a walking workout on YouTube that includes intervals of faster walking or light jogging to challenge yourself.

Tips for Getting Started with Low Impact Cardio Workouts on YouTube

Now that you know the benefits and have some workout ideas, here are a few tips for getting started with low impact cardio workouts on YouTube:

1. Start slow and gradually increase your intensity. It's important to listen to your body and not push yourself too hard too fast.

2. Invest in a good pair of workout shoes. Even though low impact workouts are gentler on your joints, you still want to make sure you're wearing supportive, comfortable shoes.

3. Find a workout buddy or accountability partner. Working out with someone else can make it more fun and help keep you motivated.

4. Mix it up! Don't be afraid to try different workouts and mix up your routine to keep things interesting.

Question and Answer

Q: Is it okay to do low impact cardio workouts every day?

A: It's generally safe to do low impact cardio workouts every day, as long as you listen to your body and don't overdo it. However, it's also important to give your body time to rest and recover, so it's a good idea to mix in some other types of exercise (like strength training or yoga) and take at least one or two rest days per week.

Q: Can low impact cardio workouts help me lose weight?

A: Yes, low impact cardio workouts can be an effective way to lose weight, especially when combined with a healthy diet. By getting your heart rate up and challenging your body, these workouts can help you burn calories and shed pounds over time.

Q: Do I need any equipment to do low impact cardio workouts on YouTube?

A: Most low impact cardio workouts on YouTube don't require any equipment, but it's always a good idea to check the video description or comments to see if there are any recommended props (like resistance bands or weights) that you might want to use.

Q: How long should I do low impact cardio workouts for?

A: The length of your workout will depend on your fitness level and goals. As a general rule, aim for at least 30 minutes of exercise per day, five days per week. You can break this up into shorter workouts throughout the day if that works better for your schedule.

Conclusion of Low Impact Cardio Workouts on YouTube

Low impact cardio workouts on YouTube are a great way to get fit without putting too much pressure on your joints. They can help improve your cardiovascular health, build strength, and relieve stress, all while being gentle on your body. With so many options available, there's something for everyone. So why not give low impact cardio workouts on YouTube a try?
