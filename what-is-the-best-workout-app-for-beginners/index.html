Discovering The Best Workout App For Beginners A Comprehensive Guide

Written by Christine Nov 22, 2023 · 4 min read

Are you a beginner looking to start your fitness journey but don't know where to begin? Do you find yourself overwhelmed by the number of workout apps available in the market? Choosing the best workout app for beginners can be a daunting task, but fear not, we've got you covered. In this article, we'll explore the best workout app for beginners and help you find the perfect fit for your fitness needs.

The world of fitness can be intimidating, especially for beginners. You may be unsure of where to start, what exercises to do, or how to track your progress. These pain points can make it difficult to stay motivated and consistent with your workouts. Investing in a workout app can help alleviate these concerns and provide structure and guidance for your fitness journey.

When it comes to finding the best workout app for beginners, there are several factors to consider. These include your fitness goals, exercise preferences, and budget. However, there is one app that stands out among the rest as the best option for beginners - FitOn.

What is FitOn and why is it the best workout app for beginners?

FitOn is a free fitness app that offers a wide range of workouts for all fitness levels. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced athlete, FitOn has something for everyone. The app offers personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals, and you can choose from a variety of workout styles, including yoga, cardio, strength training, and more.

Personally, I started my fitness journey with FitOn and found it to be the perfect app for beginners. The app offers easy-to-follow videos with clear instructions and modifications for each exercise. Additionally, the app tracks your progress and provides encouragement to stay consistent with your workouts.

Other Great Workout Apps for Beginners

While FitOn is our top pick for the best workout app for beginners, there are other great options available. These include:

1. Nike Training Club

Nike Training Club is a free app that offers a variety of workouts for all fitness levels. The app includes personalized workout plans and allows you to track your progress and set goals. Additionally, the app offers audio and video guidance for each exercise, making it easy to follow along.

2. 7 Minute Workout

For those short on time, the 7 Minute Workout app is a great option. The app offers quick, high-intensity workouts that can be done anywhere, without any equipment. While the workouts are short, they are challenging and effective.

3. JEFIT

JEFIT is a free weightlifting app that offers a variety of exercises and workout plans. The app includes detailed instructions and animations for each exercise, making it easy to learn proper form. Additionally, the app allows you to track your progress and set goals.

Question and Answer

Q: Can workout apps really help beginners?

A: Yes, workout apps can be a great tool for beginners. They provide structure and guidance for your workouts, making it easier to stay consistent and achieve your fitness goals.

Q: How much do workout apps cost?

A: The cost of workout apps varies. Some are free, while others require a monthly or yearly subscription. However, many apps offer a free trial period so you can test them out before committing to a subscription.

Q: Are workout apps suitable for all fitness levels?

A: Yes, many workout apps offer workouts for all fitness levels. Some apps even offer personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals and current fitness level.

Q: Can workout apps replace in-person training?

A: While workout apps can be a great tool for beginners, they cannot replace in-person training entirely. It's important to learn proper form and technique, which is best done with the guidance of a personal trainer or fitness professional.

Conclusion of what is the best workout app for beginners

Choosing the best workout app for beginners can be overwhelming, but with the right app, your fitness journey can be both enjoyable and effective. FitOn is our top pick for the best workout app for beginners due to its personalized workout plans, variety of workout styles, and clear instructions. However, there are other great options available, including Nike Training Club, 7 Minute Workout, and JEFIT. Regardless of which app you choose, investing in a workout app can help you stay motivated and consistent with your workouts, leading to a happier and healthier you.
