Best At Home Full Body Workout Without Equipment

Written by Petter Sep 02, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Are you tired of going to the gym or lacking the motivation to exercise? Do you want to workout from the comfort of your own home but don't have any equipment? Look no further! In this tutorial, we will provide you with the best at home full body workout without equipment that you can do anywhere, anytime.

Many people struggle to find time to exercise or lack access to gym equipment. However, it is important to prioritize fitness and maintain a healthy lifestyle. With this at home full body workout, you can achieve your fitness goals without the need for any equipment or gym membership.

The target of this tutorial is to provide you with the best at home full body workout without equipment that can be done by anyone, regardless of fitness level or experience.

In summary, this tutorial will provide you with a full body workout routine that can be done at home without the need for any equipment. We will cover exercises that target all major muscle groups and provide tips on how to modify the routine to fit your fitness level.

Push-Ups

Push-ups are a classic exercise that targets your chest, shoulders, triceps, and core. Start in a plank position with your hands shoulder-width apart and your feet hip-width apart. Lower your body towards the ground by bending your elbows and keeping your body straight. Push back up to the starting position and repeat for 3 sets of 10-15 reps.

Squats

Squats are a great exercise that targets your glutes, quads, hamstrings, and calves. Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and lower your body by bending your knees and pushing your hips back. Keep your weight on your heels and your chest up. Push back up to the starting position and repeat for 3 sets of 10-15 reps.

Lunges

Lunges are another effective exercise that targets your quads, hamstrings, glutes, and core. Start by standing with your feet hip-width apart and take a big step forward with your right foot. Lower your body towards the ground by bending both knees and keeping your chest up. Push back up to the starting position and repeat on the other side. Do 3 sets of 10-15 reps on each leg.

Plank

The plank is a great exercise that targets your core muscles. Start by getting into a push-up position and lower your body down onto your forearms. Your elbows should be directly under your shoulders and your body should form a straight line from your head to your heels. Hold this position for 30 seconds to 1 minute and repeat for 3 sets.

Burpees

Burpees are a full body exercise that targets your chest, shoulders, triceps, quads, hamstrings, glutes, and core. Start in a standing position and lower your body into a squat position. Place your hands on the ground and jump your feet back into a plank position. Do a push-up and jump your feet back to your hands. Stand up and jump into the air, reaching your hands overhead. Repeat for 3 sets of 10-15 reps.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I modify the exercises to fit my fitness level?

A: Yes, you can modify the exercises by doing fewer reps or taking breaks between sets. You can also modify the exercises by doing easier variations or using props like a chair or wall for balance.

Q: How often should I do this workout?

A: You can do this workout 2-3 times per week, with rest days in between. It is important to listen to your body and not overdo it.

Q: Can I add weights to this workout?

A: Yes, you can add weights to some of the exercises like squats and lunges to make them more challenging.

Q: Is it necessary to warm up before doing this workout?

A: Yes, it is important to warm up before doing any exercise to prevent injury. You can warm up by doing some light cardio like jumping jacks or jogging in place.

Conclusion of Best At Home Full Body Workout Without Equipment

In conclusion, this at home full body workout without equipment is a great way to maintain your fitness and achieve your goals. The exercises we covered target all major muscle groups and can be modified to fit your fitness level. Remember to warm up before exercising and listen to your body to prevent injury. With consistency and dedication, you can achieve a healthy and fit lifestyle from the comfort of your own home.
