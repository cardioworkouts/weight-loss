What Are The Best Free Weight Lifting Apps

Written by Oliver Sep 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to improve your weight lifting routine but don't want to spend money on a personal trainer? Fortunately, there are free weight lifting apps available that can help you track your progress and provide helpful tips. In this blog post, we will discuss the best free weight lifting apps and how they can benefit you.

Pain Points

Many people struggle with weight lifting because they don't know how to properly track their progress or create a routine that works for them. Others may not have access to a personal trainer or gym membership, making it difficult to improve their weight lifting skills. These pain points can be addressed with the help of free weight lifting apps.

What are the Best Free Weight Lifting Apps?

There are many free weight lifting apps available, but some stand out among the rest. Here are our top picks:

1. JEFIT

JEFIT is a comprehensive weight lifting app that provides users with detailed workout plans, progress tracking, and community support. Users can create personalized workouts and track their progress over time, making it easy to see improvements and make adjustments as needed.

2. Strong

Strong is a simple and intuitive weight lifting app that is perfect for beginners. It provides users with a variety of pre-made workouts and allows them to create custom routines based on their fitness goals. The app also tracks progress and provides helpful tips to improve form and technique.

3. Fitbod

Fitbod is a dynamic weight lifting app that generates personalized workouts based on the user's fitness level and goals. It also tracks progress and adjusts routines as needed to ensure continued improvement. The app also includes detailed instructions and videos to help users perfect their form.

4. MyFitnessPal

While not specifically a weight lifting app, MyFitnessPal is a useful tool for tracking nutrition and staying accountable. Users can log their meals and snacks, as well as their workouts, to ensure they are meeting their fitness goals.

Personal Experience

As a personal trainer, I often recommend JEFIT to my clients who are looking to improve their weight lifting routine. The app provides detailed workout plans and progress tracking, making it easy for my clients to see the improvements they are making over time. I also appreciate the community support aspect of the app, which allows users to connect with others who are working toward similar goals.

Benefits of Free Weight Lifting Apps

Using a free weight lifting app can provide numerous benefits, including:

1. Accountability:

Tracking progress and setting goals can help keep you accountable and motivated to continue improving.

2. Personalization:

Many apps allow users to create personalized workouts based on their fitness goals and level, ensuring they are getting the most out of their weight lifting routine.

3. Education:

Weight lifting apps often provide helpful tips and tutorials to improve form and technique, reducing the risk of injury and improving overall performance.

Question and Answer

Q: Are free weight lifting apps just as effective as a personal trainer?

A: While a personal trainer can provide personalized guidance and support, free weight lifting apps can be just as effective for improving your weight lifting routine. They provide detailed workout plans, progress tracking, and helpful tips to ensure you are making progress and improving your technique.

Q: Can I use free weight lifting apps if I am a beginner?

A: Absolutely! Many free weight lifting apps, such as Strong, are designed specifically for beginners and provide pre-made workouts and guidance to ensure proper technique and form.

Q: Do I need access to a gym to use free weight lifting apps?

A: While a gym membership can be helpful, many free weight lifting apps provide workouts that can be done at home with minimal equipment.

Q: Are there any downsides to using free weight lifting apps?

A: Some apps may require a premium subscription to access all features, and others may not provide as much personalized guidance as a personal trainer. However, for those looking to improve their weight lifting routine on a budget, free weight lifting apps can be a great option.

Conclusion of What are the Best Free Weight Lifting Apps

Free weight lifting apps can be a valuable tool for improving your weight lifting routine and achieving your fitness goals. Our top picks include JEFIT, Strong, Fitbod, and MyFitnessPal. By using these apps, you can track your progress, improve your technique, and stay accountable on your fitness journey.
