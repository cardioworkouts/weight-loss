Get Fit With The Best Free Weight Lifting Workout Apps

Written by Robby Jul 26, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Do you want to get in shape but don't have the time or money for a gym membership? Or maybe you're just starting out and don't know where to begin with weight lifting. Whatever your situation may be, there are plenty of free weight lifting workout apps available that can help you achieve your fitness goals.

The pain points of finding the right workout routine can be overwhelming. With so many options out there, it can be difficult to know which workout app to choose. And once you've decided on an app, you may not know how to properly use it or if it's even effective.

The target of this article is to provide you with a list of the best free weight lifting workout apps and related keywords. These apps are designed to help you build muscle, increase strength, and improve your overall fitness level. They offer a variety of workouts and training plans, so you can find the one that works best for you.

In summary, this article will cover the best free weight lifting workout apps and related keywords. We'll discuss the benefits of using these apps, how to choose the right one for you, and provide personal experiences with some of the top apps on the market.

1. JEFIT

JEFIT is a popular weight lifting app that offers a variety of workouts and training plans. It allows users to create their own custom workout routines or choose from a library of pre-made plans. The app also includes a progress tracker, so you can see how you're improving over time.

Personally, I've been using JEFIT for several months and have found it to be extremely helpful in keeping me on track with my workouts. The app is easy to use and offers a wide variety of exercises, so I never get bored with my routine.

2. StrongLifts 5x5

StrongLifts 5x5 is a simple but effective weight lifting program that focuses on five basic exercises: squat, bench press, deadlift, overhead press, and barbell row. The app guides users through the program, with each workout lasting around 45 minutes.

Personally, I found StrongLifts 5x5 to be a great starting point for weight lifting. The program is easy to follow and the app makes it simple to track your progress. Plus, the focus on basic exercises helps build a solid foundation for more advanced workouts.

3. Fitbod

Fitbod is an AI-powered weight lifting app that creates a personalized workout plan based on your fitness level and goals. The app uses machine learning algorithms to adjust your plan as you progress, ensuring that you continue to challenge yourself and see results.

The app also includes video tutorials for each exercise, so you can ensure that you're using proper form. Personally, I've found Fitbod to be a great way to keep my workouts fresh and challenging. The personalized plan ensures that I'm always pushing myself to improve.

4. MyFitnessPal

While not specifically a weight lifting app, MyFitnessPal is a great tool for tracking your calorie intake and macros. Proper nutrition is key to achieving your fitness goals, and MyFitnessPal makes it easy to ensure that you're staying on track.

The app includes a database of over 11 million foods, so you can easily log what you're eating. It also integrates with a variety of other fitness apps, such as Fitbit and Apple Health, to provide a comprehensive overview of your fitness journey.

Conclusion of Best Free Weight Lifting Workout Apps

Whether you're a beginner or an experienced weight lifter, there's an app out there that can help you achieve your fitness goals. The best free weight lifting workout apps offer a variety of workouts, personalized plans, and progress tracking, so you can stay motivated and see results. By using one of these apps, you can take your fitness journey to the next level.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I really get fit using only a free weight lifting workout app?

A: Absolutely! While a gym membership can be helpful, it's not necessary to achieve your fitness goals. With the right workout app, you can build muscle, increase strength, and improve your overall fitness level.

Q: How do I choose the right weight lifting app for me?

A: Consider your fitness goals, skill level, and preferences when selecting a weight lifting app. Look for an app that offers a variety of workouts, personalized plans, and progress tracking.

Q: Can I use these apps if I don't have any weight lifting equipment?

A: Yes! Many of these apps offer bodyweight exercises that require no equipment. And for those that do require equipment, you can often substitute household items like water bottles or bags of rice.

Q: Are these apps safe for beginners?

A: Yes! Many of these apps offer beginner-friendly workouts and include video tutorials to ensure that you're using proper form. However, it's always a good idea to consult with a doctor or trainer before starting a new fitness routine.
