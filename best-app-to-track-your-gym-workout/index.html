Free Best App To Track Your Gym Workout Muscle Gain

Written by Petter Aug 14, 2023 Â· 4 min read

Get the most out of your gym workouts with the best app to track your progress.

Are you tired of trying to remember all of the exercises you did at the gym? Maybe you're not seeing the results you want because you're not tracking your progress. Keeping track of your workouts can be time-consuming and confusing, but the best app to track your gym workout can help.

What is the Best App to Track Your Gym Workout?

The best app to track your gym workout depends on what you're looking for. Some apps are great for tracking your exercises and sets, while others offer nutrition tracking and progress photos. Here are some of the best apps to consider:

  • MyFitnessPal: This app is great for tracking your calories, macros, and workouts. It has a huge food database and allows you to scan barcodes to easily log your meals.
  • Strong: Strong is perfect for weightlifting workouts. It has a clean interface that allows you to easily log your exercises and sets.
  • Fitbod: Fitbod creates personalized workout plans based on your fitness goals and progress. It also tracks your exercises and sets.
  • JEFIT: JEFIT has a large exercise database and allows you to create and track your own workouts. It also has a social aspect that allows you to connect with other users.

Whatever your fitness goals are, there's an app out there that can help you achieve them.

My Experience with MyFitnessPal

I personally use MyFitnessPal to track my workouts and nutrition. It's great for keeping me accountable and motivated. I love being able to see my progress over time and make adjustments to my workouts and meals as needed.

One of my favorite features of MyFitnessPal is the barcode scanner. When I'm at the grocery store, I can easily scan the barcodes of the food I'm considering buying to see how it fits into my daily calorie and macro goals.

Why You Should Track Your Workouts

Tracking your workouts can help you see progress over time and make adjustments to your routines. It can also help you stay motivated and accountable. When you see that you're making progress, it can be a huge confidence boost.

Additionally, tracking your workouts can help you identify areas where you may be slacking off. Maybe you're not pushing yourself hard enough during your workouts or you're not getting enough rest between sets. By tracking your workouts, you can identify these areas and make changes to improve your overall fitness.

How to Track Your Workouts

There are a few different ways to track your workouts:

  • Pen and paper: This is the most old-fashioned way to track your workouts, but it can be effective if you're diligent about it. Just bring a notebook and pen to the gym and write down your exercises, sets, and reps.
  • Spreadsheets: If you're more tech-savvy, you may prefer to use a spreadsheet to track your workouts. This allows you to easily see your progress over time and make adjustments to your workouts.
  • Apps: As mentioned earlier, there are many apps available that can help you track your workouts. These apps often have additional features like nutrition tracking and progress photos.

My Experience with Strong

I've also used Strong in the past and I love how easy it is to use. The clean interface makes it easy to log your exercises and sets, and the app keeps track of your progress over time.

One of my favorite features of Strong is the ability to create and save custom workouts. This makes it easy to switch up my routine and keep my workouts interesting.

Conclusion of Best App to Track Your Gym Workout

The best app to track your gym workout is the one that works best for you. Whether you prefer pen and paper, spreadsheets, or apps, tracking your workouts can help you see progress over time and make adjustments to your routines. Consider trying out a few different methods to see what works best for you.

Question and Answer

Q: Can I track my progress with the free versions of these apps?

A: Yes, all of the apps listed have free versions available. However, the paid versions often offer additional features and benefits.

Q: How often should I track my workouts?

A: It's up to you! Some people prefer to track every workout, while others track once a week or once a month. The important thing is to be consistent.

Q: Can I use these apps for cardio workouts?

A: Yes, most of these apps allow you to track cardio workouts as well as weightlifting workouts.

Q: Can I connect with other users on these apps?

A: Yes, some of the apps have social features that allow you to connect with other users. This can be a great way to find workout buddies and stay motivated.
